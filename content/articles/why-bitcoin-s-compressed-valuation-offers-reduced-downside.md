### Market Impact: Reduced Downside Risk
The compressed valuation of Bitcoin offers reduced downside risk compared to traditional stocks. This is because Bitcoin's price has already factored in the potential risks and uncertainties, resulting in a lower valuation. In contrast, traditional stocks often have a higher valuation, which can lead to a greater downside risk.
