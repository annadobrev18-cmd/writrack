### Tokenization: The Driving Force Behind the Surge
Tokenization refers to the process of converting real-world assets into digital tokens that can be stored and traded on a blockchain. This concept has gained significant traction in recent years, with many companies and projects exploring its potential. The push for tokenization has been a key driver behind the recent surge in Bitcoin's price. As more assets become tokenized, the demand for Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies increases, leading to higher prices.
