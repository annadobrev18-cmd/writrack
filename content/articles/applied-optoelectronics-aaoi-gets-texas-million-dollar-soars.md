### Background on Applied Optoelectronics

Applied Optoelectronics, Inc. (AAOI) is a leading developer and manufacturer of advanced optical communications products. Founded in 1997 and headquartered in Sugar Land, Texas, AAOI has established itself as a key player in the optoelectronics industry, providing a range of products including laser diodes, photodetectors, and optical transceivers.

#### Product Portfolio and Applications

AAOI's product portfolio caters to a diverse range of applications, including fiber optic communications, cable television, and data communications. The company's products are used in various markets, such as internet data centers, telecom, and cable TV. The demand for high-speed data transmission and the increasing need for efficient and reliable optical communication systems have positioned AAOI for potential growth.
