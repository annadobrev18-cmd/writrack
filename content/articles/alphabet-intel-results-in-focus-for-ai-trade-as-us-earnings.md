#### Alphabet's AI Ambitions
Alphabet's AI ambitions are not limited to its Google subsidiary. The company has also been investing in other AI-related ventures, such as DeepMind, a UK-based AI research organization that has developed several cutting-edge AI technologies, including AlphaGo, a computer program that can play Go at a world-class level. Alphabet has also been investing in AI-powered healthcare, with its Verily Life Sciences subsidiary working on developing AI-powered medical devices and diagnostic tools.

#### Intel's AI Strategy
Intel's AI strategy is focused on developing AI-powered chips and software solutions that can be used in a variety of applications, including edge computing, autonomous vehicles, and healthcare. The company has been working on developing its Nervana Neural Stick, a deep learning accelerator that can be used to accelerate AI workloads in data centers and edge devices. Intel has also been investing in AI-powered computer vision, with its Movidius subsidiary developing AI-powered computer vision chips that can be used in applications such as smart homes and cities.
