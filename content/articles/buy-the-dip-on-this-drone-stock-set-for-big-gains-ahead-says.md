### Historical Context: The Rise of Drones
To understand the potential of the drone industry, it's essential to look at its historical context. The use of drones, or unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), dates back to the early 20th century. However, it wasn't until the 1990s and 2000s that drones started gaining traction, primarily for military purposes. The turning point came with the introduction of the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) regulations in 2016, which paved the way for commercial drone operations.
