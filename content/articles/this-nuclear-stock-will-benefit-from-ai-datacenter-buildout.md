#### Surging Energy Demand
The global energy demand is surging, driven by the growing population, urbanization, and industrialization. The International Energy Agency (IEA) estimates that the global energy demand will increase by 30% by 2040, with the majority of this growth coming from emerging economies. This surge in energy demand is expected to drive the growth of the nuclear energy sector, as countries seek to diversify their energy mix and reduce their reliance on fossil fuels.

## Nuclear Energy's Role in the Energy Mix
Nuclear energy is poised to play a significant role in the global energy mix, given its ability to provide clean and reliable energy. The World Nuclear Association estimates that nuclear energy can reduce greenhouse gas emissions by up to 2.2 billion tons per year, which is equivalent to taking 450 million cars off the road. Moreover, nuclear energy can provide baseload power, which is essential for meeting the growing energy demand.

### AI Datacenter Buildout
The buildout of AI data centers is another trend that is expected to drive the growth of the nuclear energy sector. AI data centers require significant amounts of energy to power their operations, and nuclear energy is well-positioned to meet this demand. According to a report by BloombergNEF, the energy consumption of AI data centers is expected to increase by 20% per year, reaching 8% of global electricity demand by 2030.
