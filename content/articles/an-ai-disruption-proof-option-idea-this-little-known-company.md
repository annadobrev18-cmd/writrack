## Fundamentals of Amcor's Merger with Berry Global
The merger between Amcor and Berry Global, completed in April 2025, has created a behemoth in the consumer and healthcare packaging sector. This strategic move has not only expanded Amcor's portfolio but also positioned it to realize significant operating synergies. The combined entity boasts an impressive global footprint, with operations in over 40 countries and a diverse customer base spanning the food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and personal care industries.

### Historical Context of the Merger
The packaging industry has witnessed a wave of consolidation in recent years, driven by the need for companies to achieve scale, reduce costs, and enhance their competitive positioning. Amcor's merger with Berry Global is a prime example of this trend. By combining their resources and expertise, the two companies aim to create a more efficient and innovative packaging solutions provider.
