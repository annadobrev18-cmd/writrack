## SpaceX's Sagging Stock: A Blow to Blockbuster IPOs
The recent performance of SpaceX's stock has been underwhelming, to say the least. As of Friday, the stock traded near $126, approximately 7% below its initial public offering (IPO) price of $135. This slump has significant implications for the market, particularly for upcoming blockbuster IPOs.

### Historical Context: The Rise of SpaceX
To understand the current situation, it's essential to delve into SpaceX's history. Founded in 2002 by Elon Musk, the company has been at the forefront of private space exploration and development. With numerous successful launches and innovations, SpaceX has garnered substantial attention and investment. The company's decision to go public was highly anticipated, with many expecting a significant surge in stock price.
