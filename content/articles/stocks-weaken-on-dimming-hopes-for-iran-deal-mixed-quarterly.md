### Impact of Dimming Hopes for Iran Deal

The dimming hopes for a deal between the US and Iran have significant implications for the global economy. Without a deal, oil prices are likely to remain high, which could have a negative impact on the economy. To understand the significance of the US-Iran negotiations, it's essential to look at the historical context. The US and Iran have had a tumultuous relationship for decades, with tensions escalating in recent years. The 2015 nuclear deal, which was negotiated during the Obama administration, provided a temporary reprieve from tensions. However, the deal was withdrawn by the Trump administration in 2018, leading to a significant escalation in tensions.
