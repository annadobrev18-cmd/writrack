## Citigroup's Rare Buy Rating: A Vote of Confidence for the EV Manufacturer
Citigroup has initiated coverage on a promising electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer with a rare buy rating, sending a strong signal to investors about the company's potential for growth. The bank's analysts believe that the EV play, although high risk, could jump more than 70% thanks to several tailwinds. This bullish bet is significant, given the current market landscape and the intense competition in the EV space.

### Historical Context: The Rise of Electric Vehicles
The electric vehicle market has experienced tremendous growth over the past decade, driven by increasing consumer demand, government incentives, and advancements in technology. As concerns about climate change and air pollution continue to mount, EVs have become an attractive alternative to traditional internal combustion engine vehicles. According to the International Energy Agency (IEA), there were over 17 million EVs on the road worldwide in 2022, with sales projected to reach 14 million units in 2026.
