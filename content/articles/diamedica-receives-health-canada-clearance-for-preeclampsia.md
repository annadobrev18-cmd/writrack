### Fundamentals of DiaMedica Therapeutics

DiaMedica Therapeutics is a biotech company focused on developing novel treatments for neurological and kidney diseases. The company's lead product candidate, DM199, is a recombinant human tissue kallikrein-1 (KLK1) protein, which has shown promise in treating preeclampsia, a pregnancy complication characterized by high blood pressure and damage to organs such as the kidneys and liver.

The company's financials are characterized by significant research and development expenses, which are typical for biotech companies in the clinical-stage phase. As of the latest available data, DiaMedica's cash and cash equivalents stood at $15.3 million, with a net loss of $10.3 million for the quarter ended September 30, 2025.
