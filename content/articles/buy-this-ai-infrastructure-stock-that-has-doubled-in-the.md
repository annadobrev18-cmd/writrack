### What is AI Infrastructure?
AI infrastructure refers to the underlying systems and technologies that enable the development, deployment, and management of artificial intelligence (AI) applications. This includes hardware components such as graphics processing units (GPUs), tensor processing units (TPUs), and field-programmable gate arrays (FPGAs), as well as software frameworks and platforms that support AI model development and deployment.

#### Impact of Cloud Computing on AI Infrastructure
The growing adoption of cloud computing has had a significant impact on the AI infrastructure market. Cloud providers such as Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP) offer a range of AI infrastructure services, including GPU-accelerated computing, AI model development, and deployment.
