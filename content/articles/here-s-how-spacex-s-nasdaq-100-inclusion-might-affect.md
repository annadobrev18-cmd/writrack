### Historical Context: Nasdaq-100 Index

The Nasdaq-100 index is a modified market capitalization-weighted index that tracks the performance of the 100 largest and most actively traded non-financial stocks listed on the Nasdaq stock exchange. The index is widely followed by investors and is often used as a benchmark for portfolio performance. The inclusion of SpaceX in the Nasdaq-100 index is a significant event, as it marks the first time a private company has been added to the index.

The inclusion of SpaceX in the Nasdaq-100 index is expected to have a significant impact on options pricing.
