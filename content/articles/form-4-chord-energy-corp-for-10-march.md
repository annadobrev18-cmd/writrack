### Understanding Form 4 Filings
Form 4 filings are required by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) for insiders of publicly traded companies. These filings disclose transactions such as purchases, sales, and grants of securities by corporate officers, directors, and beneficial owners of more than 10% of the company's stock. The information provided in Form 4 filings can offer valuable insights into the confidence and sentiment of insiders regarding their company's future performance.
