### Oklo: The New Kid on the Block

Oklo, a relatively new player in the tech industry, has been gaining attention for its innovative approach to nuclear energy. With a focus on developing small modular reactors, Oklo is poised to disrupt the traditional energy sector and provide a cleaner, more efficient source of power.

#### Competitor Analysis

Oklo's main competitors in the nuclear energy space include established players such as Westinghouse and General Electric. However, Oklo's unique approach to small modular reactors sets it apart from its competitors. The following table highlights a peer comparison:
