## Dexcom's Ambitious Promise
Dexcom, a leading player in the diabetes technology market, has made a lofty promise to its investors, sparking a wave of bullish sentiment among analysts. The company's commitment to delivering strong growth and expanding its continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) technology has investors and experts alike taking notice.

### Historical Context
To understand the significance of Dexcom's promise, it's essential to examine the company's historical performance. Over the past five years, Dexcom has consistently delivered strong revenue growth, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 25%. This impressive growth has been driven by the increasing adoption of CGM technology, which has revolutionized the way people with diabetes manage their condition.
