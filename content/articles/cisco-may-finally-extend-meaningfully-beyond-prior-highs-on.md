## Cisco's Historic Breakout
Cisco Systems, Inc. (CSCO) has finally surpassed its 2000 peak, marking a significant milestone for the company. This achievement is particularly noteworthy given the lengthy period it has taken for the stock to recover and reach new heights. The latest chart action suggests that Cisco may extend meaningfully beyond its prior highs, presenting an intriguing opportunity for investors.

### Historical Context
To appreciate the significance of Cisco's breakout, it's essential to consider the company's history. In 2000, Cisco's stock price soared to unprecedented levels, driven by the dot-com bubble. However, the subsequent burst of the bubble led to a sharp decline in the stock's value. It has taken over two decades for Cisco to reclaim its former glory, with the stock finally overtaking its 2000 peak in February 2026.
