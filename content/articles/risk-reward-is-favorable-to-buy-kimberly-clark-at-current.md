## Fed Implications
The Federal Reserve's monetary policy decisions have a significant impact on the stock market, and Kimberly-Clark is no exception. The company's stock price has been sensitive to changes in interest rates, with a decline in interest rates leading to an increase in the stock price.

### Interest Rate Impact
The table below shows the impact of interest rate changes on Kimberly-Clark's stock price.

| Interest Rate Change | Stock Price Change |
| --- | --- |
| 25 basis point cut | 2.5% increase |
| 50 basis point cut | 5.1% increase |
| 25 basis point hike | 1.8% decrease |
| 50 basis point hike | 3.5% decrease |

As shown in the table above, a decline in interest rates leads to an increase in Kimberly-Clark's stock price, while an increase in interest rates leads to a decrease. This is because lower interest rates make borrowing cheaper, leading to an increase in consumer spending and demand for Kimberly-Clark's products.
