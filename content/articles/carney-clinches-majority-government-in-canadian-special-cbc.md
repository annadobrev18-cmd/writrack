Carney's campaign focused on revitalizing the Canadian economy, with an emphasis on sustainable growth, job creation, and fiscal responsibility. ### Risk Factors: Challenges Ahead for Carney's Government
While Carney's victory has generated economic optimism, there are several challenges that his government must address to sustain growth momentum. These include:

* **Trade tensions**: Canada's economy is heavily reliant on trade, and ongoing tensions with major partners, such as the United States and China, pose a significant risk to growth.
* **Fiscal discipline**: Carney's government must balance its policy agenda with the need for fiscal discipline, ensuring that the country's debt-to-GDP ratio remains manageable.
* **Environmental concerns**: Canada's energy sector is a significant contributor to the country's economy, but it also poses environmental risks. Carney's government must navigate the transition to cleaner energy sources while minimizing the impact on the economy.
