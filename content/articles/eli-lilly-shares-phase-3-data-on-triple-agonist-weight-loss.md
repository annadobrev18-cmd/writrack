### Mechanism of Action

Retatrutide is a triple agonist, meaning it targets three key hormones involved in glucose and weight regulation: GLP-1, GIP, and glucagon. This multi-pronged approach has shown promising results in clinical trials, with significant weight loss and improvements in glycemic control. The drug's unique mechanism of action sets it apart from existing weight loss treatments, which often focus on a single hormone or pathway.

### Clinical Trials

The phase 3 data released by Eli Lilly demonstrates the efficacy and safety of retatrutide in a large patient population. The trials showed significant weight loss, with patients achieving an average weight reduction of 15-20% of their initial body weight. Additionally, retatrutide demonstrated a favorable safety profile, with minimal adverse effects.
