#### Key Provisions of the CLARITY Act
The CLARITY Act proposes several key provisions that could significantly impact the crypto industry:
* Clear definitions of digital assets and their classification
* Guidelines for the regulation of cryptocurrency exchanges and custodial services
* Clarity on the tax treatment of digital assets
* A framework for the issuance of new digital assets
