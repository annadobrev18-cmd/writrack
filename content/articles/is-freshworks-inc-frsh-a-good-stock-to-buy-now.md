## Freshworks Inc. (FRSH) Overview
Freshworks Inc. (FRSH) is a cloud-based software company that provides customer engagement solutions to businesses of all sizes. The company's products include Freshdesk, Freshsales, Freshmarketer, and Freshteam, among others. Freshworks Inc. has gained significant attention from investors and analysts due to its impressive growth and innovative approach to customer engagement.

The company's revenue has grown from $100 million in 2018 to over $300 million in 2022, representing a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of over 30%.
