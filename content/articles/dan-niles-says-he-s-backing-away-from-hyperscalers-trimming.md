### Historical Context: Hyperscalers and Chip Stocks
To understand the significance of Niles' move, it's essential to look at the historical context of hyperscalers and chip stocks. Chip stocks, on the other hand, have been driven by the increasing demand for semiconductors in various industries, including smartphones, automobiles, and data centers.
