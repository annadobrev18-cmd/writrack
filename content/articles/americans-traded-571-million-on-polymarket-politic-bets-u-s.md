#### Historical Context of Prediction Markets
Prediction markets have been around for decades, with traditional platforms like Intrade and Betfair offering betting services. However, these platforms were often centralized, making them vulnerable to regulatory pressures and geographical restrictions. The advent of blockchain technology and decentralized platforms like Polymarket has changed the landscape, providing users with greater autonomy and accessibility.
