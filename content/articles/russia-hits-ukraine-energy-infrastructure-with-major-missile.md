### Impact on Global Energy Markets
The attacks on Ukraine's energy infrastructure have significant implications for the global energy market. Ukraine is a critical transit country for Russian natural gas exports to Europe, and any disruption to these supplies could have far-reaching consequences for the global energy market. The conflict has already led to a surge in energy prices, with European natural gas prices increasing by over 10% in the past week alone.

#### Energy Price Volatility
The volatility in energy prices is likely to continue in the short term, as the conflict between Russia and Ukraine continues to escalate. The price of Brent crude oil has already increased by over 5% in the past week, and further increases are likely if the conflict disrupts global energy supplies.
