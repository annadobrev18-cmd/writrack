### Wolfe Research's Methodology

To identify stocks at risk of dividend cuts, Wolfe Research employed a rigorous screening process. The research firm analyzed various metrics, including dividend coverage ratios, free cash flow, and debt-to-equity ratios. By examining these key indicators, Wolfe Research was able to pinpoint companies that may struggle to maintain their current dividend payouts.

### Sector Rotation: Implications for Investors

The potential dividend cuts in various sectors may lead to a rotation in investor sentiment. As investors become increasingly risk-averse, they may shift their focus towards sectors with more stable dividend payouts. This rotation could have significant implications for the broader market, as investors seek to adjust their portfolios in response to changing market conditions.

### Global Ripple Effects: A Broader Perspective

The potential dividend cuts in the US market may have far-reaching consequences, extending beyond the borders of the United States.
