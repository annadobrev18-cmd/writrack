## Expansion into Crypto Services
Citi and Morgan Stanley, two of the world's largest financial institutions, have recently announced their plans to expand their bitcoin and crypto custody, trading, and tokenization efforts. This move is a significant development in the cryptocurrency space, as it marks a growing acceptance of digital assets by traditional financial players.

### Historical Context
The relationship between traditional finance and cryptocurrency has been evolving over the years. Initially, many financial institutions were skeptical about the potential of digital assets, viewing them as a speculative bubble or a threat to traditional financial systems. However, as the cryptocurrency market has grown and matured, more financial institutions have begun to explore ways to engage with it.

### Technical Analysis
From a technical analysis perspective, the expansion of Citi and Morgan Stanley into crypto services could lead to increased demand for cryptocurrencies, particularly bitcoin. As more institutional investors enter the market, they may help to drive up prices, which could lead to a bull run.

However, it's also important to consider the potential risks associated with increased institutional investment in cryptocurrencies. As more traditional financial institutions become involved in the market, there is a risk that they could exert greater control over the market, potentially leading to increased volatility.
