### Saylor's Investment Strategy and Belief in Bitcoin

Michael Saylor, the CEO of MicroStrategy, has been a vocal advocate for Bitcoin, believing it to be a store of value and a hedge against inflation. Under his leadership, MicroStrategy has invested heavily in Bitcoin, with the company's treasury holding a significant amount of the cryptocurrency. Saylor's belief in Bitcoin's potential for long-term growth is rooted in its limited supply and increasing demand, particularly from institutional investors.
