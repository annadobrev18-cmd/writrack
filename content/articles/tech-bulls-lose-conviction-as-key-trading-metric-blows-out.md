## Tech Bulls Lose Conviction
The tech sector, which has been a driving force behind the market's momentum in recent years, is showing signs of losing steam. A key trading metric, the put-call ratio, has blown out to its widest level since 2008, indicating a significant increase in demand for put options. This shift in sentiment aligns with slowing momentum in AI stocks, which had been consistently rewarding speculators to the upside.

### Put-Call Ratio: A Key Trading Metric
The put-call ratio is a widely followed indicator that measures the number of put options traded relative to call options. A high put-call ratio indicates that traders are becoming more bearish, as they are seeking to hedge their positions or speculate on a potential downturn. Conversely, a low put-call ratio suggests that traders are becoming more bullish, as they are seeking to capitalize on a potential uptrend.
