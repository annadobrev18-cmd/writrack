#### Impact on Oil Prices
One of the most direct and significant impacts of geopolitical tensions in the Middle East is on oil prices. As a major oil-producing nation, any instability in Iran can lead to concerns over supply chain disruptions, thereby influencing global oil prices. Historically, periods of heightened tensions in the region have been associated with spikes in oil prices, which can have far-reaching effects on the global economy.

## Fundamentals of the Current Crisis
The execution of the two individuals is seen as a crackdown by the Iranian government on opposition, which can exacerbate existing tensions both domestically and internationally. This move is likely to be met with criticism and potentially sanctions from Western countries, further complicating Iran's economic situation.

### Economic Sanctions and Their Effects
Economic sanctions imposed on Iran by the international community, particularly the United States, have had a profound impact on its economy. These sanctions limit Iran's ability to export oil and access international financial markets, leading to economic hardship for its citizens. The current situation may lead to increased sanctions, potentially worsening the economic conditions within Iran.
