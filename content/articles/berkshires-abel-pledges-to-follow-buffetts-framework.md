### Historical Context: Buffett's Framework

Warren Buffett's investment philosophy, often referred to as his 'framework,' has been the cornerstone of Berkshire Hathaway's success. This framework emphasizes long-term value investing, with a focus on acquiring high-quality businesses at reasonable prices. Buffett's approach has led to remarkable growth for Berkshire, with the company's market value increasing exponentially under his leadership.

#### Key Components of Buffett's Framework

- **Long-term Approach**: Buffett has consistently emphasized the importance of a long-term perspective in investing, often holding onto companies for decades.
- **Value Investing**: He seeks to acquire businesses at prices significantly below their intrinsic value, providing a margin of safety for investors.
- **Quality Over Quantity**: Buffett prioritizes the quality of the businesses he invests in, looking for companies with strong competitive advantages and talented management teams.
