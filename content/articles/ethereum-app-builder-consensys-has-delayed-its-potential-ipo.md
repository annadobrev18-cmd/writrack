### Historical Context: Consensys and Ethereum

To understand the significance of Consensys' IPO delay, it's essential to delve into the company's history and its relationship with Ethereum. Consensys was founded in 2014 by Joseph Lubin, one of the co-founders of Ethereum. The company has been a driving force behind the development of the Ethereum ecosystem, providing tools, infrastructure, and support for developers building on the platform.

Over the years, Consensys has been instrumental in promoting the adoption of Ethereum and has worked with various organizations to develop use cases for the blockchain. The company's products and services, such as Infura, Truffle, and MetaMask, have become staples in the Ethereum ecosystem, making it an integral part of the blockchain's infrastructure.
