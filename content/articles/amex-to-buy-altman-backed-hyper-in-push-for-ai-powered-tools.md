### Background on Hyper

Hyper is a company that specializes in AI-powered expense management tools. With the backing of Altman, Hyper has been able to develop innovative solutions that cater to the evolving needs of businesses and individuals. The company's AI-driven platform enables users to automate expense reporting, receipt tracking, and reimbursement processes, making it an attractive acquisition target for AmEx.

### Rationale Behind the Acquisition

The acquisition of Hyper is a strategic move by AmEx to expand its offerings in the expense management space. By leveraging Hyper's AI-powered tools, AmEx aims to provide its customers with more efficient and automated expense management solutions. This acquisition is also expected to enhance AmEx's competitive position in the market, as it will be able to offer a more comprehensive range of expense management tools to its customers.
