Fortescue, the Australian mining giant, has reported a significant jump in its half-year profit, driven by record shipments of iron ore. The company's impressive performance has exceeded forecasts, with a notable increase in dividend payout to shareholders.

### Financial Highlights
The key financial highlights from Fortescue's half-year report include:
- Revenue: $12.8 billion, up 34% from the same period last year
- Net profit: $4.3 billion, a 45% increase from the previous year
- Dividend payout: $1.58 per share, exceeding forecasts of $1.35 per share
