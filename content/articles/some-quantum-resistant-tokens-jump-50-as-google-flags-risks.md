However, its impact on cryptocurrency is particularly significant, as it could potentially break the encryption algorithms that secure transactions and wallets. #### Quantum-Resistant Tokens: A New Era in Cryptocurrency
Quantum-resistant tokens are designed to be secure against quantum computer attacks. These tokens utilize advanced encryption algorithms, such as lattice-based cryptography and hash-based signatures, which are resistant to quantum computing. Some of the notable quantum-resistant tokens include Quantum (QAU), Quantum Resistant Ledger (QRL), and IOTA (MIOTA).
