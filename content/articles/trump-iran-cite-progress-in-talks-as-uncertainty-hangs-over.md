### Historical Context: US-Iran Relations

To understand the current situation, it's essential to look at the historical context of US-Iran relations. The two nations have had a tumultuous relationship since the 1979 Iranian Revolution, which saw the overthrow of the US-backed Shah. The subsequent hostage crisis and the US embassy takeover in Tehran led to a significant deterioration in relations. The US has since imposed numerous sanctions on Iran, crippling its economy and limiting its access to the global financial system.

#### Sanctions and Their Impact

The sanctions imposed by the US on Iran have had a devastating impact on the country's economy. The restrictions on oil exports, in particular, have been crippling, with Iran's oil exports decreasing significantly since the imposition of sanctions. The table below illustrates the impact of sanctions on Iran's oil exports:
