### Overview of Form 4
The Form 4 filing is a required disclosure for insiders of publicly traded companies, including directors, officers, and significant shareholders. The form provides information on the insider's transactions, including purchases, sales, and other disposals of the company's securities. The recent filing by Peoples Financial Corp reveals the transactions made by its insiders, which can be used to gauge the company's prospects and make informed investment decisions.
