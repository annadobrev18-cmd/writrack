### What is Form 144?

Form 144 is a legal document that must be filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) when an insider of a company intends to sell securities. This form is used to disclose the intention to sell restricted securities, which are securities that are not registered with the SEC. The filing of Form 144 is a requirement under Rule 144 of the Securities Act of 1933.

### Linde PLC's Form 144 Filing: Key Points

The Form 144 filing by Linde PLC on February 24 indicates that certain insiders of the company plan to sell a portion of their shares. The key points of this filing are:

* The insiders intend to sell a total of 10,000 shares of Linde PLC common stock.
* The shares are being sold pursuant to Rule 144 of the Securities Act of 1933.
* The sale of the shares is expected to be completed within the next 90 days.
