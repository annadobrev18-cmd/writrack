### Historical Context: Intuit's Evolution

To appreciate the significance of Intuit's expansion into AI-powered HCM, it's essential to understand the company's historical context. Founded in 1983, Intuit has consistently innovated and adapted to changing market needs. From its early days as a personal finance software provider to its current status as a leading financial management solutions company, Intuit has demonstrated a keen ability to identify and capitalize on emerging trends. The launch of QuickBooks in 1992 marked a pivotal moment in the company's history, as it provided small businesses and accountants with a user-friendly and comprehensive financial management tool.

#### Key Milestones in Intuit's Growth

- **1992:** Launch of QuickBooks, which quickly becomes the industry standard for small business financial management.
- **2009:** Acquisition of Mint, a personal finance management platform, expanding Intuit's consumer offerings.
- **2016:** Introduction of QuickBooks Online, a cloud-based version of the popular accounting software, enabling greater accessibility and collaboration.
- **2020:** Launch of QuickBooks Cash, a small business banking platform, further integrating financial services into the QuickBooks ecosystem.
