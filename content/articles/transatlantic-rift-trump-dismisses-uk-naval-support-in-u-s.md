### Historical Context

The US and UK have historically enjoyed a special relationship, with close ties in defense, trade, and finance. However, the relationship has been strained in recent years, particularly since the election of Donald Trump as US President. The UK's decision to leave the European Union (Brexit) has also created uncertainty and tension between the two nations.

The current rift between the US and UK is not just limited to defense cooperation. The two nations have also been at odds over trade, with the US imposing tariffs on UK steel and aluminum exports. The UK has responded by imposing its own tariffs on US goods, leading to a trade war between the two nations.
