### Sector Rotation and Global Ripple Effects
The packaging industry is closely tied to the overall health of the global economy. As the economy grows, demand for packaging products increases, driving revenue and profitability for companies like PKG. However, the industry is also subject to various macroeconomic risks, such as changes in trade policies, fluctuations in raw material prices, and shifts in consumer behavior.

#### Global Economic Trends
The global economy is currently experiencing a period of slow growth, with the International Monetary Fund (IMF) forecasting a growth rate of 3.3% for 2026. This slowdown is expected to have a negative impact on the packaging industry, as demand for packaging products is likely to decrease. However, PKG's diversified product portfolio and strong market position are expected to help the company navigate this challenging environment.

### Fed Implications and Interest Rate Outlook
The Federal Reserve's monetary policy decisions have a significant impact on the packaging industry, as changes in interest rates can affect the cost of borrowing and the overall health of the economy. The current interest rate outlook is uncertain, with the Fed expected to maintain a neutral stance in the near term. However, any changes in interest rates could have a significant impact on PKG's financial performance, as the company relies on debt financing to fund its operations.

#### Impact of Interest Rate Changes
An increase in interest rates could lead to higher borrowing costs for PKG, which could negatively impact the company's profitability.
