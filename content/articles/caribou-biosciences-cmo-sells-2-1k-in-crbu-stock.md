### Understanding Insider Selling
Insider selling refers to the sale of a company's stock by its executives, directors, or other insiders. While insider selling can be a normal part of an executive's financial planning, it can also be a red flag for investors. When insiders sell their shares, it may indicate a lack of confidence in the company's future prospects or a potential decline in the stock price.
