## Merck KGaA's Strategic Move
Merck KGaA, a German multinational pharmaceutical, chemical, and life sciences company, has announced its plans to acquire Bio-Techne, a leading developer and manufacturer of high-quality purified proteins and reagent solutions, for $11.3 billion. This acquisition is a strategic move by Merck KGaA to expand its life science footprint and strengthen its position in the global market.

### Historical Context
Merck KGaA has been actively pursuing expansion opportunities in the life science sector, driven by the growing demand for innovative solutions in the fields of biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and diagnostics. The company has a long history of strategic acquisitions, including the purchase of Sigma-Aldrich in 2015, which significantly enhanced its life science portfolio.

### Market Impact
The acquisition of Bio-Techne is expected to have a significant impact on the life science market, as it will enable Merck KGaA to expand its product offerings and enhance its capabilities in the areas of protein and reagent production. Bio-Techne's portfolio of products, including its proprietary protein brands, will complement Merck KGaA's existing life science business, providing customers with a broader range of solutions for their research and development needs.

#### Financial Metrics
The acquisition is valued at $11.3 billion, representing a multiple of approximately 20 times Bio-Techne's adjusted EBITDA. The transaction is expected to be accretive to Merck KGaA's earnings per share in the first year after closing.
