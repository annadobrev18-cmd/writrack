### Recent Filing: Form PRE 14A
On April 10, 2026, International Seaways Inc filed a Form PRE 14A with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). This filing is a preliminary proxy statement that provides information about the company's upcoming annual meeting of shareholders. The filing includes details about the meeting, such as the date, time, and location, as well as information about the proposals to be voted on by shareholders.
