#### Key Drivers of Market Momentum
Several key drivers are likely to influence market momentum in the coming sessions. These include:
- **Earnings Reports**: The upcoming earnings reports from major tech companies will be closely watched by investors, as they will provide insights into the health of the sector and the overall market.

- **Global Economic Trends**: The ongoing impact of the pandemic on the global economy, as well as trends in international trade and commerce, will also influence market momentum.
