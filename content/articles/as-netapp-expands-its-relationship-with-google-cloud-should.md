NetApp, a leading provider of cloud data services, has recently expanded its relationship with Google Cloud. NetApp has been a key player in the cloud data services market, providing a range of solutions for managing and storing data in the cloud. Google Cloud, on the other hand, is one of the leading cloud infrastructure providers, offering a suite of cloud-based services for computing, storage, and networking. ### The Expanded Partnership
The expanded partnership between NetApp and Google Cloud includes several key components:
* **Deeper Integration**: NetApp's storage solutions will be more deeply integrated with Google Cloud's services, making it easier for customers to manage their data across multiple cloud environments.
* **New Solutions**: The two companies will develop new solutions for managing and analyzing data in the cloud, including artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) capabilities.
* **Increased Support**: NetApp will provide increased support for Google Cloud's customers, including 24/7 support and dedicated account management.
