## Custodia Bank's Legal Battle: An Overview
Custodia Bank, a digital asset bank, has been embroiled in a high-profile legal battle with the Federal Reserve. The bank had applied for a master account with the Fed, which would have granted it direct access to the central bank's payment systems. However, the Fed had initially rejected the application, citing concerns over the bank's business model and the potential risks associated with digital assets.

### Background on Master Accounts
A master account with the Federal Reserve is a critical component of the US payment system. It allows banks to settle transactions and manage their liquidity more efficiently. However, the Fed has traditionally been cautious in approving master accounts for new or unconventional banks, citing concerns over risk management and regulatory compliance.
