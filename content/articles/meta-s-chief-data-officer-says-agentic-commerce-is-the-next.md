## The Emergence of Agentic Commerce
Meta's Chief Data Officer has recently stated that Agentic Commerce is the next tier of business, marking a significant shift in the way companies operate and interact with customers. This new paradigm is expected to revolutionize the retail industry, enabling businesses to provide more personalized and immersive experiences for their customers.

### What is Agentic Commerce?
Agentic Commerce refers to the use of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to create autonomous agents that can interact with customers, understand their needs, and provide personalized recommendations. These agents can be integrated into various platforms, including social media, messaging apps, and e-commerce websites, to create a seamless and engaging shopping experience.

#### Key Characteristics of Agentic Commerce
Some of the key characteristics of Agentic Commerce include:
* **Autonomy**: Agents can operate independently, making decisions and taking actions without human intervention.
* **Personalization**: Agents can learn about individual customers and provide tailored recommendations and offers.
* **Immersive Experience**: Agents can create interactive and engaging experiences, using technologies such as augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR).
* **Real-time Feedback**: Agents can provide real-time feedback and support, enabling customers to make informed purchasing decisions.
