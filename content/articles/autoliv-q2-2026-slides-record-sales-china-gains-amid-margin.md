## Risk Factors and Challenges
Despite the positive Q2 2026 performance, Autoliv faces several risk factors and challenges that could impact its future growth. These include:

### Global Economic Uncertainty
The global automotive industry is heavily influenced by macroeconomic conditions. Economic downturns, trade tensions, and changes in government policies can affect vehicle sales and production, thereby impacting demand for Autoliv's products.

### Technological Disruption
The automotive industry is undergoing significant technological changes, with a focus on electrification, autonomous driving, and connectivity. While these trends present opportunities for suppliers like Autoliv, they also require significant investments in research and development to stay competitive.

### Supply Chain Disruptions
Autoliv, like many other suppliers, is vulnerable to supply chain disruptions. These can arise from various factors, including natural disasters, geopolitical events, and component shortages, potentially affecting the company's ability to deliver products to its customers.

The competitive landscape of the automotive safety systems market is characterized by a few large players, including Autoliv, and several smaller, specialized suppliers. ### Competitive Strengths
Autoliv's competitive strengths include its ability to innovate and adapt to changing technological and regulatory requirements. The company has a long history of developing safety-critical components and systems, which has enabled it to build a strong reputation among its customers.

### Competitive Weaknesses
