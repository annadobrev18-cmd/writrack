## The Rise of Agentic Artificial Intelligence
The world of technology is on the cusp of a revolution, driven by the emergence of agentic artificial intelligence (AI). This cutting-edge technology has the potential to transform industries and create new opportunities for growth. At the forefront of this revolution is Okta, a cloud-based identity and access management company. According to BMO Capital Markets, Okta's stock is poised for a 22% rise, driven by its position as an early winner in the agentic AI boom.

### Historical Context
To understand the significance of Okta's position in the agentic AI market, it's essential to look at the company's history. Founded in 2009, Okta has established itself as a leader in the identity and access management space. The company's cloud-based platform provides a secure and scalable solution for managing user identities and access to applications and data. Over the years, Okta has expanded its product offerings to include advanced security features, such as multi-factor authentication and behavioral analytics.

* 2017: Okta acquires Stormpath, a user management platform
