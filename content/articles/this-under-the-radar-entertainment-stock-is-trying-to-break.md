## Fundamentals of Live Nation Entertainment
Live Nation Entertainment is a leading live entertainment company that operates in the global market. The company's business model is built around promoting and producing live events, such as concerts, festivals, and theater productions. With a strong portfolio of brands, including Ticketmaster, Live Nation has established itself as a dominant player in the entertainment industry.

### Revenue Growth and Profitability
In recent years, Live Nation has demonstrated impressive revenue growth, driven by an increase in ticket sales and sponsorship revenue. The company's revenue has grown from $10.8 billion in 2020 to $14.6 billion in 2025, representing a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.5%. Additionally, Live Nation has improved its profitability, with net income increasing from $120 million in 2020 to $350 million in 2025.
