## Fundamentals of Quantum Computing
To understand the significance of MARA Holdings' initiative, it is essential to grasp the fundamentals of quantum computing and its potential impact on blockchain security. Quantum computers operate on a different paradigm than classical computers, using quantum bits or qubits to process information. This allows them to perform certain calculations much faster than classical computers, which could enable them to break encryption codes currently used in blockchain technology.

### Quantum Computing and Blockchain Security
The potential consequences of a quantum computer breaking blockchain encryption are severe:
* **Private key compromise**: A quantum computer could potentially factorize large numbers, compromising private keys and allowing unauthorized access to funds.
* **Transaction manipulation**: A quantum computer could manipulate transactions, enabling double-spending and other forms of fraud.
* **Network instability**: A quantum computer could destabilize the network, leading to a loss of trust and confidence in the cryptocurrency.
