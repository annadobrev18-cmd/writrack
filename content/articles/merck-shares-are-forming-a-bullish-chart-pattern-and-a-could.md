## Risk Factors
While Merck's shares appear to be poised for a breakout, there are several risk factors that investors should be aware of. These include:

The pharmaceutical sector is heavily regulated, and changes in regulations can have a significant impact on a company's profitability. For example, changes in pricing regulations or the introduction of new regulations governing the approval of new drugs can affect Merck's ability to generate revenue.

### Competitive Risks
The pharmaceutical sector is highly competitive, with several large players competing for market share. Merck faces competition from companies such as Pfizer, Johnson & Johnson, and Eli Lilly, which can affect its ability to generate revenue and maintain its market share.
