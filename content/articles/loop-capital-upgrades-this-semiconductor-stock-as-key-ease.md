#### Key Headwinds Easing
One of the primary reasons for Loop Capital's upgrade is the easing of key headwinds that have impacted Qualcomm's stock in recent years. These headwinds include:
* **Regulatory challenges**: Qualcomm has faced significant regulatory challenges in recent years, including a high-profile lawsuit with Apple and ongoing antitrust investigations. However, with many of these challenges now resolved, the company is free to focus on growth and innovation.
* **Competition**: The semiconductor industry is highly competitive, with many players vying for market share. However, Qualcomm's diversification of its product offerings and expansion into new markets has helped the company to stay ahead of the competition.
* **Global smartphone market slowdown**: The global smartphone market has experienced a slowdown in recent years, which has impacted Qualcomm's sales. However, with the company's expansion into new markets, such as the Internet of Things (IoT) and 5G, Qualcomm is well-positioned to drive growth despite a slowing smartphone market.
