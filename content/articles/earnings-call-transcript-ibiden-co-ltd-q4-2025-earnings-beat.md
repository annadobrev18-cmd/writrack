### Financial Highlights

The Q4 2025 earnings report revealed a significant increase in revenue, with the company posting a 15% year-over-year (YoY) growth. This was driven primarily by the strong demand for the company's electronic components, particularly in the automotive and industrial sectors. The company's net income also saw a substantial increase, rising by 20% YoY.

### Segment Analysis

Ibiden Co Ltd's business is divided into three main segments: Electronics, Ceramics, and Others. The Electronics segment, which accounts for the majority of the company's revenue, saw a significant increase in sales, driven by the strong demand for electronic components.

#### Electronics Segment

The Electronics segment posted a 18% YoY increase in revenue, with the company's automotive and industrial components business seeing particularly strong growth. The segment's operating margin also improved, rising to 20.5% from 18.1% in Q4 2024.

#### Ceramics Segment

The Ceramics segment saw a modest increase in revenue, with sales rising by 5% YoY. The segment's operating margin remained relatively stable, at 15.6% compared to 15.3% in Q4 2024.
