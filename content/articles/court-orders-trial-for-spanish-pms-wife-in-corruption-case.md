#### Impact on Foreign Investment
Foreign investment is a critical component of the Spanish economy, and corruption scandals can have a deterrent effect on potential investors. A decline in foreign investment could have far-reaching consequences, including reduced economic growth, lower tax revenues, and decreased competitiveness.
