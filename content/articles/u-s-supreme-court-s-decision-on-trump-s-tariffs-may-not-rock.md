The crypto market, being a relatively new and evolving space, has been influenced by these tariffs, albeit indirectly. The tariffs led to increased trade tensions, which, in turn, affected the global economy and, by extension, the crypto market.

### Market Impact: Potential Scenarios
The impact of the U.S. Supreme Court's decision on Trump's tariffs on the crypto market can be considered under several scenarios:

1. **Economic Uncertainty**: If the decision leads to increased economic uncertainty, investors might seek safe-haven assets, potentially benefiting cryptocurrencies.
2. **Trade Tensions**: Escalating trade tensions could negatively affect the global economy, which might, in turn, affect the crypto market, especially if investors become risk-averse.
