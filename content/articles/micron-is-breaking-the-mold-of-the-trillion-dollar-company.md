### Risk Factors: The Challenges Ahead
Despite Micron's unique position and impressive financial metrics, the company faces several challenges that could impact its future performance. The following section outlines some of the key risk factors associated with Micron.

#### Pricing Pressures
The memory chip market is subject to fluctuating demand and pricing pressures. The COVID-19 pandemic and subsequent supply chain disruptions have resulted in increased demand for memory chips, leading to higher prices. However, the market is expected to normalize in the coming months, which could lead to downward pressure on prices.

#### Competition from New Entrants
The memory chip market is witnessing the entry of new players, including Chinese companies such as Yangtze Memory Technologies. These new entrants could pose a significant challenge to Micron's market share and pricing power.
