## Fundamentals of Nvidia's Success
Nvidia has been a standout performer in the technology sector, with its stock price consistently outpacing the broader market. The company's success can be attributed to its dominant position in the field of artificial intelligence (AI), graphics processing units (GPUs), and high-performance computing. Nvidia's GPUs are used in a wide range of applications, including gaming, professional visualization, datacenter, and automotive.

### Historical Performance
Over the past year, Nvidia's stock has returned over 50%, significantly outperforming the S&P 500 index. The company's revenue has also shown impressive growth, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 20% over the past five years. This growth is expected to continue, driven by increasing demand for AI and machine learning technologies.
