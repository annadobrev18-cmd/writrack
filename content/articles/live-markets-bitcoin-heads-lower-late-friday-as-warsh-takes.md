#### The Impact of Interest Rates on Bitcoin
When the Fed raises interest rates, it can lead to a decrease in Bitcoin's price. This is because higher interest rates make alternative investments, such as bonds and stocks, more attractive, causing investors to move away from riskier assets like Bitcoin. On the other hand, when the Fed lowers interest rates, it can lead to an increase in Bitcoin's price, as investors seek higher returns in riskier assets.

## Market Impact: Warsh's Appointment and Bitcoin's Price
