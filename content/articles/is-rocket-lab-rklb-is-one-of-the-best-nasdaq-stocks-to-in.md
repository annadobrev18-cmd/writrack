## Fundamentals of Rocket Lab (RKLB)
Rocket Lab (RKLB) is a leading space technology company that has gained significant attention in the NASDAQ stock market. Founded in 2006 by Peter Beck, the company is headquartered in Long Beach, California, and has become a pioneer in the development of launch vehicles, satellites, and spacecraft components. With a strong focus on innovation and a commitment to making space accessible, Rocket Lab has established itself as a major player in the space industry.

### Key Products and Services
Rocket Lab's product portfolio includes the Electron launch vehicle, the Photon satellite platform, and a range of spacecraft components such as propulsion systems, power systems, and communication systems. The company's services include launch services, satellite manufacturing, and space systems development. With a strong track record of successful launches and a growing customer base, Rocket Lab has demonstrated its capability to deliver high-quality products and services to the space industry.
