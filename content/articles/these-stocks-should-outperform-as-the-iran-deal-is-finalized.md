### Fed Implications: Monetary Policy and Interest Rates
The Iran deal could also have implications for the Federal Reserve's monetary policy, particularly with regards to interest rates. A potential settlement could lead to increased economic growth, which may prompt the Fed to reassess its interest rate strategy. This, in turn, could impact the stock market, with potential beneficiaries including:
#### Interest Rate-Sensitive Sectors
- Financials: Banks and other financial institutions could see increased lending activity, driving up their stock prices.
- Real Estate: Lower interest rates could lead to increased demand for housing, benefiting real estate companies.

### Global Ripple Effects: A Broader Perspective
The Iran deal's implications extend beyond the US stock market, with potential ripple effects on the global economy. Some key areas to watch include:
#### Global Trade and Sanctions
