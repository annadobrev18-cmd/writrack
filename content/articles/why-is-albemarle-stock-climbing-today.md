Albemarle Corporation, a leading specialty chemicals company, has seen its stock climb significantly in recent trading sessions. This upward trend has caught the attention of investors and market analysts alike, prompting a closer examination of the factors driving this surge.

The company has been a major player in the lithium market, which has experienced significant growth due to the increasing demand for electric vehicle (EV) batteries.
