### Overview of Form 13F Filings

Form 13F is a quarterly report filed by institutional investment managers with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). The report discloses the manager's equity holdings and provides a snapshot of their investment portfolio. The filing is an essential tool for investors, researchers, and analysts to gain insights into the investment strategies and preferences of institutional managers.
