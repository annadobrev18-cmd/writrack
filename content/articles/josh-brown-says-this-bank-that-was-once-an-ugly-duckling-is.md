#### Key Reforms
Several key reforms contributed to the bank's resurgence:
- **Asset Quality Improvement**: The bank aggressively addressed its non-performing assets, significantly reducing its provision for loan losses.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Through digital transformation and process simplification, the bank achieved substantial cost savings, which were reinvested in growth initiatives.
- **Brand Revitalization**: A new branding strategy helped to reposition the bank as a modern, customer-centric institution, attracting a new wave of clients.

## Market Impact

- **Economic Conditions**: Economic downturns can affect the bank's asset quality and revenue growth, posing a risk to its turnaround story.
