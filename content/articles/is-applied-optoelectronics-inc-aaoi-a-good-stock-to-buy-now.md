## Overview of Applied Optoelectronics, Inc. (AAOI)
Applied Optoelectronics, Inc. (AAOI) is a leading provider of fiber-optic networking products, primarily serving the internet data center, cable television, and fiber-to-the-home markets. The company's product portfolio includes optical modules, lasers, and other components used in high-speed data transmission. As the demand for high-speed internet and cloud computing continues to grow, AAOI's products have become increasingly important for data centers and telecommunications companies.

### Historical Context
AAOI's stock has experienced significant volatility over the past few years. In 2017, the stock price surged to an all-time high of over $100 per share, driven by strong demand for the company's products and a booming data center market. However, the stock price declined sharply in 2018 due to increased competition, production delays, and a slowdown in demand from some of the company's major customers.
