### Historical Context
To understand the potential impact of autonomous vehicles on Lyft's stock, it's essential to look at the company's history. Lyft was founded in 2012 and quickly gained popularity as a ridesharing service. However, the company has struggled to turn a profit, with increasing competition from Uber and other ridesharing services. The COVID-19 pandemic also had a significant impact on Lyft's business, with a decline in ridesharing demand.
