### Historical Context: Russia-Ukraine Conflict
The conflict between Russia and Ukraine has been ongoing since 2014, with periods of relative calm punctuated by episodes of heightened violence. The annexation of Crimea by Russia in 2014 marked the beginning of this protracted dispute, which has resulted in significant economic and humanitarian costs. The ongoing conflict has led to a decline in investor confidence, affecting not only the Russian and Ukrainian economies but also the broader European and global markets.
