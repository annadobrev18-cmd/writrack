## Valuation and Risk Factors
The current overbought conditions of Deere and Quanta Services pose significant risks for investors. The 14-day relative strength index (RSI) for both stocks has surpassed 70, indicating that they may be due for a correction.

### Valuation Multiples
To assess the valuation of Deere and Quanta Services, we can examine their price-to-earnings (P/E) ratios. Deere's P/E ratio stands at around 25, while Quanta Services' P/E ratio is approximately 20. These multiples are relatively high compared to their industry peers, suggesting that the stocks may be overvalued.

### Risk Factors
Several risk factors could contribute to a potential correction in Deere and Quanta Services' stock prices. These include:
* Economic downturn: A decline in global economic growth could lead to reduced demand for Deere's products and Quanta Services' infrastructure services.
* Industry competition: Intensifying competition in the agricultural machinery and infrastructure services industries could erode Deere and Quanta Services' market share and profitability.
* Regulatory changes: Changes in government regulations or policies could negatively impact Deere and Quanta Services' operations and financial performance.
