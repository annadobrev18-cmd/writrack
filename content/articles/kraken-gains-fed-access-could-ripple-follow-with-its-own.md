### Historical Context: The Evolution of Crypto and Traditional Finance

The relationship between cryptocurrency and traditional finance has undergone significant changes over the years. Initially, the two were seen as separate entities, with cryptocurrency operating outside the realm of traditional finance. However, as the cryptocurrency market has grown and matured, there has been an increasing trend towards integration with traditional finance.

#### Key Milestones in Crypto-Traditional Finance Integration

* 2014: The first Bitcoin ATM is installed, marking one of the earliest attempts to integrate cryptocurrency with traditional finance.
* 2017: The Chicago Mercantile Exchange (CME) and the Chicago Board Options Exchange (CBOE) launch Bitcoin futures, providing a regulated platform for institutional investors to participate in the cryptocurrency market.
* 2020: PayPal announces that it will allow users to buy, sell, and hold cryptocurrency, further blurring the lines between cryptocurrency and traditional finance.
