### Technical Analysis
From a technical perspective, the stock is looking attractive. The relative strength index (RSI) is at 30, indicating that the stock is oversold. Additionally, the moving average convergence divergence (MACD) is showing a bullish crossover, suggesting that the stock is due for a rebound.

#### Chart Patterns
The stock's chart is showing a bullish inverse head and shoulders pattern, which is a reversal pattern. This pattern is formed when the stock makes a lower low, followed by a higher low, and then a higher high. The pattern is complete when the stock breaks out above the neckline, which is currently at $40.

#### Support and Resistance
