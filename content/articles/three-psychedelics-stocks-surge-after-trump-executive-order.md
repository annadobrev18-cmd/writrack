## Psychedelic Stocks Experience Sudden Surge
The stock market has witnessed a significant surge in psychedelic stocks following a recent executive order by former President Trump, paving the way for the use of these substances in mental health treatments. This development has sparked optimism among investors, with Oppenheimer predicting further upside for these stocks.

### Historical Context of Psychedelic Research
Psychedelic research has been ongoing for decades, with initial studies in the 1950s and 1960s exploring the potential therapeutic benefits of substances like LSD and psilocybin. However, due to the restrictive regulatory environment, research in this area was severely limited. The recent executive order marks a significant shift in the government's stance on psychedelic substances, potentially unlocking new avenues for treatment and investment opportunities.
