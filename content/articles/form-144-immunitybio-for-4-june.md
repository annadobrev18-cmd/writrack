## ImmunityBio Insider Activity: Understanding Form 144
The recent Form 144 filing by ImmunityBio on June 4 has garnered significant attention from investors and market analysts. This filing indicates that certain insiders of the company intend to sell a portion of their shares. To comprehend the implications of this filing, it is essential to delve into the details of Form 144 and its relevance to ImmunityBio's current market standing.

### What is Form 144?
Form 144 is a legal document filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) that notifies the public of an insider's intention to sell restricted securities. These securities can include stocks, bonds, or other financial instruments that were previously not available for public trading. The form is typically filed by executives, directors, or significant shareholders of a company.
