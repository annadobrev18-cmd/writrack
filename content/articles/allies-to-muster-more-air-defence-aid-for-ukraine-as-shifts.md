### Historical Context: Ukraine's Struggle for Independence

Ukraine's struggle for independence dates back to the early 1990s, when the country declared its sovereignty from the Soviet Union. However, the current conflict began in 2014, when Russia annexed Crimea, sparking a protracted and bloody conflict in the eastern regions of Ukraine. The international community has been engaged in a delicate balancing act, providing economic and military aid to Ukraine while avoiding direct confrontation with Russia.

#### Economic Sanctions and Their Impact on Global Markets

The imposition of economic sanctions on Russia has had a profound impact on global markets, particularly in the energy sector. The sanctions have restricted Russia's access to international markets, leading to a significant decline in oil and gas exports. This, in turn, has driven up energy prices, affecting businesses and consumers worldwide.
