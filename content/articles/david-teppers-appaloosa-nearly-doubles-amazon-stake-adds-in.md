Amazon has been a dominant player in the e-commerce space, and its cloud computing business, Amazon Web Services (AWS), has been a major driver of growth. With its diverse range of businesses, including advertising, artificial intelligence, and physical retail, Amazon is well-positioned to continue its upward trajectory.
