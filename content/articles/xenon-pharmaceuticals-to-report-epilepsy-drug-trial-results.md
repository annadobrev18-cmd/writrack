## Epilepsy Treatment Market Overview
The epilepsy treatment market is a growing sector, driven by the increasing prevalence of epilepsy and the need for effective treatments. Epilepsy is a neurological disorder that affects millions of people worldwide, with approximately 50 million people living with the condition. The current treatment options for epilepsy are limited, and there is a significant unmet need for new and innovative therapies.

### Market Size and Growth
The global epilepsy treatment market was valued at USD 8.3 billion in 2020 and is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.5% from 2020 to 2027. The market is driven by the increasing prevalence of epilepsy, advancements in technology, and the development of new treatments.
