### Overview of Cerebras Systems

Cerebras Systems is a California-based company that was founded in 2016 by Andrew Feldman and Michael James. The company's primary focus is on developing high-performance artificial intelligence computing systems, specifically designed for deep learning applications. Cerebras' flagship product, the WSE (Wafer Scale Engine), is the world's largest and most powerful AI-focused chip, designed to accelerate AI workloads and reduce the time and cost associated with training AI models.
