The Iran-US ceasefire memorandum, signed in 2020, was a significant development in the tumultuous relationship between the two nations. The agreement aimed to reduce tensions and prevent further conflict in the region. However, recent events have led to a fraying of the memorandum, causing concerns among investors and market analysts.

### Background of the Ceasefire Memorandum
The ceasefire memorandum was a result of intense diplomatic efforts between Iran, the US, and other regional players. The agreement included provisions for a reduction in military presence, an end to hostile rhetoric, and the establishment of a dialogue channel to resolve outstanding issues. The memorandum was seen as a positive step towards normalizing relations between Iran and the US, which had been strained since the 1979 Iranian Revolution.
