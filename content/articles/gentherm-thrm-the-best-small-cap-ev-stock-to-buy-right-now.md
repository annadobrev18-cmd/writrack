## Fundamentals of Gentherm (THRM)
Gentherm, a global developer and marketer of innovative thermal management technologies, has been gaining attention in the small-cap stock arena, particularly within the electric vehicle (EV) sector. Established in 1991 and headquartered in Northville, Michigan, Gentherm has evolved its product line to cater to the burgeoning demand for electric vehicles. The company's thermal management solutions are crucial for battery performance, passenger comfort, and overall vehicle efficiency.

### Historical Performance
Historically, Gentherm has demonstrated resilience and adaptability in the face of market fluctuations. Over the past five years, the company has shown a steady increase in revenue, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of approximately 10%. This growth is attributed to its strategic expansion into the EV market, coupled with its commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction.
