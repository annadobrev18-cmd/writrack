### Background on Form 144

Form 144 is a filing required by the SEC when an insider of a company intends to sell securities. The form provides information about the proposed sale, including the number of shares to be sold, the price at which they will be sold, and the identity of the seller. This information is crucial for investors, as it can indicate potential changes in the company's ownership structure and impact the stock price.
