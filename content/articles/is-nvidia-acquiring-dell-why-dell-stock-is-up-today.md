Dell, on the other hand, is a well-established player in the computer hardware and software industry, with a strong presence in the enterprise market.

The potential acquisition of Dell by Nvidia would create a tech giant with a diverse portfolio of products and services. The combined entity would have a strong presence in the gaming, enterprise, and datacenter markets, making it a formidable competitor to other tech giants like AMD, Intel, and HP.
