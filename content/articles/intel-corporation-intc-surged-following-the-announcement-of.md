## Intel Corporation's Recent Surge: Understanding the Context
Intel Corporation (INTC) has been a household name in the technology sector for decades, known for its dominance in the microprocessor market. However, the company has faced significant challenges in recent years, including increased competition from rival chipmakers and the rise of emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI) and the Internet of Things (IoT). Despite these challenges, Intel has been working to revamp its business strategy and stay ahead of the curve.

### Historical Context: Intel's Struggles and Opportunities
In the past, Intel has struggled to keep up with the rapid pace of innovation in the tech industry. The company's traditional business model, which focused on producing high-performance microprocessors for personal computers, has been disrupted by the shift towards mobile devices and cloud computing. Additionally, Intel has faced significant competition from rival chipmakers such as AMD and NVIDIA, which have been gaining market share in recent years.
