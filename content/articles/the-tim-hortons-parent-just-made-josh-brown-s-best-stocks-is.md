### Risk Factors
While QSR has a strong track record, there are several risk factors that investors should be aware of. These include:

* **Competition**: The quick-service restaurant industry is highly competitive, with many established players. QSR faces competition from McDonald's, Starbucks, and Yum! Brands, among others.
* **Regulatory risks**: QSR is subject to various regulations, including food safety and labor laws. Changes in regulations or non-compliance can impact the company's operations and reputation.
* **Economic risks**: QSR's business is sensitive to economic conditions, including recessions and changes in consumer spending habits.
