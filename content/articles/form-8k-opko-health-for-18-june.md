#### Key Milestones in OPKO Health's History
- 2007: OPKO Health's initial public offering (IPO) on the NASDAQ stock exchange.
- 2010: Acquisition of OPKO Diagnostics, marking the company's entry into the diagnostics market.
- 2013: Partnership with Pfizer to develop and commercialize somatrogon, a growth hormone deficiency treatment.
- 2020: Launch of the Rayaldee (calcifediol) extended-release capsules for the treatment of secondary hyperparathyroidism.
