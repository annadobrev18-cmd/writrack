## Shaq's Unconventional Investment Approach
The world of finance is not typically associated with NBA legends, but Shaquille O'Neal is breaking the mold. In a recent interview, Shaq revealed his unique investment strategy, which relies heavily on the consensus of his panel. The strategy is straightforward: if four out of five members of his panel like an investment, they'll do it.

### The Power of Diverse Perspectives
Shaq's approach highlights the importance of diverse perspectives in investment decisions. By surrounding himself with a team of experts from various backgrounds, Shaq can tap into their collective knowledge and experience. This approach can help mitigate the risks associated with individual biases and emotional decision-making.
