This move is expected to increase crypto adoption in the state, as local banks will be able to provide more personalized services to their customers. Moreover, this development is likely to attract more cryptocurrency businesses to the state, further boosting the local economy.

#### Key Benefits for Local Banks
The benefits of this move for local banks are numerous. For one, it allows them to tap into the growing demand for crypto-related services, which can help increase their revenue streams. Additionally, by offering crypto services, local banks can attract a new customer base, including cryptocurrency enthusiasts and businesses. This can help them stay competitive in a rapidly changing financial landscape.
