## Block Inc. Overview
Block Inc., formerly known as Square Inc., is a technology company that specializes in financial services and mobile payments. The company was founded in 2009 by Jack Dorsey and has since grown to become one of the leading players in the fintech industry. Block Inc.'s product portfolio includes the Square payment platform, Cash App, and TIDAL, among others.

### Business Segments
Block Inc. operates through two main business segments: Square and Cash App. The Square segment provides payment and point-of-sale solutions to merchants, while the Cash App segment offers a range of financial services to individuals, including peer-to-peer payments, bitcoin trading, and investing.

#### Square Segment
The Square segment is the largest contributor to Block Inc.'s revenue, accounting for approximately 70% of total sales. The segment provides a range of products and services to merchants, including payment processing, point-of-sale solutions, and online store management. Square's payment processing solutions allow merchants to accept credit and debit card payments, as well as other forms of payment such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.

#### Cash App Segment
The Cash App segment is a rapidly growing business for Block Inc., with revenue increasing by over 50% year-over-year in 2022. The segment provides a range of financial services to individuals, including peer-to-peer payments, bitcoin trading, and investing. Cash App also offers a debit card, known as the Cash Card, which allows users to spend their Cash App balance at merchants.
