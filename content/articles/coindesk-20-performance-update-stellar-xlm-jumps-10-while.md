### Fundamentals of Stellar (XLM)

Stellar is an open-source, decentralized blockchain network that facilitates cross-border payments and asset transfer. Its native cryptocurrency, XLM, is used to pay for transaction fees and serve as a bridge between different currencies. Stellar's network is designed to be fast, secure, and scalable, with the ability to process thousands of transactions per second.

#### Key Features of Stellar

* **Decentralized Exchange**: Stellar has a built-in decentralized exchange (DEX) that allows users to trade assets directly on the blockchain.
* **Multi-Asset Support**: Stellar supports a wide range of assets, including fiat currencies, cryptocurrencies, and commodities.
* **Low Transaction Fees**: Stellar's transaction fees are significantly lower than those of other blockchain networks, making it an attractive option for cross-border payments.
