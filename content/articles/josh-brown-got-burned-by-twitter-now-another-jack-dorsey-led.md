## Fundamentals of Block Inc.
Block Inc., formerly known as Square Inc., is a fintech company co-founded by Jack Dorsey, who also co-founded Twitter. The company has undergone significant transformations since its inception, expanding its services beyond mobile payments to include a range of financial and merchant services. Block Inc.'s stock has been a topic of interest among investors, particularly after Josh Brown, a well-known financial analyst, added it to his Best Stocks list.

### Historical Performance
To understand Block Inc.'s potential, it's essential to examine its historical performance. The company has demonstrated remarkable growth, with its revenue increasing from $9.5 billion in 2020 to $17.4 billion in 2022. This growth can be attributed to the expansion of its services, including the acquisition of Afterpay, a buy-now-pay-later platform.
