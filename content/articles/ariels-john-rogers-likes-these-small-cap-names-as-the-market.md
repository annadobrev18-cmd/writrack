## Current Market Landscape
The stock market has been experiencing a broadening rally, with small-cap stocks gaining significant attention from investors. Ariel Investments' John Rogers has been at the forefront of this trend, highlighting promising small-cap names that are poised for growth. One such company that has caught Rogers' eye is Covista, a for-profit education company.

## Historical Context of Small-Cap Stocks
Small-cap stocks have historically been a high-risk, high-reward investment opportunity. These companies typically have a market capitalization of less than $2 billion and are often characterized by their potential for rapid growth. However, they can also be highly volatile, making them a challenging investment option for risk-averse investors. Despite these challenges, small-cap stocks have consistently outperformed their large-cap counterparts over the long term, making them an attractive option for investors seeking high returns.
