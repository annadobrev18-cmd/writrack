### Historical Context
US-Iran relations have been strained for decades, with periods of heightened tension and brief moments of diplomacy. The 2015 Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA) aimed to curb Iran's nuclear program in exchange for relief from economic sanctions. However, the US withdrawal from the agreement in 2018 and the subsequent reimposition of sanctions have led to a deterioration in relations.
