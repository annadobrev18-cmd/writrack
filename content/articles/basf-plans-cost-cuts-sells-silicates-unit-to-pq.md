## BASF's Strategic Restructuring: An Overview
BASF, the German chemical giant, has announced plans to implement cost cuts and sell its silicates unit to PQ, a leading global supplier of specialty silicates. This move is part of the company's broader strategy to optimize its portfolio, reduce costs, and enhance its competitiveness in a rapidly changing market environment.

### Historical Context: BASF's Previous Restructuring Efforts
BASF has a long history of restructuring efforts, with the company having undergone several significant transformations over the years. In 2019, BASF announced a major restructuring program aimed at reducing costs and improving efficiency. The program, which included the sale of several non-core businesses, was expected to generate significant cost savings and improve the company's profitability.
