### Historical Context: Bitcoin's Price Volatility
Bitcoin has been known for its volatile nature since its inception. The cryptocurrency has experienced numerous price swings, with some being more drastic than others. For instance, in 2017, Bitcoin's price surged to nearly $20,000 before plummeting to around $3,000 in 2018.
