### Historical Context: Bitcoin's Price Volatility
Bitcoin has been known for its price volatility, with significant fluctuations in its value over the years. In 2020, the cryptocurrency experienced a remarkable surge, reaching an all-time high of nearly $65,000 in April 2021. However, this upward trend was short-lived, as Bitcoin's price plummeted to around $30,000 in July 2021. The cryptocurrency managed to recover and reach new heights in 2022, but the recent drop below $70,000 has raised concerns among investors.
