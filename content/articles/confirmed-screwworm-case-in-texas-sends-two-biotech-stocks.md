## Fundamentals of the Screwworm Outbreak
The recent confirmation of a screwworm case in Texas has sent shockwaves through the livestock industry, with significant implications for biotech companies specializing in animal health. Two companies, in particular, have seen a surge in their stock prices: Zoetis and Idexx Laboratories. As a senior market analyst, it is essential to delve into the fundamentals of this outbreak and its impact on these biotech stocks.

### What is Screwworm?
Screwworm, also known as Cochliomyia hominivorax, is a parasitic fly that infests livestock, causing significant damage to the animal's tissue and potentially leading to death. The screwworm was eradicated from the United States in the 1960s, but recent cases in Florida and now Texas have raised concerns about the potential for a widespread outbreak.
