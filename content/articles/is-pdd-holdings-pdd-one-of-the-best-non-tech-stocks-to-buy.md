### Sector Rotation and Global Ripple Effects
The recent sector rotation in the stock market has led to a shift in investor focus from tech stocks to non-tech stocks. This rotation has been driven by concerns over inflation, interest rates, and the potential for a recession. As a result, investors are seeking safe-haven assets and companies with stable cash flows.

PDD Holdings, with its strong financial performance and growth prospects, is well-positioned to benefit from this sector rotation. The company's e-commerce platform, Pinduoduo, has become an essential part of China's retail landscape, with over 700 million active users.
