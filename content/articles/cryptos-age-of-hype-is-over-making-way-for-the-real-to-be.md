## The End of an Era: Crypto's Age of Hype
The cryptocurrency market has undergone significant transformations since its inception. From the early days of Bitcoin to the current landscape of thousands of cryptocurrencies, the market has experienced periods of extreme hype and subsequent corrections. However, as we navigate the current market environment, it has become increasingly clear that crypto's age of hype is over, making way for the real infrastructure to be built.

### A Brief History of Crypto Hype
To understand the significance of this shift, it is essential to examine the history of crypto hype. The first major wave of hype occurred in 2017, with the price of Bitcoin reaching nearly $20,000. This was followed by a significant correction, with the price dropping to around $3,000. The subsequent years saw periods of increased adoption, regulatory clarity, and technological advancements, which contributed to the growth of the market.
