### Sector Analysis: E-commerce and Healthcare
The e-commerce and healthcare sectors, in which Amazon and Cigna Group operate, respectively, are experiencing significant growth. The rise of online shopping has been a boon for e-commerce companies like Amazon, while the healthcare sector has seen increased demand due to an aging population and a greater focus on health and wellness.

The e-commerce sector has been driven by advancements in technology, changes in consumer behavior, and the expansion of online marketplaces. Amazon, as a leader in this space, has been at the forefront of these trends, leveraging its vast resources to innovate and improve the customer experience.
