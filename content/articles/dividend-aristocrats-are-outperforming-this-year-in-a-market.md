### What are Dividend Aristocrats?

Dividend Aristocrats are a group of S&P 500 companies that have increased their dividend payments for 25 consecutive years or more. This exclusive club consists of companies that have demonstrated a commitment to sharing their profits with shareholders, providing a relatively stable source of income. The Dividend Aristocrats index is widely followed and has become a benchmark for investors seeking income-generating stocks.
