### Bull Call Spread Strategy

A bull call spread is a popular options trading strategy that involves buying a call option with a lower strike price and selling a call option with a higher strike price. This strategy is ideal for investors who are bullish on a stock but want to limit their risk exposure.

#### Bull Call Spread Example

* Buy 1 MSFT call option with a strike price of $250 (expiring in 2 months)
* Sell 1 MSFT call option with a strike price of $260 (expiring in 2 months)

The bull call spread strategy offers several benefits, including:

* Limited risk exposure: The maximum loss is limited to the premium paid for the call option.
* Potential for high returns: If MSFT's stock price surges above the higher strike price, the investor can earn a significant profit.
* Flexibility: The investor can adjust the strike prices and expiration dates to suit their risk tolerance and investment goals.
