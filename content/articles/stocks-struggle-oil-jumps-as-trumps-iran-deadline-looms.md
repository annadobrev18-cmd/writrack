## Geopolitical Uncertainty Weighs on Stocks
The stock market has been experiencing a period of high volatility in recent days, with the looming deadline set by the Trump administration for Iran to renegotiate the nuclear deal weighing heavily on investor sentiment. As the May 12 deadline approaches, investors are becoming increasingly nervous about the potential consequences of a failed agreement, including the possibility of renewed sanctions on Iran and the impact on global oil supplies.

### Historical Context: US-Iran Relations
The US-Iran relationship has been strained for decades, with the two countries having a complex and often contentious history. The 1979 Iranian Revolution, which saw the overthrow of the US-backed Shah, marked a significant turning point in relations between the two nations. Since then, the US has imposed numerous sanctions on Iran, citing concerns over the country's nuclear program and human rights record.
