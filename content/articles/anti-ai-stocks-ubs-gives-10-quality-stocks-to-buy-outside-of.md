In a recent report, UBS identified 10 quality stocks that offer solid diversification opportunities away from the dominant AI theme in the market. ### Market Impact: The Need for Diversification
The dominance of AI stocks in the market has led to concerns about over-concentration in portfolios. Diversification is a key strategy in managing risk and ensuring that investments are resilient to market fluctuations. By investing in a variety of sectors and themes, investors can mitigate the impact of any single sector experiencing a downturn. UBS's list of recommended stocks outside of AI offers investors a chance to achieve this diversification.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **What is the primary reason for diversifying a portfolio away from AI stocks?**
   - The primary reason is to manage risk by not over-concentrating investments in a single theme or sector, thereby protecting the portfolio from potential downturns in that sector.

2. **How can investors determine the right mix of stocks for diversification?**
   - Investors can determine the right mix by considering their investment goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon, and then allocating their investments across various sectors and asset classes to achieve a balanced portfolio.

3. **What role do dividend yields play in the selection of stocks for a diversified portfolio?**
