### Sector Rotations and Market Trends
The technology sector, particularly companies involved in cloud computing and AI, has experienced significant sector rotations in recent years. These rotations are driven by investor preferences for growth stocks over value stocks, especially during periods of economic uncertainty. ServiceNow, with its strong growth profile and AI-driven expansion strategy, is well-positioned to benefit from these trends.

Federal Reserve policies, including interest rate adjustments, can have profound effects on the stock market and sector rotations.
