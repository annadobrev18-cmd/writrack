## Adobe's Struggle and the 'SaaSpocalypse'
Adobe, a leader in the software industry, has faced significant challenges in recent times. The 'SaaSpocalypse,' a term coined to describe the downturn in software-as-a-service (SaaS) stocks, has had a profound impact on Adobe's stock price. However, with the help of a buyback and a strategic partnership with Nvidia, led by Jensen Huang, Adobe is attempting to climb out of the abyss.

### Historical Context
To understand the current situation, it's essential to look at Adobe's historical performance. The company has consistently delivered strong financial results, with revenue growth and expanding margins. However, the 'SaaSpocalypse' led to a significant decline in Adobe's stock price, with investors becoming increasingly cautious about the SaaS sector as a whole.
