The recent agreement among 7 OPEC+ countries to modestly expand monthly oil production has sent ripples through the global energy market. ### Historical Context: OPEC+'s Role in Oil Production
To understand the significance of this agreement, it is essential to delve into the historical context of OPEC+'s role in oil production. The Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC) was founded in 1960, with the primary objective of coordinating the production of oil among its member countries. Over the years, OPEC has played a crucial role in shaping the global oil market, often using its production levels to influence prices.
