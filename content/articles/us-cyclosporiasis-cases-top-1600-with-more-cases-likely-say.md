## Cyclosporiasis Outbreak: A Growing Concern for the US Healthcare Sector
The recent surge in cyclosporiasis cases in the US, with over 1,600 lab-confirmed cases reported, has significant implications for the healthcare sector. As health officials anticipate more cases, investors are closely watching the impact on healthcare stocks.

### Background on Cyclosporiasis
Cyclosporiasis is a foodborne and waterborne illness caused by the Cyclospora cayetanensis parasite. The parasite can contaminate fruits, vegetables, and other foods, leading to outbreaks. The symptoms of cyclosporiasis include diarrhea, abdominal cramps, fatigue, and weight loss.
