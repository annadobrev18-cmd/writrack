JPMorgan has initiated coverage on Customers Bancorp, a bank holding company, with an Overweight rating. This move is significant, as it reflects JPMorgan's confidence in Customers Bancorp's growth prospects and potential for long-term success.

### Background on Customers Bancorp
Customers Bancorp is a bank holding company that operates through its subsidiary, Customers Bank. The company provides a range of banking services, including commercial and consumer lending, deposit products, and investment management. Customers Bancorp has a strong presence in the Mid-Atlantic region, with operations in Pennsylvania, New York, New Jersey, and other states.
