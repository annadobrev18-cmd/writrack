## Insider Activity: A Key Indicator of Market Sentiment
The recent filing of Form 144 by FNB Corp has sparked interest among investors, as it indicates insider activity that could potentially influence the company's stock price. Form 144 is a notice of proposed sale of securities, which must be filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) by insiders who plan to sell securities. This filing provides valuable insights into the company's internal dynamics and can be a key indicator of market sentiment.

### Understanding Form 144
Form 144 is a required filing for insiders who intend to sell securities, including company executives, directors, and significant shareholders. The form provides details about the proposed sale, including the number of shares to be sold, the price range, and the intended use of proceeds. By analyzing Form 144 filings, investors can gain insights into the company's internal dynamics and potential future developments.
