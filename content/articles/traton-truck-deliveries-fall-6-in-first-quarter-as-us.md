## Traton's Q1 Performance: A Review of the Numbers
Traton, a leading commercial vehicle manufacturer, has reported a 6% decline in truck deliveries for the first quarter of 2026. This drop in sales is primarily attributed to the persistent weakness in the US market. The company's Q1 performance has raised concerns among investors and analysts, who are closely monitoring the situation to assess its impact on the overall industry.

### Historical Context: Traton's US Market Presence
Traton has a significant presence in the US market, with its subsidiaries, including Scania and MAN, operating in the region. However, the company has been facing challenges in the US market, including intense competition, regulatory changes, and shifting consumer preferences. In 2020, Traton's US sales accounted for approximately 20% of its total global sales, highlighting the importance of this market for the company's overall performance.
