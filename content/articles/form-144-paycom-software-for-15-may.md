### What is a Form 144 Filing?
A Form 144 filing is a notice filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) by an insider of a publicly traded company who intends to sell restricted or control securities. This filing is required under Rule 144 of the Securities Act of 1933 and provides the public with information about the proposed sale, including the number of shares to be sold, the identity of the seller, and the proposed sale price.
