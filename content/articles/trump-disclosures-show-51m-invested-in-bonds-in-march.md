## Fundamentals of Bond Investing
Bond investing is a popular strategy among investors seeking predictable income and relatively low risk. Bonds are essentially loans made to borrowers, who promise to repay the principal amount with interest. The fundamentals of bond investing include:

* **Credit Risk**: The risk that the borrower will default on the loan
* **Interest Rate Risk**: The risk that changes in interest rates will affect the bond's value
* **Liquidity Risk**: The risk that the bond cannot be easily sold or traded
