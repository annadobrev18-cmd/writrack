#### Key Events in US-Iran Relations
- 1979: The Iranian Revolution leads to the overthrow of the US-backed Shah, resulting in a significant deterioration of relations between the two nations.
- 2015: The Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA) is signed, aiming to limit Iran's nuclear program in exchange for relief from economic sanctions.
- 2018: The US withdraws from the JCPOA, reimposing sanctions on Iran and leading to a significant escalation of tensions.
- 2020: The US and Iran engage in a series of military exchanges, bringing the two nations to the brink of war.
- 2022: Diplomatic efforts resume, with the US and Iran engaging in indirect talks to revive the JCPOA.
