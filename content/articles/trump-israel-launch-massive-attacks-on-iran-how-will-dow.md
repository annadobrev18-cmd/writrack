### Historical Context: US-Iran Relations

To understand the current situation, it's essential to examine the historical context of US-Iran relations. The two countries have been at odds since the 1979 Iranian Revolution, which overthrew the US-backed Shah of Iran. Since then, the US has imposed numerous economic sanctions on Iran, citing concerns over its nuclear program and human rights record. In 2018, the US withdrew from the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA), a nuclear deal signed between Iran, the US, and other world powers. This move led to a significant escalation of tensions between the two countries.
