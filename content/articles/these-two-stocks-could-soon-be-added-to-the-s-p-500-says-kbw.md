## S&P 500 Inclusion: A Catalyst for Growth
The S&P 500 index is widely considered a benchmark for the US stock market, and inclusion in this index can have a significant impact on a company's stock price. Recently, KBW (Keefe, Bruyette & Woods) predicted that Rocket and UWM (United Wholesale Mortgage) could soon be added to the S&P 500. This prediction is based on the fact that Rocket is now the sixth-largest company that's eligible for inclusion in the S&P 500.

### Historical Context: S&P 500 Inclusion
In the past, companies that have been added to the S&P 500 have seen their stock prices increase significantly. This is because inclusion in the index leads to increased visibility and investor attention, which can result in higher trading volumes and a higher stock price. For example, when Zoom Video Communications was added to the S&P 500 in 2020, its stock price increased by over 10% in a single day.
