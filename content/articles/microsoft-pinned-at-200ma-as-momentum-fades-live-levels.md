## Microsoft's Stock Price Movement
Microsoft's stock price has been pinned at its 200-day moving average (200MA) as momentum fades. This is a significant technical indicator, as the 200MA is often used to determine the overall trend of a stock. When a stock is trading above its 200MA, it is generally considered to be in an uptrend, while trading below it indicates a downtrend.

### Historical Data
Looking at Microsoft's historical data, we can see that the stock has been trading in a range between $230 and $280 over the past year. The stock price has been unable to break above the $280 level, which has acted as a resistance point. On the other hand, the $230 level has acted as a support point, with the stock price bouncing off this level on several occasions.
