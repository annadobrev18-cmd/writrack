## Bitcoin's Recent Rally
Bitcoin has hit a two-week high, surging above $65,500. This sudden increase in price can be attributed to the recent US-Iran deal, which has sent oil prices sliding. The deal, aimed at reviving the 2015 nuclear agreement, has eased tensions between the two nations, resulting in a decrease in oil prices.

### Historical Context
The relationship between Bitcoin and oil prices has been a topic of interest in recent years. Historically, Bitcoin has been seen as a hedge against inflation and a store of value, similar to gold. However, its correlation with oil prices has been less straightforward. In the past, when oil prices have dropped, Bitcoin's price has not always followed suit. Nevertheless, the current situation seems to be different, with Bitcoin's price surging as oil prices slide.
