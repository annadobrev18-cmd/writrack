## Technical Analysis
Technical analysis is a crucial tool for evaluating the potential of biotech stocks. By examining charts and trends, investors can gain insights into the stock's performance and make informed decisions.

### Chart Patterns
One of the most attractive chart patterns in biotech stocks is the ascending triangle. This pattern indicates a bullish trend, with the stock price increasing over time. The ascending triangle is formed by a series of higher highs and higher lows, indicating a strong uptrend.

#### Key Technical Indicators
Some key technical indicators to watch in biotech stocks include:
* Moving Averages: A 50-day moving average above a 200-day moving average indicates a bullish trend.
* Relative Strength Index (RSI): An RSI below 30 indicates oversold conditions, while an RSI above 70 indicates overbought conditions.
* Bollinger Bands: A stock price above the upper Bollinger Band indicates overbought conditions, while a stock price below the lower Bollinger Band indicates oversold conditions.

## Sector Rotations
Sector rotations can have a significant impact on biotech stocks. A rotation into the healthcare sector can lead to increased investment in biotech stocks, driving up prices.

### Global Ripple Effects
The biotech industry is a global market, and events in one region can have a ripple effect on the entire industry. For example, a breakthrough in gene editing technology in the United States can lead to increased investment in biotech stocks globally.
