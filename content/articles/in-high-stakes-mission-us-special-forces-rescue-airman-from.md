### Historical Context: US-Iran Relations

To understand the potential impact on global markets, it's essential to examine the historical context of US-Iran relations. The two countries have been at odds since the 1979 Iranian Revolution, with periods of heightened tension and occasional diplomacy. The current situation is particularly volatile, with the US withdrawal from the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA) in 2018 and the subsequent reimposition of sanctions on Iran.

#### Key Events in US-Iran Relations

* 1979: Iranian Revolution and US embassy hostage crisis
* 1980s: Iran-Iraq War and US support for Iraq
* 2002: US invasion of Iraq and regime change
* 2015: JCPOA signed, lifting sanctions on Iran
* 2018: US withdrawal from JCPOA and reimposition of sanctions
