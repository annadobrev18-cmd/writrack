## Booking Holdings Inc. (BKNG) Overview
Booking Holdings Inc. (BKNG) is a leading online travel agency that provides services in booking hotel rooms, airline tickets, rental cars, and other travel-related services. The company operates several brands, including Booking.com, Priceline, Agoda, and Kayak. With a market capitalization of over $100 billion, BKNG is one of the largest players in the online travel industry.

### Recent News: Stephen Mandel Trims Position
Recently, it was reported that Stephen Mandel, a well-known hedge fund manager, has trimmed his position in Booking Holdings Inc. (BKNG). This news has sparked interest among investors, as Mandel's fund, Lone Pine Capital, has been a significant shareholder of BKNG. The reduction in position size may indicate a shift in Mandel's investment strategy or a change in his outlook on the company's prospects.
