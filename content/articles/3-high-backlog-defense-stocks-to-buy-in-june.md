## High Backlog Defense Stocks: A Safe Haven for Investors
The defense industry has long been a staple of the stock market, providing a safe haven for investors looking for stable and secure returns. One key metric that investors look for when evaluating defense stocks is backlog, which refers to the amount of orders that a company has received but has not yet fulfilled. A high backlog is a good indicator of a company's future revenue and profitability.

### What is Backlog and Why is it Important?
Backlog is a critical metric for defense companies, as it provides a clear picture of a company's future revenue stream. A high backlog indicates that a company has a strong pipeline of orders, which can provide a sense of security and stability for investors. Additionally, a high backlog can also indicate that a company is well-positioned to take advantage of future growth opportunities.
