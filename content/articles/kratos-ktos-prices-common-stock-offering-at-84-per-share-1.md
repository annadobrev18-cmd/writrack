## Kratos Defense & Security Solutions Inc. Prices Common Stock Offering
Kratos Defense & Security Solutions Inc. (KTOS) has priced its common stock offering at $84 per share, expecting to generate approximately $1.17 billion in proceeds. This strategic move is set to bolster the company's financial position, enabling it to capitalize on emerging opportunities in the defense sector.

### Background and Rationale
The decision to issue common stock is a testament to Kratos' confidence in its growth prospects and the attractiveness of its value proposition to investors. The company has been at the forefront of innovation in the defense and security solutions space, providing a range of services and products that cater to the evolving needs of its clients. By raising capital through this offering, Kratos aims to strengthen its balance sheet, reduce debt, and enhance its financial flexibility.
