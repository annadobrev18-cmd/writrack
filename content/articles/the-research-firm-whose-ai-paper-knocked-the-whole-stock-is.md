### Citrini Research's Call: A Warning for Equities

#### Key Factors Driving Citrini's Call
There are several key factors driving Citrini's call. These include:

* **High energy prices**: Energy prices have been trending upward over the past few years, driven by a combination of factors including supply chain disruptions, geopolitical tensions, and increasing demand.
* **Reduced consumer spending power**: High energy prices can lead to reduced consumer spending power, as households are forced to allocate a larger portion of their income towards energy expenses.
* **Decreased corporate earnings**: High energy prices can also lead to decreased corporate earnings, as companies face increased production costs and reduced demand for their products.

### Risk Factors: What Investors Need to Consider
When evaluating the potential impact of high energy prices on equities, investors need to consider several risk factors. These include:
