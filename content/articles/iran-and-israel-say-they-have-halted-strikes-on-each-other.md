#### Key Events Leading Up to the Ceasefire
Several key events have contributed to the current state of affairs:
* The US withdrawal from the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA) in 2018, which led to a significant increase in tensions between Iran and the West.
* The subsequent attacks on Saudi oil facilities in 2019, which were attributed to Iran-backed militias.
* The targeted killing of top Iranian military commander Qasem Soleimani by the US in January 2020, which sparked a wave of retaliatory attacks.
