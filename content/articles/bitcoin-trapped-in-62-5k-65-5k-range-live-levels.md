## Bitcoin's Current Price Conundrum
Bitcoin's price has been trading in a tight range between $62,500 and $65,500, leaving investors and traders wondering what's next for the cryptocurrency. This range-bound trading has been ongoing for several days, with the price failing to break out above the resistance level of $65,500 or fall below the support level of $62,500.

### Historical Context
To understand the significance of this range, it's essential to look at the historical context of Bitcoin's price movements. In the past, Bitcoin has experienced similar range-bound trading periods, which have often been followed by significant price movements. For example, in 2020, Bitcoin's price was stuck in a range between $8,000 and $10,000 for several months before breaking out above $10,000 and eventually reaching an all-time high of over $64,000.
