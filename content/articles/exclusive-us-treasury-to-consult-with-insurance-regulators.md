### Private Credit Lending: An Overview

Private credit lending has experienced rapid growth in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for alternative financing options. This sector provides loans to businesses and individuals who may not qualify for traditional bank financing. The private credit lending market has attracted a wide range of investors, including hedge funds, private equity firms, and insurance companies.
