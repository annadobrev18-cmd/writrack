## Risk Factors and Challenges
Despite the positive outlook, Extra Space Storage faces certain risks and challenges that could impact its performance. These include:

The self-storage industry is highly competitive, with numerous players competing for market share. Intensifying competition could lead to decreased occupancy rates and rental income.

### Regulatory Environment
Changes in local zoning laws, environmental regulations, and tax policies could affect the development and operation of self-storage facilities.

### Economic Downturn
An economic downturn could reduce demand for self-storage units, negatively impacting Extra Space Storage's revenue and profitability.
