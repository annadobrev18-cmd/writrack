Stockton has identified three industrial stocks that she believes have the potential for strong moves higher. These stocks are:

## Market Impact
The industrial sector has a significant impact on the overall economy, as it provides the necessary goods and services for construction, manufacturing, and other industries. A strong industrial sector can lead to increased economic growth, job creation, and higher demand for raw materials. On the other hand, a weak industrial sector can lead to decreased economic growth, job losses, and lower demand for raw materials.
