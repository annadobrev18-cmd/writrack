This move marks a significant milestone in the adoption of cryptocurrency for mainstream payments. The integration of XRP into Rakuten's payment system is expected to enhance the user experience, providing customers with a fast, secure, and low-cost payment option.

### Background on Rakuten and XRP
Rakuten is one of the largest e-commerce companies in Japan, offering a wide range of products and services, including online shopping, digital content, and financial services. XRP, on the other hand, is a cryptocurrency developed by Ripple, designed for fast and low-cost cross-border payments. With a market capitalization of over $20 billion, XRP is one of the most widely recognized and traded cryptocurrencies in the world.
