## Storebrand Asset Management's 13F Filing: An Overview
Storebrand Asset Management's 13F filing for June 17 has been released, providing insight into the company's investment strategy and portfolio composition. The 13F filing is a quarterly report that institutional investment managers with over $100 million in assets under management are required to submit to the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). This report provides a snapshot of the company's holdings at the end of the quarter, allowing investors and analysts to gain a deeper understanding of their investment approach.

### Historical Context
Storebrand Asset Management is a Norwegian asset management company that has been in operation for over 20 years. The company has a long history of managing assets for institutional clients, including pension funds, insurance companies, and sovereign wealth funds. Storebrand Asset Management's investment strategy is focused on generating long-term returns for its clients, with a strong emphasis on environmental, social, and governance (ESG) considerations.
