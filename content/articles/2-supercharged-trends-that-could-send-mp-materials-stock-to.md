## Risk Factors
While MP Materials has strong growth prospects, there are several risk factors that investors should consider.

### Dependence on Rare Earth Materials
The company's business is heavily dependent on the demand for rare earth materials, which can be volatile. Any decline in demand for these materials could negatively impact the company's revenue and profitability.

### Competition from Chinese Producers
MP Materials faces significant competition from Chinese producers, who have lower production costs and can offer rare earth materials at lower prices. This competition could negatively impact the company's market share and revenue.

### Regulatory Risks
The rare earth materials industry is subject to various regulations and laws, which can impact the company's operations and profitability. Any changes in regulations or laws could negatively impact the company's business.
