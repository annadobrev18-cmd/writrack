### Market Impact: Trading Strategies
Jay Woods, a seasoned NYSE insider, offered trading strategies for investors looking to capitalize on the upcoming earnings reports from Delta Air Lines and Constellation Brands. Woods emphasized the importance of understanding the companies' historical performance, industry trends, and market expectations. He also highlighted the need for investors to be prepared for potential surprises, such as earnings beats or misses, which can significantly impact stock prices.

#### Delta Air Lines: Trading Strategies
For Delta Air Lines, Woods recommended a bullish approach, citing the company's strong brand, loyal customer base, and ongoing efforts to improve operational efficiency. He suggested that investors consider buying call options or establishing a long position in the stock, anticipating a potential earnings beat.

#### Constellation Brands: Trading Strategies
In contrast, Woods adopted a more cautious approach for Constellation Brands, citing the company's high valuation multiples and the potential for increased competition in the beverage industry. He recommended that investors consider buying put options or establishing a short position in the stock, anticipating a potential earnings miss.
