## Fundamentals of Clipper Realty
Clipper Realty Inc. is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates a portfolio of residential and commercial properties in the United States. The company's primary focus is on multifamily properties, which account for the majority of its revenue. Clipper Realty's properties are located in high-growth markets, including New York City, Washington D.C., and Philadelphia.

### Historical Performance
Clipper Realty has a history of delivering stable cash flows and steady growth in its funds from operations (FFO). However, the company's performance has been impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic, which has resulted in reduced rent collections and increased vacancy rates. In 2020, Clipper Realty's FFO per share decreased by 10% compared to the previous year, primarily due to the pandemic's impact on its operations.
