## EZCORP, Inc. (EZPW) Overview
EZCORP, Inc. (EZPW) is a leading provider of pawn loans in the United States and Latin America. The company operates through two primary segments: Pawn and Retail Merchandise. The Pawn segment offers pawn loans, which are non-recourse loans collateralized by tangible personal property, while the Retail Merchandise segment sells merchandise, including jewelry, electronics, tools, and other items, to customers.
