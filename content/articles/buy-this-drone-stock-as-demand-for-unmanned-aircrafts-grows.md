## The Rise of Drone Technology
The use of drones, also known as unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), has been increasing rapidly over the past few years. What was once considered a niche market has now become a thriving industry, with applications in various sectors such as military, commercial, and recreational. The growth of the drone market can be attributed to advancements in technology, decreasing costs, and increasing demand for unmanned aircrafts.

### Historical Context
The concept of drones has been around for decades, but it wasn't until the early 2000s that the technology started to gain traction. The first commercial drones were used for surveillance and monitoring purposes, but as the technology improved, new applications emerged. Today, drones are used in a wide range of industries, including agriculture, construction, and film production.
