## Bitcoin's Recent Rally
Bitcoin's price has been on a tear lately, with the cryptocurrency surging to new heights in recent weeks. The rally has been fueled by a combination of factors, including increasing institutional investment, improving sentiment, and a declining US dollar. However, the rally may be facing a key hurdle with the upcoming Federal Reserve meeting on Wednesday.

### Impact of Fed Meeting on Bitcoin
The Fed meeting is widely expected to result in an interest rate hike, which could have a negative impact on Bitcoin's price. Higher interest rates tend to strengthen the US dollar, making it more attractive to investors and potentially leading to a decrease in demand for alternative assets like Bitcoin. Additionally, higher interest rates can also increase the cost of borrowing, which could reduce the amount of money available for investment in the crypto market.

### Security Risks
Security risks are also a significant concern for Bitcoin investors. The cryptocurrency's decentralized nature makes it vulnerable to hacking and other forms of cyber attacks.
