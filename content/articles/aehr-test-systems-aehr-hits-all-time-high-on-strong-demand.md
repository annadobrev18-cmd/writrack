### Overview of Aehr Test Systems (AEHR)

Aehr Test Systems (AEHR) is a semiconductor test and burn-in equipment supplier that provides solutions for the testing and validation of semiconductor devices. The company's products are used by leading semiconductor manufacturers to test and validate their devices, ensuring that they meet the required standards for quality and reliability. AEHR's product portfolio includes a range of test and burn-in systems, including the ABTS (Advanced Burn-in and Test System) and the FOX (Full wafer on Board Level Test and Burn-in System).
