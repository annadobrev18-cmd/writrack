## The Rise of Prediction Markets
The recent $50 billion World Cup breakout has marked a significant milestone in the history of sports betting, with prediction markets outperforming traditional sportsbooks. This shift in the betting landscape is largely attributed to the increasing adoption of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology, which has enabled the creation of decentralized and transparent prediction markets.

### Key Drivers of Growth
Several factors have contributed to the growth of prediction markets, including:
- **Decentralized nature**: Prediction markets operate on blockchain networks, allowing for decentralized and trustless transactions.
- **Transparency**: All transactions and bets are recorded on a public ledger, ensuring transparency and accountability.
- **Lower fees**: Prediction markets often charge lower fees compared to traditional sportsbooks, making them more attractive to bettors.
- **Wider range of markets**: Prediction markets offer a wider range of betting options, including exotic markets and micro-bets.
