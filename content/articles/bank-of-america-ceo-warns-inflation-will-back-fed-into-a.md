### Valuation and Risk Factors
The impact of inflation on valuation and risk factors cannot be overstated. As inflation rises, the value of money decreases, which can lead to higher costs for businesses and consumers. This, in turn, can affect the valuation of assets, including stocks and bonds. The risk factors associated with inflation include the potential for reduced purchasing power, increased costs for borrowing, and decreased demand for certain goods and services.
