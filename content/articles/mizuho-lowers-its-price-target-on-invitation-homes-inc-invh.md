## Fundamentals of Invitation Homes Inc. (INVH)
Invitation Homes Inc. (INVH) is a leading Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) that owns and operates a large portfolio of single-family homes for lease in the United States. The company was founded in 2012 and is headquartered in Dallas, Texas. INVH's primary business strategy is to acquire, renovate, and lease single-family homes in desirable neighborhoods, providing residents with high-quality, affordable housing options.

### Business Model
INVH's business model is centered around the acquisition and management of single-family homes. The company identifies attractive markets with strong demand for rental housing and acquires homes through various channels, including bulk purchases, auctions, and individual sales. Once acquired, the homes are renovated to meet INVH's standards and then leased to residents. The company's management platform is designed to provide residents with a seamless leasing experience, including online applications, digital payments, and 24/7 customer support.
