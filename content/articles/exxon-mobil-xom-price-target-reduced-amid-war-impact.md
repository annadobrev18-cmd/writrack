### Historical Context of Exxon Mobil
Exxon Mobil has a long history dating back to 1870 when John D. Rockefeller and his associates formed the Standard Oil Company. Over the years, the company has undergone numerous transformations, mergers, and acquisitions, eventually becoming the Exxon Mobil we know today. The company's success has been largely attributed to its ability to adapt to changing market conditions and its strategic investments in oil and gas exploration, production, and refining.
