## Fundamentals of Block, Inc. (SQ)
Block, Inc. (SQ), formerly known as Square, Inc., is a technology company that creates tools to help sellers start, run, and grow their businesses. The company's segments include Square and Cash App. The Square segment offers tools for businesses, such as point-of-sale software, payment processing, and financial services. The Cash App segment provides financial services to individuals, including the ability to send, spend, and invest money.
