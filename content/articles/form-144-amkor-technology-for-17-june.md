## Fundamentals of Amkor Technology
Amkor Technology, Inc. (AMKR) is a leading provider of semiconductor packaging and test services. The company's services include package design, wafer bumping, wafer level packaging, and test services. Amkor's customers include leading semiconductor companies, and its services are used in a wide range of applications, including consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial.
