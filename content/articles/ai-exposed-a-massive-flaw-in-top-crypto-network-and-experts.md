The recent revelation that AI has uncovered a massive flaw in a top crypto network has sent shockwaves throughout the financial and technological communities. ### Historical Context of Crypto Vulnerabilities
Cryptocurrencies and their underlying blockchain technology have been touted for their security and transparency. However, this is not the first time a significant vulnerability has been discovered in a crypto network. Historically, there have been instances where bugs or flaws have led to significant financial losses. The most notable example is the DAO hack on the Ethereum network in 2016, which resulted in the theft of millions of dollars' worth of ether. This event led to a hard fork of the Ethereum blockchain, creating Ethereum Classic.
