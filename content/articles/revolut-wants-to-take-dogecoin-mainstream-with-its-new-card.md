By integrating Dogecoin into its payment card, Revolut aims to provide users with a seamless and convenient way to make transactions using the cryptocurrency.

### Historical Context
Dogecoin, which was created as a joke in 2013, has gained significant traction over the years. Its popularity can be attributed to its active community, charitable endeavors, and growing acceptance as a form of payment. However, despite its growing popularity, Dogecoin has faced challenges in terms of mainstream adoption. Revolut's move to support Dogecoin on its physical payment card could be a significant step towards overcoming these challenges.
