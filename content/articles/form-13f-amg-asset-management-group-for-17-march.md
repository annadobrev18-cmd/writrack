### Historical Context of 13F Filings

To appreciate the significance of AMG Asset Management Group's 13F filing, it is essential to understand the historical context of these filings. The Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) requires institutional investment managers with over $100 million in equity assets under management to submit a 13F filing on a quarterly basis. This filing discloses the manager's equity holdings, providing a snapshot of their investment portfolio at the end of each quarter.
