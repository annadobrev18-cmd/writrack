## Treasury Yields Hit Two-Month High
The recent escalation of tensions between the US and Iran, coupled with a surge in oil prices, has led to a significant increase in Treasury yields. This surge, which has seen yields reach a two-month high, is a direct response to the heightened geopolitical uncertainty and the potential impact on global oil supplies.

### Impact of Iran Tensions on Oil Prices
The tensions between the US and Iran have been escalating over the past few months, with the US imposing sanctions on Iranian oil exports. This has led to a decrease in global oil supplies, resulting in a surge in oil prices. The increase in oil prices has a direct impact on the global economy, as higher oil prices can lead to higher inflation and slower economic growth.
