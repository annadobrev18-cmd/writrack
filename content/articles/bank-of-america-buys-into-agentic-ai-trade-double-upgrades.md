## Global Ripple Effects: Trade Wars and Supply Chain Disruptions
The CPU market is a global industry, and as such, it is susceptible to trade wars and supply chain disruptions. ### Trade War Impact on CPU Market
The trade war between the US and China has had a significant impact on the CPU market. The imposition of tariffs on Chinese imports has led to increased costs for many companies, which has resulted in decreased demand for CPUs. Additionally, the trade war has also led to a decrease in foreign investment in the US, which has further exacerbated the decline in demand.
