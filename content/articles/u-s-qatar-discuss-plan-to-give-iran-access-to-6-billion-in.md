### Historical Context: Iran's Frozen Assets
Iran's frozen assets are a result of the economic sanctions imposed by the US and other Western countries in response to the country's nuclear program. The sanctions, which were tightened in 2018, have severely limited Iran's access to the global financial system, effectively freezing billions of dollars in assets. The $6 billion in question is a fraction of the estimated $100 billion in Iranian assets frozen worldwide.
