## Valley National Bancorp: An Overview
Valley National Bancorp, the holding company for Valley National Bank, is a regional bank headquartered in Wayne, New Jersey. The bank operates across New Jersey, New York, Florida, and Alabama, offering a range of financial services including commercial and consumer loans, deposit products, and investment services. As of 2026, Valley National Bancorp has approximately $54 billion in assets and over 200 branches across its operating regions.
