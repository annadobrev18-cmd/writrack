The recent U.S.-Iran deal has sent shockwaves through the energy sector, with Exxon's stock price taking a significant hit. As an investor, it's essential to understand the context behind this development and its potential implications for Exxon's future prospects.

### Historical Context: U.S.-Iran Relations And The Energy Sector
The U.S.-Iran relationship has been complex and often tumultuous, with significant implications for the global energy landscape. The 2015 Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA) aimed to curb Iran's nuclear program in exchange for relief from economic sanctions. However, the U.S. withdrawal from the deal in 2018 and subsequent reimposition of sanctions had a devastating impact on Iran's economy and the global energy market.
