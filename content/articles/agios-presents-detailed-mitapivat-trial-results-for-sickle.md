## Agios Pharmaceuticals: A Leader in Orphan Diseases
Agios Pharmaceuticals, a biotechnology company, has been at the forefront of developing innovative treatments for orphan diseases. The company's focus on rare genetic disorders has led to the development of several promising therapies, including mitapivat, a potential treatment for sickle cell disease.

### Mitapivat: A Novel Approach to Sickle Cell Disease
Mitapivat is an oral, small molecule pyruvate kinase activator that has shown significant promise in treating sickle cell disease. The therapy works by activating the pyruvate kinase enzyme, which plays a critical role in the production of adenosine triphosphate (ATP), the primary energy source for red blood cells. By increasing ATP levels, mitapivat aims to reduce the symptoms of sickle cell disease, including anemia, pain crises, and organ damage.
