## RELX PLC: An Overview
RELX PLC, formerly known as Reed Elsevier, is a British multinational information and analytics company. The company operates in four main segments: Scientific, Technical & Medical; Risk & Business Analytics; Legal; and Exhibitions. RELX PLC is listed on the London Stock Exchange and is a constituent of the FTSE 100 Index.

### Business Segments
The company's business segments are diverse and cater to various industries. The Scientific, Technical & Medical segment provides information and analytics to the scientific, technical, and medical communities. The Risk & Business Analytics segment provides data and analytics to the financial and business communities. The Legal segment provides information and analytics to the legal community. The Exhibitions segment organizes trade exhibitions and conferences.
