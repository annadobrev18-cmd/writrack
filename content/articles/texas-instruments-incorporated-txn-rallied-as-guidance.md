## Fundamentals of Texas Instruments Incorporated (TXN)
Texas Instruments Incorporated (TXN) is a leading American technology company that designs, manufactures, and supplies semiconductors and other electronic components. The company's products are used in a wide range of applications, including industrial, automotive, personal electronics, and enterprise systems. TXN is known for its analog and embedded processing technologies, which enable its customers to create innovative products that are more efficient, reliable, and connected.

### Business Segments
TXN operates through two main business segments: Analog and Embedded Processing. The Analog segment produces high-performance analog products, including power management, signal chain, and high-volume analog and logic products. The Embedded Processing segment provides microcontrollers, processors, and connectivity products.
