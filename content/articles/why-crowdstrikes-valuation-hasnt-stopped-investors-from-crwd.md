## CrowdStrike's Valuation Conundrum
CrowdStrike's valuation has been a topic of discussion among investors and analysts, with some questioning whether the stock is overvalued. Despite these concerns, investors continue to buy CRWD stock, driving its price higher. To understand this phenomenon, it's essential to delve into CrowdStrike's financials, historical context, and market impact.

### Historical Context
CrowdStrike was founded in 2011 by George Kurtz, Dmitri Alperovitch, and Gregg Marston. The company's early success was fueled by its innovative approach to cybersecurity, which focused on endpoint protection and threat intelligence. CrowdStrike's Falcon platform uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to detect and prevent cyber threats in real-time. This unique approach has resonated with customers, and the company has experienced rapid growth since its inception.
