## Market Impact
The current volatility in the automotive sector presents an opportunity for options traders to capitalize on the uncertainty. By selling options on auto stocks, traders can generate premium income while managing their risk exposure.

### Options Trading Strategy
A potential strategy for options traders is to sell cash-secured puts on auto stocks. This involves selling put options on a stock that the trader is willing to own, with the intention of buying the stock at a lower price if the option is exercised.

#### Example
Suppose an options trader sells a cash-secured put on General Motors (GM) with a strike price of $50. If the stock price falls below $50, the trader will be obligated to buy the stock at $50. However, if the stock price remains above $50, the trader will retain the premium income from the sale of the put option.
