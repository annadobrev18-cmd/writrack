### Historical Context of Safehold Stock

To understand the significance of this rating, it's essential to examine the historical context of Safehold stock. Safehold Inc. is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that focuses on acquiring, owning, and leasing land beneath commercial and residential properties. The company's unique business model involves providing ground leases to property owners, which can offer a stable source of income.

Over the past few years, Safehold has demonstrated impressive growth, with its stock price increasing significantly. This growth can be attributed to the company's strategic acquisitions, strong financial performance, and the increasing demand for ground leases.
