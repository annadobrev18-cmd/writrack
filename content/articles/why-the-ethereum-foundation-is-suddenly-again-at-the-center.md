### Historical Context: Ethereum's Rise to Prominence

To understand the Ethereum Foundation's role in the culture war, it's essential to examine the history of Ethereum. Launched in 2015, Ethereum was created by Vitalik Buterin, a young programmer who sought to build a decentralized platform for creating smart contracts. The project quickly gained traction, and by 2017, Ethereum had become one of the largest cryptocurrencies by market capitalization.

#### Key Milestones in Ethereum's History

* 2015: Ethereum is launched, with the goal of creating a decentralized platform for smart contracts
* 2017: Ethereum becomes one of the largest cryptocurrencies by market capitalization
* 2020: Ethereum transitions from a proof-of-work (PoW) to a proof-of-stake (PoS) consensus algorithm
