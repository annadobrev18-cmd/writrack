### Pre-IPO Share Transfers
Pre-IPO share transfers refer to the practice of transferring ownership of shares in a company before it goes public through an initial public offering (IPO). In the context of AI tokens, pre-IPO share transfers involve the transfer of tokens that represent ownership in an AI project before it is listed on a public exchange. These transfers are often used by early investors to realize gains on their investments or by project developers to raise additional capital.
