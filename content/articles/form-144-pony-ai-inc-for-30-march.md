### What is Form 144?
Form 144 is a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) that notifies the public of an intended sale of securities by an insider. This can include executives, directors, or significant shareholders. The form provides information about the number of shares to be sold, the price range, and the intended use of the proceeds.
