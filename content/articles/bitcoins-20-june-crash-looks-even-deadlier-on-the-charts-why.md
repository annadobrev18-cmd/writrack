Another factor that contributed to the crash was market sentiment. The cryptocurrency market has been known for its volatility, and the price of Bitcoin is often influenced by sentiment rather than fundamentals. In June 2026, the sentiment turned bearish, with many investors becoming risk-averse and selling their holdings.
