### Historical Context: Zimbabwe's Mining Sector
Zimbabwe's mining sector has a long and complex history, dating back to the early 20th century. The country was once one of the largest gold producers in the world, with the sector contributing significantly to the country's GDP. However, the sector's growth was hindered by the country's political instability, corruption, and economic mismanagement. The introduction of the Indigenisation and Economic Empowerment Act in 2008, which required foreign-owned companies to cede 51% ownership to local investors, further discouraged foreign investment in the sector.
