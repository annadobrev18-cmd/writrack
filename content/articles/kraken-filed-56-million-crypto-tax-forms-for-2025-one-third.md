### Historical Context: The Rise of Cryptocurrency Taxation
In recent years, governments worldwide have begun to take a closer look at the cryptocurrency space, with a particular focus on taxation. The introduction of the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act in 2017 marked a significant turning point, as it classified cryptocurrencies as property for tax purposes. This classification meant that gains from cryptocurrency transactions would be subject to capital gains tax.
