The global markets have witnessed a significant rebound in recent days, with both Bitcoin and stocks erasing major losses. This recovery has been largely attributed to Iran's decision to signal cooperation on a key shipping route, easing tensions and restoring investor confidence.

As a critical passage for international trade, any disruptions to this route can have far-reaching consequences for the global economy. In recent years, tensions have escalated, leading to increased uncertainty and volatility in the markets.
