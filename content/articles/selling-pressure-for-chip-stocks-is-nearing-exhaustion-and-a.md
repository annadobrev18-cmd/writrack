## The Perfect Storm: Understanding the Selling Pressure in Chip Stocks
The semiconductor industry has been under siege in recent months, with stocks experiencing a significant decline in value. The selling pressure has been intense, with market sentiment turning decidedly bearish. However, as the old adage goes, 'the darkest hour is just before dawn.' Charts are beginning to show signs that the selling pressure is nearing exhaustion, and a turnaround could be on the horizon.

### Historical Context: The Rise and Fall of Semiconductor Stocks
To understand the current state of the semiconductor industry, it's essential to look at the historical context. The sector experienced a remarkable run-up in 2020 and 2021, driven by the pandemic-induced demand for electronic devices and the growing need for advanced technologies like 5G and artificial intelligence. However, as the global economy began to slow down, and supply chain issues persisted, the sector's momentum began to wane.
