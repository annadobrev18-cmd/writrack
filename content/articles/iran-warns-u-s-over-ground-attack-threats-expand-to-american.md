#### Key Events in Iran-US Relations
- 1979: The Iranian Revolution leads to the overthrow of the US-backed Shah, resulting in a significant deterioration of relations between the two countries.
- 1980: The US imposes economic sanctions on Iran, which have been periodically tightened and loosened over the years.
- 2015: The Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA), also known as the Iran nuclear deal, is signed, providing Iran with relief from sanctions in exchange for limits on its nuclear program.
- 2018: The US withdraws from the JCPOA, reimposing sanctions on Iran and leading to a significant escalation of tensions.
