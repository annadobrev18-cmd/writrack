## Airbnb's Bond Sale: A Strategic Move
Airbnb, the online marketplace for short-term lodging, has sold $2.5 billion of bonds as its convertible bonds come due. This move is seen as a strategic decision to manage its debt and strengthen its financial position. The sale of bonds is a common practice among companies to raise capital, and in this case, Airbnb is using it to address its convertible bonds that are set to mature.

The company's revenue has been increasing steadily, with a growth rate of 30% in 2022 compared to the previous year.
