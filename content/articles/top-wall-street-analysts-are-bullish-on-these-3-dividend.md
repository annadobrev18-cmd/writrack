## Valuation and Risk Factors

When evaluating the valuation of Chord Energy and EOG Resources, it is essential to consider the risk factors associated with investing in the energy sector. One of the primary risks is the volatility of oil and natural gas prices, which can significantly impact the revenue and profitability of energy companies.

### Sensitivity to Commodity Prices

The following table illustrates the sensitivity of Chord Energy and EOG Resources to changes in oil and natural gas prices:

| Company | Oil Price Sensitivity | Natural Gas Price Sensitivity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Chord Energy | 10% increase in oil price = 15% increase in revenue | 10% increase in natural gas price = 5% increase in revenue |
