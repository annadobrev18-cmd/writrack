The aviation and data center markets are highly competitive, and FTAI faces intense competition from established players. This competition could lead to pricing pressure and reduced market share.

#### Economic Risks
FTAI's revenue is closely tied to the overall economy, and a downturn could significantly impact the company's financial performance. For example, a recession could lead to reduced air travel and cargo transportation, resulting in lower revenue for FTAI.

### Competitive Landscape
The competitive landscape for FTAI is complex and evolving. The company operates in multiple segments, including aviation, data centers, and aerospace. Each segment has its unique competitive dynamics, and FTAI must navigate these complexities to maintain its market position.
