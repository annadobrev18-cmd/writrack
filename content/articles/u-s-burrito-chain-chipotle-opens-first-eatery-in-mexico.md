According to a report by Euromonitor International, the Mexican fast food market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 4.5% from 2020 to 2025.

Mexico's demographic profile is characterized by a large and growing youth population, with over 50% of the population under the age of 30. This demographic is more likely to be drawn to fast-casual dining options, presenting a significant opportunity for Chipotle to tap into this market.
