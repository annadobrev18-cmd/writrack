## Global Interest Rates and Crypto Market Dynamics
The upcoming week is poised to be significant for the cryptocurrency market, with several key factors set to influence its trajectory. Among these, global interest rates stand out as a critical determinant of market sentiment. The decisions made by central banks regarding interest rates have a profound impact on the overall financial landscape, affecting the attractiveness of riskier assets like cryptocurrencies.

### Historical Context of Interest Rates and Crypto
Historically, changes in interest rates have had a notable impact on the cryptocurrency market. When interest rates are low, investors often seek higher returns in riskier assets, which can include cryptocurrencies. Conversely, when interest rates rise, the appeal of riskier investments may diminish as safer, interest-bearing assets become more attractive. The current global economic situation, with its mix of inflation concerns and slowing growth, makes the upcoming interest rate decisions particularly crucial.
