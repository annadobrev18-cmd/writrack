#### Reasons Behind Insider Selling
There are several reasons why insiders may sell their shares. Some of the common reasons include:
* Personal financial needs: Insiders may need to sell their shares to meet personal financial obligations, such as paying off debts or funding their children's education.
* Diversification: Insiders may sell their shares to diversify their investment portfolio and reduce their exposure to the company's stock.
* Tax planning: Insiders may sell their shares to realize capital gains and offset losses from other investments.
