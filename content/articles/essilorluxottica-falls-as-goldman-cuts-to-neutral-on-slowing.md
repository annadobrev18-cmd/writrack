### Historical Context

To understand the significance of this development, it's essential to look at the historical context. EssilorLuxottica has been at the forefront of innovation in the eyewear industry, with a strong track record of investing in research and development. The company's decision to venture into AI-powered glasses was seen as a strategic move to stay ahead of the curve and capitalize on the growing demand for smart wearable devices.

However, the AI glasses market has been slower to take off than expected. Despite the hype surrounding the technology, consumers have been hesitant to adopt AI-powered glasses, citing concerns over privacy, cost, and functionality. As a result, EssilorLuxottica's sales in this segment have been underwhelming, leading to a re-evaluation of the company's growth prospects.
