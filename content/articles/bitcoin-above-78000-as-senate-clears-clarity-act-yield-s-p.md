## Bitcoin's Recent Rally
The price of Bitcoin has surged past $78,000, marking a significant milestone for the cryptocurrency. This rally comes on the heels of the Senate clearing the Clarity Act yield hurdle, a move that is expected to have far-reaching implications for the cryptocurrency market.

### Historical Context
To understand the significance of this development, it's essential to examine the historical context. The Clarity Act, which was introduced to provide clarity on the tax treatment of cryptocurrency transactions, has been a major point of contention in the industry. The yield hurdle, which refers to the minimum yield required for a bond or other investment to be considered a qualified investment, has been a significant obstacle for cryptocurrency investors.

#### Impact on Bitcoin Price
The clearing of the yield hurdle is expected to have a positive impact on the price of Bitcoin. With the uncertainty surrounding the tax treatment of cryptocurrency transactions removed, investors are likely to be more confident in investing in the asset class. This increased demand is expected to drive up the price of Bitcoin, making it an attractive investment opportunity.
