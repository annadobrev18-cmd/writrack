## Fundamental Analysis of Ameren Corporation (AEE)
Ameren Corporation, a public utility holding company, has been making headlines with its recent price target increase by Wells Fargo to $113, accompanied by an overweight rating. This move signifies the growing confidence of analysts in the company's potential for future growth. To understand the reasoning behind this upgrade, it's essential to delve into the fundamental aspects of Ameren Corporation.

### Business Overview
Ameren Corporation operates through its subsidiaries to provide utilities services, including electricity and natural gas, to a wide range of customers across the United States. The company's operations are divided into four main segments: Ameren Missouri, Ameren Illinois Electric Distribution, Ameren Illinois Natural Gas, and Ameren Transmission. This diversified approach allows Ameren to maintain a stable revenue stream and navigate the complexities of the energy market effectively.
