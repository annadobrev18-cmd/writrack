Drift is a decentralized trading platform that allows users to trade cryptocurrency assets with leverage. The platform uses a unique liquidity pool model, which allows users to provide liquidity and earn interest on their assets. Drift has gained popularity among traders due to its high leverage ratios and low fees.

### Risk Factors
The Drift platform is not without risk, and investors should be aware of the following factors:
* **Smart contract risk**: The Drift platform relies on smart contracts to facilitate transactions. However, smart contracts can be vulnerable to bugs and exploits, which could result in financial losses for users.
* **Liquidity risk**: The Drift platform's liquidity pool model relies on users providing liquidity to facilitate trading. However, if users withdraw their liquidity, the platform may experience liquidity shortages, which could result in trading disruptions.
