DHL's success can be attributed to its diversified business model, which encompasses a wide range of services including express delivery, freight forwarding, supply chain management, and e-commerce logistics. The company's global network spans over 220 countries and territories, providing it with a unique competitive advantage. DHL's commitment to innovation and digitalization has also enabled it to stay ahead of the curve, with investments in artificial intelligence, robotics, and data analytics.
