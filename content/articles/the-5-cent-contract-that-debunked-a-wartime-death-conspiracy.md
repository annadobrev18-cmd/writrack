### The Role of Blockchain
Blockchain technology has emerged as a pivotal tool in verifying historical documents and events. The application of blockchain in this context not only ensures the integrity of historical documents but also provides a means to debunk conspiracy theories by offering irrefutable evidence.

#### Blockchain and Historical Documents
The use of blockchain in conjunction with historical documents such as the 5-cent contract offers several benefits:
- **Immutable Record**: Once a document is recorded on the blockchain, it cannot be altered, ensuring that historical records remain intact and tamper-proof.
- **Transparency**: Blockchain technology allows for the transparent storage and sharing of historical documents, making them accessible to a wide audience and facilitating research and verification.
- **Security**: The decentralized nature of blockchain ensures that historical records are not dependent on a single entity or location, reducing the risk of loss or destruction.
