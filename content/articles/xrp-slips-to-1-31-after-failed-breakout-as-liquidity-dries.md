### Failed Breakout: A Technical Analysis
The failed breakout of XRP can be attributed to the lack of momentum in the price movement. Despite the initial surge, the price was unable to sustain the upward trend, resulting in a downward correction. This can be seen in the technical indicators, such as the Relative Strength Index (RSI) and the Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD). The RSI, which measures the magnitude of recent price changes, had reached overbought levels, indicating a potential reversal. Similarly, the MACD, which measures the relationship between two moving averages, had also signaled a bearish crossover, confirming the downward trend.
