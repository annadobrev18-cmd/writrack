### Historical Context

The US-Iran relationship has been strained for decades, with the 1979 Iranian Revolution marking a significant turning point. The US has imposed numerous sanctions on Iran, including the 2015 Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA), which was aimed at curbing Iran's nuclear program. However, the Trump administration's decision to withdraw from the JCPOA in 2018 and impose new sanctions has led to a significant escalation of tensions.

### Economic Impact

The potential war with Iran could have severe consequences for the global economy and US market stability. The most significant impact would be on the oil market, as Iran is a major oil producer and exporter. A disruption to oil supplies could lead to a significant increase in oil prices, which would have a ripple effect on the global economy.
