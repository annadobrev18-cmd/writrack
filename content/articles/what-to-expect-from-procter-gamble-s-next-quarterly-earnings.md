### Segment Performance and Outlook

P&G's business is organized into six reportable segments: Beauty, Grooming, Health Care, Fabric & Home Care, Baby, Feminine & Family Care, and Family Care. Each segment has its unique characteristics, trends, and challenges. The Beauty segment, for example, has been a significant contributor to the company's growth, driven by the increasing demand for premium skincare and haircare products.

#### Beauty Segment Analysis

The Beauty segment has been a bright spot for P&G, with sales growth driven by the company's premium brands, such as SK-II and Olay. The segment has also benefited from the increasing popularity of e-commerce, with online sales growing at a faster rate than offline sales.
