## CrowdStrike's Position in the Market
CrowdStrike is a leading provider of cloud-delivered endpoint protection, offering a comprehensive suite of security solutions to protect against advanced cyber threats. The company's Falcon platform utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to detect and prevent attacks in real-time, making it a key player in the rapidly evolving cybersecurity landscape.

### The Rise of AI-Powered Cybersecurity
The increasing use of AI and ML in cybersecurity has created a new paradigm for threat detection and prevention. As AI-powered systems become more prevalent, the potential for AI-driven cyber attacks also grows. This has led to a heightened focus on AI cybersecurity risks, with companies like CrowdStrike at the forefront of developing solutions to mitigate these threats.
