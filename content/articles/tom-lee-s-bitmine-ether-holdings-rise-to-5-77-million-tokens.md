### Historical Context of Ethereum
Ethereum was launched in 2015 by Vitalik Buterin, with the primary goal of creating a decentralized platform for building smart contracts and decentralized applications. Over the years, Ethereum has undergone significant upgrades, including the transition from proof-of-work (PoW) to proof-of-stake (PoS) consensus algorithm. This transition, known as the Merge, has improved the network's scalability, security, and energy efficiency.
