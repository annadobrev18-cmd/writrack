## Netflix's Current State
Netflix, the pioneer in the streaming industry, is set to release its earnings report on Thursday. The current sentiment around the stock is souring, with Wall Street looking for a catalyst to revive the company's momentum. The streaming giant has been facing increasing competition from new entrants in the market, such as Disney+, HBO Max, and Apple TV+, which has led to a decline in its stock price.

### Historical Context
To understand the current state of Netflix, it's essential to look at its historical performance. The company has been a darling of Wall Street, with its stock price increasing rapidly over the years. However, the recent decline in subscriber growth and increasing competition have raised concerns among investors. In 2020, Netflix's stock price reached an all-time high of $575.37, but it has since declined by over 40%.

## Expert Opinions
Several experts have weighed in on Netflix's upcoming earnings report. Some believe that the company needs to provide a clear roadmap for its future growth, including its plans for expanding its content offerings and improving its user experience. ### Bearish View

## What Netflix Can Say to Regain Its Mojo
To regain its momentum, Netflix needs to provide a clear roadmap for its future growth.
