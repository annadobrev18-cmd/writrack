AeroVironment, a leading manufacturer of unmanned aircraft systems (UAS), recently posted solid earnings, sending its stock sharply higher. ### Historical Context: AeroVironment's Journey to Success
AeroVironment was founded in 1971 by Paul MacCready, a renowned aerodynamicist and engineer. Initially, the company focused on developing human-powered aircraft, but it later shifted its attention to UAS. Over the years, AeroVironment has become a pioneer in the drone industry, providing innovative solutions for military, commercial, and recreational applications. The company's product portfolio includes the Raven, Puma, and Switchblade drones, which have gained widespread acceptance among customers.
