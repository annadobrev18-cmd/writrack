## Heron Therapeutics, Inc. Q1 2026 Earnings Call Summary
Heron Therapeutics, Inc. recently announced its Q1 2026 earnings, providing insight into the company's performance and future prospects. As a seasoned senior financial analyst, this report will delve into the key highlights of the earnings call, examining the company's financial metrics, product pipeline, and competitive landscape.

### Financial Metrics
The company reported a net loss of $23.1 million, or $0.43 per share, compared to a net loss of $30.4 million, or $0.57 per share, in the same period last year. Total revenues for the quarter were $43.8 million, a 25% increase from $35.1 million in Q1 2025. The increase in revenue was primarily driven by sales of the company's lead product, Cinvanti, which is used to prevent chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting.

Heron Therapeutics has a diverse product pipeline, with several products in various stages of development. The company's lead product, Cinvanti, has been a significant contributor to its revenue growth.
