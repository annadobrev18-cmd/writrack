### The Emergence of a New ETF
Adding to the potential volatility is the recent launch of the Roundhill T-REX 2X Long DRAM Daily Target ETF (RAM). This ETF is a 2x levered version of the popular DRAM ETF, which tracks the performance of companies involved in the production of dynamic random-access memory (DRAM). The RAM ETF aims to provide investors with twice the daily return of the underlying DRAM index, making it a potentially attractive option for those seeking to capitalize on the volatility surrounding Micron's earnings report.

#### Key Features of the RAM ETF
The RAM ETF has several key features that make it an interesting option for investors:
* **2x Leverage**: The ETF provides twice the daily return of the underlying DRAM index, allowing investors to potentially amplify their gains.
* **Daily Target**: The ETF is designed to provide a daily target return, rather than a long-term investment strategy, making it more suitable for active traders.
* **DRAM Focus**: The ETF tracks the performance of companies involved in the production of DRAM, providing investors with exposure to a specific segment of the technology sector.
