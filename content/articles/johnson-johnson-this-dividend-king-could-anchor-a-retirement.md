## Johnson & Johnson: A Dividend King with a Rich History
Johnson & Johnson, one of the world's largest healthcare companies, has been a staple in the pharmaceutical industry for over 135 years. Founded in 1886 by Robert Wood Johnson I, Johnson & Johnson has evolved into a multinational conglomerate with a diverse portfolio of medical devices, pharmaceuticals, and consumer products. The company's commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has earned it a reputation as a reliable and stable investment opportunity.

### Historical Context: A Legacy of Dividend Payments
Johnson & Johnson has a long history of paying consistent dividends to its shareholders. The company has increased its dividend payout for 59 consecutive years, earning it the prestigious title of Dividend King. This impressive track record is a testament to Johnson & Johnson's financial strength, stability, and commitment to rewarding its shareholders. The company's dividend yield currently stands at around 2.7%, providing a relatively attractive income stream for investors seeking stable returns.
