## The Rise of Space Stocks
The space industry has been gaining significant attention in recent years, with companies like SpaceX, Blue Origin, and Virgin Galactic leading the charge. As these companies continue to push the boundaries of space exploration and technology, investors are taking notice. One of the most notable trends in the space stock market is the surge in options volume, particularly in stocks related to SpaceX.

### Options Volume Boom
According to recent data, options volume in space stocks has boomed, with investors looking for any way to get long SpaceX. This trend is driven by the lack of direct investment opportunities in SpaceX, which is a private company. As a result, investors are turning to options trading as a way to gain exposure to the company's potential growth. Danny Kirsch, head of options trading at Piper Sandler, notes that there's a ton of short-dated call buying in these names as a way to get long SpaceX.
