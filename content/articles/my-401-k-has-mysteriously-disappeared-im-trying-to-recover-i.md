### Historical Context: The Evolution of 401(k) Plans
To understand the significance of this event, it's essential to delve into the history of 401(k) plans. Introduced in 1978, these plans were designed to provide employees with a tax-advantaged way to save for retirement. Over the years, 401(k) plans have become a cornerstone of retirement planning in the United States, with millions of Americans relying on them for their future financial security.

### Recovery Efforts: Strategies for Individuals
For individuals facing the disappearance of their 401(k), experts recommend a multi-step approach to recovery. This includes contacting the plan administrator, filing a complaint with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), and seeking professional advice from a financial advisor.

#### Preventive Measures: Protecting Your 401(k)
To protect their 401(k) plans, individuals can take several preventive measures. These include regularly monitoring account activity, keeping contact information up to date, and understanding the fees associated with their plan.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **What are the first steps to take if my 401(k) disappears?**
   - Contact your plan administrator immediately to report the issue and request assistance in recovering your account.
   - File a complaint with the SEC to initiate an investigation.
   - Consult with a financial advisor to explore options for recovery and to discuss strategies for protecting your remaining assets.

2. **How can I protect my 401(k) from potential fraud or mismanagement?**
   - Regularly review your account statements for any suspicious activity.
   - Ensure your contact information is current to receive important notifications from your plan administrator.
   - Educate yourself on the fees associated with your plan and understand how they impact your savings.

3. **What regulatory changes could help prevent the disappearance of 401(k) plans in the future?**
   - Strengthening cybersecurity requirements for financial institutions.
   - Implementing more frequent and rigorous audits of 401(k) plans.
   - Enhancing consumer protection laws to provide clearer recourse for individuals affected by the disappearance of their 401(k).
