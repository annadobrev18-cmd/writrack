#### Product 1: Grindr Premium
The first product is an upgraded version of Grindr's existing premium service. Grindr Premium offers additional features such as unlimited profiles, no ads, and the ability to view who has viewed your profile. The new version of Grindr Premium will include even more features, such as the ability to view profiles anonymously and the option to hide your online status. This is likely to attract more users to the premium service, increasing Grindr's revenue.

#### Product 2: Grindr Live
