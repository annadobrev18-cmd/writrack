### Background on KAST

KAST was founded with the goal of providing a fast, secure, and reliable payments platform that utilizes stablecoins. Stablecoins are a type of cryptocurrency that is pegged to the value of a traditional currency, such as the US dollar. This allows for the benefits of cryptocurrency, including fast transaction times and low fees, while minimizing the volatility associated with traditional cryptocurrencies.
