### Fed Implications and Sector Rotations
The Federal Reserve's monetary policy decisions may also impact Tesla's stock price. The Fed's decision to raise or lower interest rates can influence the overall stock market, and sector rotations may occur in response to changes in interest rates.

| Interest Rate | Sector Rotation |
| --- | --- |
| Rising interest rates | Rotation out of growth stocks, such as tech and EV, and into value stocks, such as finance and energy |
| Falling interest rates | Rotation into growth stocks, such as tech and EV, and out of value stocks, such as finance and energy |

As shown in the table above, changes in interest rates can cause sector rotations, which may impact Tesla's stock price. If interest rates rise, investors may rotate out of growth stocks, such as Tesla, and into value stocks.
