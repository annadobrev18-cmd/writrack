### Agentic AI and Its Role in Cybersecurity
Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence that can act autonomously, making decisions without human intervention. In the context of cybersecurity, Agentic AI can be used to detect and respond to threats in real-time, improving the efficiency and effectiveness of security systems. The integration of Agentic AI into cybersecurity solutions is expected to revolutionize the industry, providing advanced threat detection, incident response, and security analytics.

#### Key Benefits of Agentic AI in Cybersecurity
- **Improved Threat Detection**: Agentic AI can analyze vast amounts of data, identifying patterns and anomalies that may indicate a potential threat.
- **Enhanced Incident Response**: Agentic AI can respond to threats in real-time, minimizing the impact of a cyber attack.
- **Advanced Security Analytics**: Agentic AI can provide detailed analytics and insights, helping organizations to better understand their security posture.
