## Alphabet's Bold Move in AI Spending
Alphabet, the parent company of Google, has recently announced a significant surge in its AI spending, a move that is expected to have far-reaching implications for the tech industry, particularly for hyperscaler stocks. The company's decision to increase its investment in AI comes at a time when the industry is witnessing a boom in infrastructure development, leading to sagging free cash flows.

### Historical Context
To understand the significance of Alphabet's move, it is essential to look at the historical context of the company's spending on AI. Over the years, Alphabet has been at the forefront of AI research and development, with a significant portion of its budget allocated to this area. The company's AI initiatives have led to the development of various innovative products and services, including Google Assistant, Google Cloud AI Platform, and Waymo, its autonomous vehicle subsidiary.
