## Resolute Mining's Strategic Move
Resolute Mining's approval of the Doropo Gold Project's Final Investment Decision (FID) marks a significant milestone for the company, as it seeks to expand its presence in the West African gold mining sector. The Doropo project, located in Côte d'Ivoire, is expected to contribute substantially to Resolute's production profile, with estimated annual gold production of 120,000 ounces over a minimum 8-year mine life.

### Historical Context
Resolute Mining has been operating in West Africa for over two decades, with a strong focus on gold production. The company's flagship asset, the Syama Gold Mine in Mali, has been a key contributor to its production profile. However, with the Doropo project, Resolute is diversifying its portfolio and reducing its dependence on a single asset. This strategic move is expected to enhance the company's growth prospects and mitigate potential risks associated with operating in a single jurisdiction.
