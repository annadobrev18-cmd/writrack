### Company Overview
ContextLogic, also known as Wish, is a mobile e-commerce platform that allows users to browse and purchase products from various sellers. The company was founded in 2010 and has since become one of the largest e-commerce platforms in the world, with over 500 million monthly active users. Wish has been known for its affordable prices and wide selection of products, making it a popular destination for budget-conscious consumers.
