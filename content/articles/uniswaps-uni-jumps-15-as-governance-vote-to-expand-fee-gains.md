## Uniswap's Governance Vote and Its Impact on UNI Token
Uniswap's native token, UNI, has seen a significant surge of 15% in recent trading sessions. This upward momentum is largely attributed to the ongoing governance vote regarding the expansion of the fee switch on the Uniswap protocol. The proposal, which aims to redirect a portion of the protocol's fees to UNI token holders, has garnered substantial support from the community, leading to increased confidence and investment in the token.

### Historical Context of Uniswap and UNI Token
Uniswap, a decentralized exchange (DEX) protocol, was launched in 2018 and has since become one of the most widely used DeFi platforms. The introduction of the UNI token in 2020 marked a significant milestone for the protocol, as it enabled community participation in governance decisions. The token's performance has been closely tied to the adoption and development of the Uniswap ecosystem.

This development has sparked interest among investors, leading to the recent price surge of the UNI token.
