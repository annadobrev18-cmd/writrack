### Historical Context: Understanding Form 144 Filings

Form 144 is a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) that indicates an insider's intention to sell securities. This filing is typically made by executives, directors, or significant shareholders of a company. The purpose of Form 144 is to provide transparency into insider activity, allowing investors to make informed decisions about their investments.

To better understand the significance of Associated Banc-Corp's Form 144 filing, let's examine the company's historical insider activity. Over the past year, Associated Banc-Corp has seen a mix of insider buying and selling activity.
