## UK Financial Regulator's Historic Decision
The UK financial regulator has made a groundbreaking move by allowing mutual funds to have up to 10% exposure to crypto Exchange-Traded Notes (ETNs). This decision marks a significant shift in the regulator's stance on cryptocurrency investments and is expected to have far-reaching implications for the financial industry.

### Historical Context
The UK financial regulator has been cautious about cryptocurrency investments in the past, citing concerns about market volatility and lack of regulatory oversight. However, with the growing popularity of cryptocurrencies and the increasing demand for crypto-based investment products, the regulator has been under pressure to revisit its stance. The decision to allow mutual funds to invest in crypto ETNs is a significant departure from the regulator's previous stance and reflects a growing recognition of the potential benefits of cryptocurrency investments.
