#### Diversification
Diversification involves spreading investments across different asset classes, sectors, and geographies to reduce exposure to any one particular market or sector. By diversifying his investments, Ferdinand has been able to reduce the risk of significant losses during periods of market volatility.

#### Hedging
Hedging involves taking positions in securities that are expected to perform well during periods of market volatility. This can include investing in bonds, commodities, or other assets that are negatively correlated with the overall market. By hedging his investments, Ferdinand has been able to reduce the risk of significant losses during periods of market downturn.

#### Active Portfolio Management
Active portfolio management involves continuously monitoring and adjusting the portfolio to ensure that it remains aligned with the investor's objectives and risk tolerance. By actively managing his portfolio, Ferdinand has been able to respond quickly to changes in market conditions, reducing the risk of significant losses.
