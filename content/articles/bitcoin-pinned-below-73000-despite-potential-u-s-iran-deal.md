## Risk Factors: Regulatory Environment and Market Volatility
While Bitcoin's price action is influenced by a range of factors, the regulatory environment and market volatility are two key risk factors that investors should be aware of. Regulatory developments, such as the introduction of stricter anti-money laundering laws, could negatively impact Bitcoin's price. Similarly, market volatility, driven by factors such as economic uncertainty and geopolitical tensions, could also affect the cryptocurrency's value.
