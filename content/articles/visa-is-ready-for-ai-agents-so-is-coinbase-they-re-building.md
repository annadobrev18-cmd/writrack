## The Emergence of AI Agents in Finance
The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) agents in the financial sector is transforming the way transactions are processed, and companies like Visa and Coinbase are at the forefront of this revolution. As the use of AI agents becomes more prevalent, it is likely to lead to the creation of new, decentralized systems that will change the fabric of the internet as we know it.

### Visa's Approach to AI Agents
Visa, the global payments giant, has been investing heavily in AI research and development. The company's focus is on using AI to improve the efficiency and security of its payment processing systems. By leveraging machine learning algorithms, Visa aims to reduce the risk of fraud and enhance the overall user experience. However, Visa's approach to AI agents is more focused on augmenting its existing infrastructure rather than building a new, decentralized internet.

## Risk Factors
The integration of AI agents in the financial sector is not without risks. One of the main concerns is the potential for job displacement, as AI agents automate tasks that were previously performed by humans. Additionally, there is a risk of bias in AI decision-making, which could lead to unfair outcomes for certain groups of people.
