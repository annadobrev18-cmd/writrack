- **Regulatory Environment**: News and developments related to cryptocurrency regulation can significantly impact XRP's price, as clarity and favorable regulations can boost investor confidence.
- **Technological Advancements**: Upgrades and improvements to the XRP Ledger, such as the implementation of new features and protocols, can enhance the network's efficiency and attractiveness to users and investors.
