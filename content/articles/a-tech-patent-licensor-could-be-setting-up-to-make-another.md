## InterDigital's Business Model and Financial Performance
InterDigital is a unique company that collects royalties from device makers without producing any physical products. This business model allows the company to generate revenue without incurring the costs associated with manufacturing and distribution. As a result, InterDigital's financial performance is closely tied to the demand for its patented technologies.

### Revenue Streams
InterDigital's revenue streams come from licensing its patents to device makers. The company has a portfolio of over 32,000 patents and applications, covering a range of technologies including 5G, LTE, and other wireless communication standards. The royalties generated from these licenses are a significant source of revenue for the company.
