### Historical Context of EPA Air Pollution Policies

To understand the significance of Szabo's resignation, it is essential to examine the historical context of EPA air pollution policies. The EPA has been at the forefront of environmental regulation in the United States, implementing policies such as the Clean Air Act and the Clean Power Plan. These policies have had a profound impact on the economy, influencing the growth and development of various industries, including energy, manufacturing, and transportation.

#### Key Milestones in EPA Air Pollution Policies

* 1970: The Clean Air Act is passed, establishing the EPA and granting it the authority to regulate air pollution.
* 2015: The Clean Power Plan is introduced, aiming to reduce carbon emissions from power plants by 32% by 2030.
* 2020: The EPA announces plans to roll back certain aspects of the Clean Power Plan, citing concerns about economic impact.
