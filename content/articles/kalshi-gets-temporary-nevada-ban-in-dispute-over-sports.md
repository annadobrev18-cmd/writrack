#### Historical Context: Sports Betting in the US
The sports betting landscape in the US has undergone significant changes since the Supreme Court's decision in Murphy v. National Collegiate Athletic Association (2018), which effectively allowed states to legalize sports betting. Since then, numerous states have moved to regulate and tax sports betting, creating a patchwork of different laws and regulations across the country. Nevada, with its long history of gaming, has been at the forefront of this shift.
