## NEAR Protocol's Recent Surge: Understanding the Catalysts
The cryptocurrency market has witnessed significant volatility in recent times, with various assets experiencing substantial price swings. Among these, NEAR Protocol (NEAR) has emerged as a notable performer, with its price surging by 6.3% to lead the CoinDesk 20 index higher. ### Historical Context of NEAR Protocol
NEAR Protocol, launched in 2020, is a sharded proof-of-stake blockchain that aims to provide a scalable and secure platform for decentralized applications (dApps). Its native token, NEAR, is used for transaction fees, staking, and governance. Since its inception, NEAR has garnered significant attention due to its innovative approach to scalability and its potential to support a wide range of use cases.
