The cryptocurrency market has been experiencing a significant surge in recent days, with many top coins reaching new highs. To understand the current market situation, it's essential to look at the historical context of XRP's price movement. The recent surge in price can be attributed to the increasing adoption of the Ripple network by banks and financial institutions, as well as the growing demand for cross-border payments.

#### XRP's Price Movement
XRP's price movement over the past year has been marked by significant volatility. The coin reached an all-time high of $3.84 in January 2018 but subsequently crashed to $0.26 in December 2018. Since then, the coin has been trading in a range-bound manner, with occasional breakouts and corrections.

## Market Impact
The failed breakout attempt by XRP has significant implications for the cryptocurrency market. If the coin can break out above $1.40, it could lead to a significant surge in price, potentially reaching $2.00 or higher. On the other hand, if the coin fails to break out, it could lead to a correction, potentially falling to $0.80 or lower.
