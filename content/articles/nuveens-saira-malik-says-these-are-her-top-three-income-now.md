## Risk Factors of Nuveen's Income Ideas
Each of Malik's income ideas comes with its own set of risk factors, which investors need to carefully consider before investing.

### High-Yield Bonds
High-yield bonds are subject to default risk, which is the risk that the issuer will fail to make interest or principal payments. They are also subject to interest rate risk, which is the risk that changes in interest rates will affect their value.

### Preferred Securities
Preferred securities are subject to credit risk, which is the risk that the issuer will fail to make interest or principal payments. They are also subject to liquidity risk, which is the risk that investors will not be able to sell their securities quickly enough or at a fair price.

### Emerging Market Debt
Emerging market debt is subject to currency risk, which is the risk that changes in exchange rates will affect its value. It is also subject to sovereign risk, which is the risk that the government will fail to make interest or principal payments.
