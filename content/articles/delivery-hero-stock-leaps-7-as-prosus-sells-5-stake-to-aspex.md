### Historical Context: Delivery Hero's Rise to Prominence

To grasp the significance of this event, it is essential to delve into Delivery Hero's history. Founded in 2011, Delivery Hero has evolved into one of the world's leading food delivery companies, operating in over 50 countries. The company's success can be attributed to its strategic acquisitions, partnerships, and investments in emerging markets. Delivery Hero's stock has been a favorite among investors, with its market value skyrocketing in recent years.

### Market Impact: Prosus' Sale of Delivery Hero Stake

Prosus, a Dutch technology investor, has been a significant shareholder in Delivery Hero since 2017. The sale of its 5% stake to Aspex, a private equity firm, marks a notable shift in the company's investment strategy. This transaction not only generates a substantial return on investment for Prosus but also demonstrates the growing appeal of food delivery companies to private equity firms.

The market reaction to this news has been overwhelmingly positive, with Delivery Hero's stock price increasing by 7%. This surge can be attributed to the perceived vote of confidence from Aspex, which has invested heavily in the company. Furthermore, the sale of Prosus' stake has reduced the overhang of potential selling pressure, allowing investors to focus on the company's fundamentals.
