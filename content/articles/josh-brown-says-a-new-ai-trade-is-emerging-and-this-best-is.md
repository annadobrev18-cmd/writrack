## The Emergence of a New AI Trade
The world of artificial intelligence (AI) has been making waves in the stock market, with various trades emerging and gaining traction. Recently, Josh Brown, a well-known financial expert, discussed the emergence of a new AI trade that is taking the place of early AI trades, such as chip stocks. This new trade focuses on the customers of AI, rather than the technology itself.

### The Shift from Chip Stocks to AI Customers
The early days of AI trades saw a significant focus on chip stocks, with companies like NVIDIA and AMD leading the charge. However, as the AI landscape continues to evolve, investors are now shifting their attention to the customers of AI. This shift is driven by the growing demand for AI-powered solutions and the increasing adoption of AI across various industries.
