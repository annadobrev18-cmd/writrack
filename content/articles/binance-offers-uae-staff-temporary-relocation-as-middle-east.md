The recent escalation of conflict in the Middle East has sent shockwaves across global markets, with investors and businesses alike scrambling to assess the potential implications. Binance, one of the world's leading cryptocurrency exchanges, has taken a proactive approach by offering its UAE-based staff temporary relocation. This move not only underscores the company's commitment to employee safety but also highlights the growing uncertainty surrounding the region's stability.
