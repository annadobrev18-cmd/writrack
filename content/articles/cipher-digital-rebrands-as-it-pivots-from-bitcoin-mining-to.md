### The Pivot to HPC: Opportunities and Challenges

The decision to pivot to HPC is a bold move, as it requires significant investment in new infrastructure and talent. However, the potential rewards are substantial, given the growing demand for high-performance computing in various industries, including healthcare, finance, and climate modeling.

#### HPC Market Overview

The HPC market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% from 2023 to 2028, driven by the increasing adoption of cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things (IoT). Cipher Digital's entry into this market could provide a much-needed boost to its revenue and profitability.

#### Competitive Landscape

The HPC market is highly competitive, with established players such as Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP). Cipher Digital will need to differentiate itself through innovative solutions and strategic partnerships to gain traction in this market.
