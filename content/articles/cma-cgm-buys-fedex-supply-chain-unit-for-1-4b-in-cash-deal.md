## Fundamentals of the Deal
CMA CGM, a leading global shipping and logistics company, has recently announced the acquisition of FedEx Supply Chain, a subsidiary of FedEx Corporation, for $1.4 billion in cash. This strategic move aims to expand CMA CGM's presence in the logistics market, particularly in the e-commerce and supply chain management sectors.

### Overview of CMA CGM
CMA CGM is a French container transportation and shipping company founded in 1978. The company has grown to become one of the largest shipping lines in the world, with a fleet of over 500 vessels and a presence in more than 160 countries. CMA CGM's services include container shipping, logistics, and supply chain management.

### Overview of FedEx Supply Chain
FedEx Supply Chain, formerly known as GENCO, is a leading third-party logistics provider that offers a range of services, including warehousing, distribution, and transportation management. The company was acquired by FedEx in 2015 and has since operated as a subsidiary, providing logistics solutions to a diverse range of industries.
