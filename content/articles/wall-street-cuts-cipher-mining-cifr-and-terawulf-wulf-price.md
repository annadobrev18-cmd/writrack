## Wall Street's Shift in Sentiment
The recent move by Wall Street to cut price targets for Cipher Mining (CIFR) and TeraWulf (WULF) signals a significant shift in sentiment towards these cryptocurrency mining stocks. ### Historical Context
To understand the implications of this move, it's essential to consider the historical context. Cipher Mining and TeraWulf, like many other cryptocurrency mining companies, saw their stocks soar during the crypto boom of 2021. However, as the market began to correct and regulatory pressures mounted, these stocks have faced significant challenges. The cut in price targets by Wall Street analysts reflects a more cautious approach, taking into account the current market realities and future uncertainties.
