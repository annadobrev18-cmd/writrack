## Qualcomm's Strategic Expansion into Data Center Chips
Qualcomm, a renowned leader in the semiconductor industry, has recently unveiled its data center chips, marking a significant expansion into a new market segment. This move is seen as a strategic attempt to diversify its revenue streams and capitalize on the growing demand for data center infrastructure. The company has also announced a partnership with Meta, a major player in the tech industry, to supply its data center chips.

### Historical Context and Market Trends
The data center chip market has experienced rapid growth in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things (IoT). This trend is expected to continue, with the global data center chip market projected to reach $15.6 billion by 2025, growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 20.3%. Qualcomm's entry into this market is timely, as it seeks to leverage its expertise in designing high-performance, low-power chips for mobile devices and apply it to the data center segment.
