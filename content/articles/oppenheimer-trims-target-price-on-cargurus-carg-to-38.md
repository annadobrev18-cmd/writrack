## CarGurus: An Overview
CarGurus (CARG) is a leading online automotive marketplace, connecting buyers with sellers across the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. Founded in 2006 by Langley Steinert, the company has established itself as a trusted platform for car shoppers, providing a comprehensive suite of tools and resources to facilitate informed purchasing decisions.

### Historical Context
Since its initial public offering (IPO) in 2017, CarGurus has demonstrated impressive growth, with revenues increasing from $198.1 million in 2017 to $741.4 million in 2022. This expansion can be attributed to the company's strategic focus on developing innovative products, enhancing user experience, and expanding its dealer network.
