With its extensive experience in managing risk, enhancing efficiency, and reducing costs for market participants, the DTCC is well-positioned to leverage blockchain technology to further modernize the financial infrastructure.

### Tokenized Securities
Tokenized securities represent a digital version of traditional securities, such as stocks, bonds, and mutual funds. By utilizing blockchain technology, these securities can be issued, traded, and settled in a more efficient and secure manner. The use of tokenized securities has the potential to reduce counterparty risk, increase liquidity, and provide greater access to investment opportunities.

## Valuation and Market Impact
The introduction of tokenized securities into live trading is expected to have a significant impact on the market.
