### Background on Venture Global and Atlantic-SEE
Venture Global is a leading developer of LNG export facilities in the United States, with a focus on providing low-cost, reliable energy solutions to its customers worldwide. Atlantic-SEE, on the other hand, is a significant player in the energy sector, with a diverse portfolio of energy assets and a strong commitment to sustainability.
