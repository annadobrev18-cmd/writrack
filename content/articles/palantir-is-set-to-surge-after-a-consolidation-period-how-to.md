* **Covid-19 Pandemic**: The Covid-19 pandemic accelerated the adoption of digital technologies, benefiting Palantir's business and driving the stock price up to $30.
* **Recent Consolidation**: Palantir's stock price has been rangebound between $20 and $30, as investors await further catalysts for growth.
