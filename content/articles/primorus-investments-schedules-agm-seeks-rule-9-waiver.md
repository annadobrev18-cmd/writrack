### Recent Developments
The announcement of the AGM and the pursuit of a Rule 9 waiver are critical recent developments. The AGM is a crucial event for any publicly traded company, as it provides a platform for shareholders to be updated on the company's performance and future plans. The request for a Rule 9 waiver, however, is more specific and pertains to the UK's Takeover Code. Rule 9 of the Takeover Code requires that if a company acquires an interest in another company which, when taken together with its existing interests, carries 10% or more of the voting rights, it must make a cash offer for the remainder of the company's shares. A waiver of this rule would allow Primorus Investments to avoid making such an offer under certain circumstances, providing it with more flexibility in its investment strategies.
