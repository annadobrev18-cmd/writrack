### Fundamentals of the Market

The US stock market has experienced a significant rally in recent months, driven by the strong performance of the technology sector. The S&P 500 index has reached new highs, and the NASDAQ composite has also shown impressive gains. However, the market's momentum has slowed down in recent days, as investors await the earnings reports from the Big Tech companies.

The earnings reports from Apple, Amazon, Alphabet, and Facebook will be closely watched, as they are expected to provide insights into the health of the technology sector. The market is also anticipating the Federal Reserve's meeting, which could provide clues about the future direction of monetary policy.
