### Background: The Rise and Fall of FTX

FTX, once a leading cryptocurrency derivatives exchange, rose to prominence under the leadership of Sam Bankman-Fried. However, its rapid ascent was followed by an equally dramatic collapse in November 2022, leaving millions of investors with significant losses. The collapse was precipitated by a liquidity crisis, which was exacerbated by the exchange's risky business practices and alleged misuse of customer funds.

### Allegations Against Sam Bankman-Fried

Sam Bankman-Fried faces multiple allegations, including fraud and making false statements to investors. The U.S. Senate's unanimous opposition to clemency for Bankman-Fried reflects the gravity of these allegations and the public's demand for accountability. This stance is also indicative of the broader regulatory environment, where there is a growing push for stricter oversight of cryptocurrency exchanges and related entities.
