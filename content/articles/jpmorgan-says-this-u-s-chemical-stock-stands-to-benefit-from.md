The recent escalation of tensions between the US and Iran has sent shockwaves throughout the global economy, with the chemical industry being one of the most affected sectors. As the conflict continues to unfold, JPMorgan has identified Eastman Chemical as a potential beneficiary of the supply disruption caused by the war.

### Historical Context: Chemical Stocks and Geopolitical Events
The chemical industry has historically been sensitive to geopolitical events, particularly those affecting the Middle East. The region is home to a significant portion of the world's oil and natural gas reserves, which are essential feedstocks for the production of various chemicals. Any disruption to the supply chain can have far-reaching consequences for the industry.
