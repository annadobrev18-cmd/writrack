### Historical Context: Granite Point Mortgage Trust (GPMT)
GPMT is a mortgage REIT that focuses on investing in senior floating-rate commercial mortgage loans. The company's primary objective is to generate income for distribution to its stockholders through the origination and acquisition of these loans. Over the years, GPMT has navigated the complexities of the mortgage market, adapting to changes in interest rates, regulatory environments, and economic conditions. However, like many of its peers, GPMT faces challenges related to interest rate fluctuations, loan defaults, and the competitive landscape of the mortgage industry.
