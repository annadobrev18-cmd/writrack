## Stock Market Overview
The current stock market landscape is characterized by a decline in major indexes, including the Dow, S&P 500, and Nasdaq, primarily due to the surge in bond yields. This increase in bond yields has put significant pressure on the tech sector, leading to a retreat in tech stocks. The Dow Jones Industrial Average, S&P 500, and Nasdaq Composite have all experienced a slide, with the tech-heavy Nasdaq being the most affected.

### Impact of Rising Bond Yields
Rising bond yields have a profound impact on the stock market, particularly on the tech sector. When bond yields increase, it becomes more expensive for companies to borrow money, which can lead to a decrease in investment and spending. This, in turn, can cause a decline in stock prices. Additionally, higher bond yields make bonds a more attractive investment option compared to stocks, leading to a shift in investor sentiment.
