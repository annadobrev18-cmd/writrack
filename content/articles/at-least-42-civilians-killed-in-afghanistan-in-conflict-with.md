The conflict between Afghanistan and Pakistan is not a new phenomenon. The two countries have a long and complex history of tensions, with disputes over border territories and ideological differences contributing to the ongoing violence. However, the recent escalation of hostilities has sparked concerns among investors, who are increasingly wary of the potential for further destabilization in the region.
