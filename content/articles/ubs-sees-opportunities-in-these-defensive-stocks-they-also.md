## Fundamentals of Defensive Stocks
Defensive stocks are known for their ability to withstand economic downturns and provide stable returns to investors. These stocks typically belong to industries that are less affected by economic fluctuations, such as consumer staples, healthcare, and utilities. The current market scenario, with its uncertainties and potential for recession, makes defensive stocks an attractive option for investors seeking to minimize risk.

### Characteristics of Defensive Stocks
Defensive stocks usually have the following characteristics:
- Low volatility: They tend to have lower beta values, indicating that their prices are less sensitive to market fluctuations.
- Stable earnings: They often have consistent earnings and dividend payments, providing a regular income stream to investors.
- Inelastic demand: The demand for their products or services remains relatively stable, even during economic downturns.
