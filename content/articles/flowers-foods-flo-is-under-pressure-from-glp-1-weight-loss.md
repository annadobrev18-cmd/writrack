## Fundamentals of Flowers Foods
Flowers Foods, Inc. (FLO) is a leading producer and marketer of packaged bakery foods in the United States. The company was founded in 1919 and is headquartered in Thomasville, Georgia. Flowers Foods operates through two segments: Direct-Store-Delivery (DSD) and Warehouse Delivery. The DSD segment distributes fresh bread, buns, and rolls directly to retail stores, while the Warehouse Delivery segment delivers frozen bread, buns, and rolls to retail stores and foodservice providers.

### Historical Performance
Historically, Flowers Foods has demonstrated a strong track record of financial performance, with steady revenue growth and expanding profit margins. However, the company's recent results have been impacted by softer consumer sentiment and the rise of GLP-1 weight-loss treatments. In 2022, Flowers Foods reported net sales of $4.52 billion, a 10.3% increase from the prior year. However, the company's gross margin contracted by 120 basis points to 47.1%, primarily due to higher input costs and supply chain disruptions.

## Impact of GLP-1 Weight-Loss Treatments

This trend has negatively impacted the demand for traditional baked goods, such as bread and buns, which are core products for Flowers Foods. According to a recent survey, 62% of consumers are more likely to choose low-carb or low-sugar options when shopping for baked goods.
