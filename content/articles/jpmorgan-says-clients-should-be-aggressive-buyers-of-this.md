## Broadcom's Undervaluation: A Buying Opportunity
JPMorgan's recent advice to clients to be 'aggressive buyers' of Broadcom stock has sparked interest among investors. The semiconductor company is currently trading at a relative discount, making it an attractive buying opportunity. To understand the rationale behind JPMorgan's recommendation, it's essential to delve into Broadcom's financials and market performance.

### Historical Context: Broadcom's Stock Price
Broadcom's stock price has experienced significant fluctuations over the past year. The company's shares reached an all-time high in August 2021 but have since declined by approximately 30%. This downward trend can be attributed to various factors, including increased competition in the semiconductor industry, global supply chain disruptions, and rising inflation.
