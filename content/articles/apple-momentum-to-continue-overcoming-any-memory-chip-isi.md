### Historical Context

Apple's stock has been on a tear in recent years, with the company's market capitalization surpassing $2 trillion. This growth can be attributed to the company's ability to innovate and expand its product lines, including the highly successful iPhone series. The iPhone, in particular, has been a significant contributor to Apple's revenue, with the company selling over 1 billion units worldwide.

### Catalysts for Growth

According to Evercore ISI, several catalysts are driving Apple's momentum, including:

* **5G Adoption**: The increasing adoption of 5G technology is expected to drive demand for Apple's iPhones, particularly the newer models that support 5G connectivity.
* **Services Segment**: Apple's services segment, which includes Apple Music, Apple TV+, and Apple Arcade, is expected to continue growing, providing a stable source of revenue for the company.
* **Wearables**: The growing demand for wearables, such as the Apple Watch, is expected to contribute to Apple's revenue growth.

### Memory Chip Shortage: A Potential Roadblock

The memory chip shortage has been a significant concern for the tech industry, with many companies struggling to secure sufficient supplies. However, Evercore ISI believes that Apple is well-positioned to overcome this shortage, thanks to its strong relationships with suppliers and its ability to diversify its supply chain.
