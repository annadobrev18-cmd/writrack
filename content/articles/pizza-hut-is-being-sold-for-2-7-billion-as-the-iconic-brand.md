The rise of food delivery apps such as Uber Eats, DoorDash, and GrubHub has disrupted the traditional fast food business model, making it challenging for brands like Pizza Hut to stay competitive.

### The Rise of Food Delivery Apps
Food delivery apps have become increasingly popular in recent years, with the global online food delivery market expected to reach $154.34 billion by 2023. These apps offer customers a wide range of dining options, convenient ordering and payment processes, and fast delivery times. As a result, traditional fast food chains like Pizza Hut have seen a decline in sales as customers opt for the convenience of food delivery apps.
