### The Artemis Mission: A New Era for Space Exploration
The Artemis mission, scheduled to launch in 2024, aims to return humans to the moon by 2025 and establish a sustainable presence on the lunar surface. The mission will utilize SpaceX's Starship and Blue Origin's New Armstrong lunar lander, marking a significant shift towards private-public partnerships in space exploration.
