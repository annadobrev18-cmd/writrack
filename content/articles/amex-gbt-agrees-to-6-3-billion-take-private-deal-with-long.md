### Background of Amex GBT

Amex GBT is a leading business-to-business travel platform that provides travel management services to corporations and organizations. The company was formed in 2018, after American Express acquired a 50% stake in the business. Amex GBT has a significant presence in the global travel industry, with operations in over 140 countries and a client base of over 8,000 companies.

### Details of the Take-Private Deal

The take-private deal between Amex GBT and Long Lake is valued at $6.3 billion, with Long Lake acquiring a majority stake in the company. The deal is subject to regulatory approvals and is expected to close in the second half of 2026. As part of the deal, Amex GBT will become a privately-held company, with Long Lake having a controlling stake.
