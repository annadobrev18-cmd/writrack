### What is PFE-Compliant Certification?

PFE-compliant certification is a third-party verification process that evaluates the performance, quality, and safety of solar trackers. The certification process involves a series of tests and inspections that assess the tracker's ability to withstand various environmental conditions, such as extreme temperatures, humidity, and wind loads. The certification also evaluates the tracker's electrical and mechanical components, as well as its overall design and construction.
