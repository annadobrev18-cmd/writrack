Treatment-resistant depression (TRD) is a growing concern, affecting millions of people worldwide. Traditional treatments often have limited efficacy, leaving patients and healthcare providers seeking alternative solutions. Psychedelic therapies, such as psilocybin and MDMA, have shown significant promise in clinical trials, offering new hope for patients struggling with TRD.
