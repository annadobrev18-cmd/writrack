### Historical Context: Nvidia's Rise to Prominence

To understand the significance of the Goldman Sachs report, it's essential to look at Nvidia's history. Founded in 1993, Nvidia has evolved from a small startup to a dominant player in the tech industry, with a market capitalization of over $500 billion. The company's success can be attributed to its innovative products, particularly in the field of graphics processing units (GPUs) and high-performance computing.

#### Key Milestones

* 1999: Nvidia goes public with an initial public offering (IPO) that raises $67 million
* 2007: Nvidia acquires Ageia Technologies, expanding its presence in the physics engine market
* 2016: Nvidia launches its Pascal-based GPUs, which become a huge success in the gaming and professional markets
