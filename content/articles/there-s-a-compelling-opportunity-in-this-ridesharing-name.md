### Market Impact
The bull call spread opportunity highlighted by Nishant Pant is based on the expectation that the ridesharing company's stock price will increase in the near future. A bull call spread is a options trading strategy that involves buying a call option with a lower strike price and selling a call option with a higher strike price.
