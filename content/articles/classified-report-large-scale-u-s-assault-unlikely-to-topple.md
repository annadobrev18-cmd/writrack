The recent classified report suggesting that a large-scale US assault is unlikely to topple Tehran has significant implications for global markets. ### Historical Context: US-Iran Relations
The US and Iran have had a complex and often tumultuous relationship, with periods of heightened tensions and diplomatic efforts to ease relations. The 1979 Iranian Revolution, which overthrew the US-backed Shah, marked a significant turning point in the relationship. Since then, the US has imposed various economic sanctions on Iran, including those related to its nuclear program.
