### Historical Context

Wells Fargo has been operating under asset restrictions since 2018, when the Federal Reserve imposed a cap on the bank's assets due to concerns over its risk management practices. The restrictions limited the bank's ability to grow its assets, which had a negative impact on its revenue and profitability. However, with the lifting of these restrictions, Wells Fargo is now free to pursue growth opportunities and expand its operations.
