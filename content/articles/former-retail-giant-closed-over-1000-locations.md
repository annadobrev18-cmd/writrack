## Sector Rotations

The closure of over 1,000 stores will have a significant impact on the commercial real estate market. Many of these locations will be left vacant, leading to a surge in available retail space.

#### Vacancy Rates
The vacancy rate for retail space is expected to increase, putting downward pressure on rents. This could have a ripple effect on the entire real estate market, leading to a decline in property values.
