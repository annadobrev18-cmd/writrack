## American Homes 4 Rent: An Overview
American Homes 4 Rent is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that focuses on acquiring, renovating, and leasing single-family homes. The company was founded in 2012 and has since grown to become one of the largest single-family home REITs in the United States.

### Historical Context
The single-family home rental market has experienced significant growth in recent years, driven by changing demographics and housing preferences. American Homes 4 Rent has been at the forefront of this trend, capitalizing on the demand for rental housing and expanding its portfolio of properties.
