### Strengths

Corteva's strengths can be attributed to its diverse product portfolio, global presence, and commitment to research and development. The company offers a wide range of products, including seeds, traits, and crop protection chemicals, which cater to the needs of farmers worldwide. Corteva's global presence, with operations in over 140 countries, enables the company to tap into emerging markets and capitalize on growth opportunities.

| Financial Metrics | 2022 | 2021 | 2020 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Revenue | $15.6B | $14.2B | $13.1B |
| Net Income | $1.3B | $1.1B | $943M |
| Gross Margin | 42.1% | 40.5% | 39.2% |

Corteva's commitment to research and development is evident in its significant investment in R&D, which has resulted in the development of innovative products such as the Enlist weed control system and the PowerCore trait. These products have been well-received by farmers and have contributed to the company's revenue growth.

### Weaknesses

Despite its strengths, Corteva also faces several weaknesses that could impact its growth potential. One of the significant weaknesses is the company's dependence on a few key products, such as the Enlist weed control system and the PowerCore trait. Any decline in demand for these products could significantly impact Corteva's revenue and profitability.

Another weakness is the company's high debt levels, which could limit its ability to invest in new products and technologies. Corteva's debt-to-equity ratio is higher than that of its peers, which could make it more challenging for the company to navigate economic downturns.

### Opportunities

The agribusiness industry is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, driven by increasing demand for food production and the need for sustainable agricultural practices. Corteva is well-positioned to capitalize on this growth, with its diverse product portfolio and global presence.

One of the significant opportunities for Corteva is the growing demand for sustainable agricultural practices. The company's commitment to sustainability is evident in its development of products such as the Enlist weed control system, which reduces the use of herbicides and promotes more sustainable farming practices.

#### Emerging Markets

Corteva also has significant opportunities in emerging markets, where the demand for food production is increasing rapidly. The company's global presence, with operations in over 140 countries, enables it to tap into these markets and capitalize on growth opportunities.

### Threats

The agribusiness industry is highly competitive, with several players competing for market share. Corteva faces significant competition from companies such as Bayer, DowDuPont, and Syngenta, which could impact its revenue and profitability.

Another threat is the regulatory environment, which is becoming increasingly stringent. The use of crop protection chemicals and genetically modified organisms (GMOs) is being scrutinized by regulatory bodies, which could impact Corteva's ability to develop and market new products.

#### Global Economic Uncertainty

The global economic uncertainty, driven by factors such as trade wars and COVID-19, could also impact Corteva's growth potential. The company's global presence, with operations in over 140 countries, makes it vulnerable to economic downturns in key markets.
