## The Pizza Industry: An Overview
The pizza industry is a highly competitive and saturated market, with numerous players vying for dominance. The industry has experienced significant growth over the years, driven by changing consumer preferences and the rise of online ordering and delivery. In this analysis, we will delve into the financial performance of the industry's key players, including Domino's, Papa John's, and Pizza Hut.

### Historical Context
The pizza industry has a rich history, with the first pizzeria opening in Naples, Italy in 1738. However, it wasn't until the mid-20th century that the industry began to experience significant growth, with the rise of chain restaurants such as Pizza Hut and Domino's. Today, the industry is worth over $45 billion in the United States alone, with thousands of restaurants across the country.
