## Geopolitical Tensions and the Stock Market
The recent escalation of tensions between the US and Iran has sent shockwaves through the global financial markets, causing stocks to retreat. The Dow Jones Industrial Average, a key benchmark for the US stock market, has declined significantly as investors become increasingly risk-averse. This is not an isolated incident, as geopolitical tensions have consistently had a profound impact on the stock market.

### Historical Context: US-Iran Relations
The US and Iran have had a complex and tumultuous relationship for decades, with periods of heightened tensions and diplomacy. The current escalation is rooted in the US withdrawal from the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA), also known as the Iran nuclear deal, in 2018. Since then, the US has reimposed sanctions on Iran, leading to a significant decline in Iran's economy. Iran has responded by increasing its uranium enrichment activities, which has raised concerns about the country's nuclear program.
