## US Labor Market Posts Largest Jobs Gain in 15 Months
The US labor market has posted its largest jobs gain in 15 months, with the economy adding a significant number of jobs in March. This uptick in employment growth is a positive sign for the US economy, which has been facing challenges from the ongoing Iran war. The unemployment rate also fell to 4.3%, indicating a strong labor market.

### Breakdown of the Jobs Report
The jobs report showed that the economy added 311,000 jobs in March, exceeding expectations. The jobs gain was driven by the service sector, which added 221,000 jobs, while the goods-producing sector added 90,000 jobs. The construction sector also saw a significant increase, with 60,000 jobs added.
