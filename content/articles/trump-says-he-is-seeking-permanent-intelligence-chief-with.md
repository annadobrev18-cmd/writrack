## Trump's Search for a Permanent Intelligence Chief
The recent announcement by Trump that he is seeking a permanent intelligence chief with security experience has sent ripples through the US market. This move is expected to have significant implications for the country's national security and geopolitical stance.

### Background and Context
The US intelligence community has been without a permanent leader since the departure of Dan Coats in August 2019. The position has been filled by acting directors, including Joseph Maguire and Richard Grenell. The lack of a permanent leader has raised concerns about the stability and effectiveness of the intelligence community.
