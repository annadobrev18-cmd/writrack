## Fundamentals of J.M. Smucker
J.M. Smucker (SJM) is a leading manufacturer and marketer of food and beverage products in North America. The company's portfolio includes iconic brands such as Smucker's, Folgers, and Jif. SJM has a long history of delivering strong financial performance, with a focus on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

### Historical Financial Performance
In recent years, SJM has demonstrated a solid track record of revenue growth, driven by a combination of organic expansion and strategic acquisitions. The company's net sales have consistently increased, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 4.5% over the past five years. Additionally, SJM has maintained a strong profit margin, with an average operating margin of 15.6% during the same period.
