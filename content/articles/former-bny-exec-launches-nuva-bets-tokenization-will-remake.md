## The Emergence of Tokenization
The traditional financial landscape of Wall Street is on the cusp of a significant transformation, driven by the advent of tokenization. This innovative technology enables the conversion of tangible and intangible assets into digital tokens, which can be traded on blockchain networks. The brainchild of a former BNY executive, NUVA is a pioneering platform that is poised to capitalize on the vast potential of tokenization, with the aim of revolutionizing the way assets are traded and managed on Wall Street.

### Historical Context
The concept of tokenization has been gaining traction over the past few years, with various industries exploring its potential applications. The art world, for instance, has seen the emergence of tokenized art pieces, allowing investors to purchase fractional ownership of high-value artworks. Similarly, the real estate sector has witnessed the tokenization of properties, enabling investors to buy and sell fractional ownership of physical assets. However, the application of tokenization in the financial sector, particularly on Wall Street, is a relatively new phenomenon.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Tokenization is the process of converting tangible and intangible assets into digital tokens, which can be traded on blockchain networks. The process involves the creation of a digital representation of the asset, which is then stored on a blockchain.
