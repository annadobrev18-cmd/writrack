## Neoclouds: The Stopgap Solution to GPU Shortage
The recent surge in neocloud stocks on Wall Street has left many investors and analysts wondering if the hype is justified. Neoclouds emerged as a stopgap solution to address the GPU shortage, which has been a major bottleneck in the development and deployment of artificial intelligence (AI) technologies. However, a recent report by McKinsey has warned that the economics of neoclouds are fragile, and investors should exercise caution when investing in these stocks.

### The Rise of Neoclouds
Neoclouds are cloud-based infrastructure providers that offer AI-specific computing resources, such as graphics processing units (GPUs) and tensor processing units (TPUs), to companies and developers. The demand for these resources has skyrocketed in recent years, driven by the growing adoption of AI technologies across various industries. Neoclouds have filled the gap by providing access to these resources on a pay-as-you-go basis, allowing companies to develop and deploy AI models without having to invest in expensive hardware.

### The Fragile Economics of Neoclouds
Despite the growing demand for neocloud services, the economics of these companies are fragile. According to McKinsey, the cost of providing neocloud services is high, and the margins are thin. The report warns that the neocloud industry is likely to experience significant consolidation in the coming years, as companies struggle to achieve scale and profitability.

#### Cost Structure of Neoclouds
The cost structure of neoclouds is complex, with several key components:
* Hardware costs: The cost of purchasing and maintaining GPUs and other AI-specific hardware is high.
* Energy costs: Neoclouds require significant amounts of energy to power their data centers, which adds to their costs.
* Labor costs: Neoclouds require skilled labor to manage and maintain their infrastructure, which is a significant cost component.
* Marketing and sales costs: Neoclouds need to invest heavily in marketing and sales to attract and retain customers.
