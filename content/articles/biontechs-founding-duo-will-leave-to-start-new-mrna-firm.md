### Background on BioNTech and its Founders

BioNTech, a German-based biotechnology company, was founded in 2008 by Ugur Sahin and Ozlem Tureci. The company has been at the forefront of mRNA technology, developing innovative treatments and vaccines for various diseases. Sahin and Tureci, both renowned scientists and entrepreneurs, have been instrumental in driving the company’s success.

#### Key Achievements of BioNTech

* Developed the first approved mRNA-based COVID-19 vaccine, Comirnaty, in collaboration with Pfizer
* Established partnerships with major pharmaceutical companies, including Pfizer and Sanofi
* Expanded its product pipeline to include treatments for cancer, infectious diseases, and rare genetic disorders
