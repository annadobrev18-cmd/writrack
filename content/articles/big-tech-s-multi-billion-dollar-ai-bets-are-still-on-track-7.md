## The Mag 7 Giants' AI Bets: An Overview
The Mag 7 giants, consisting of Amazon, Apple, Google, Facebook, Microsoft, NVIDIA, and Tesla, have been at the forefront of the artificial intelligence (AI) revolution. These tech behemoths have been investing heavily in AI research and development, with the aim of integrating AI into their various products and services. The recent earnings reports from these companies have provided valuable insights into the progress of their AI endeavors.

### Historical Context: The Rise of AI in Big Tech
The concept of AI has been around for decades, but it wasn't until the 2010s that Big Tech began to take notice of its potential. In 2011, IBM's Watson supercomputer defeated human contestants on the game show Jeopardy!, marking a significant milestone in the development of AI. Since then, the Mag 7 giants have been actively investing in AI research, with Google's acquisition of DeepMind in 2014 being a notable example.
