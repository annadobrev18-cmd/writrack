### Historical Context of Revolving Credit Facilities in Real Estate

Revolving credit facilities have long been a staple of corporate finance, particularly in the real estate sector. These facilities allow companies to draw down funds as needed, repay them, and then draw down again, providing a flexible and efficient way to manage cash flows and capitalize on new opportunities. For real estate companies like Sirius, such facilities are crucial for navigating the cyclical nature of the property market, where opportunities can arise suddenly and require swift action.

#### Key Features of the Facility

The €300m revolving credit facility secured by Sirius Real Estate is notable not only for its size but also for its terms, which reflect the company's strong financial position and the confidence of lenders in its business model. The facility is expected to have a competitive interest rate and a maturity period that aligns with Sirius's long-term strategic objectives. This financing arrangement underscores the company's ability to access capital markets on favorable terms, a testament to its reputation and financial health.
