## Revolut's Meteoric Rise
Revolut, the London-based fintech company, has made headlines once again with its staggering $115 billion valuation in a recent employee share sale. This news has sent shockwaves through the financial world, leaving many to wonder what's behind the company's soaring valuation. To understand this phenomenon, it's essential to delve into Revolut's history, its business model, and the factors contributing to its immense success.

### Historical Context
Revolut was founded in 2015 by Nikolay Storonsky and Tom Blomfield, with the aim of revolutionizing the way people manage their finances. The company's early success can be attributed to its innovative approach to banking, which included features like real-time exchange rates, fee-free transactions, and a user-friendly mobile app. Over the years, Revolut has expanded its services to include investment products, credit cards, and even cryptocurrency trading.
