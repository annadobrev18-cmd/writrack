## Trump's Crypto Stance: A Historical Perspective
The cryptocurrency market has been known for its volatility, and one factor that has contributed to this volatility is the stance of influential figures, including former President Donald Trump. Trump's views on cryptocurrency have been mixed, with periods of skepticism and criticism, particularly towards Bitcoin. However, his recent re-entry into the crypto fight raises questions about the potential impact on the market and, more specifically, on stocks like Coinbase.

### Trump's Past Comments on Crypto
Historically, Trump has expressed concerns about the potential for cryptocurrencies to facilitate illicit activities, such as money laundering and drug trafficking. In 2019, he tweeted that he was not a fan of Bitcoin, citing its volatility and the potential for it to be used for unlawful activities. These comments led to a significant drop in the price of Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies.
