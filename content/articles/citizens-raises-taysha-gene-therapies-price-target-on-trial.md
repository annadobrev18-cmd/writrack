## Fundamentals of Taysha Gene Therapies
Taysha Gene Therapies is a clinical-stage biotechnology company focused on developing gene therapies for the treatment of monogenic diseases of the central nervous system. The company's pipeline includes several promising candidates, each targeting a specific genetic disorder. With a strong foundation in scientific research and a commitment to advancing medical treatments, Taysha Gene Therapies has positioned itself as a leader in the gene therapy sector.

### Pipeline Overview
The company's pipeline is diverse, with multiple programs in various stages of development. This includes treatments for rare genetic disorders such as GM2 gangliosidosis, Rett syndrome, and giant axonal neuropathy, among others. Each of these programs has the potential to address significant unmet medical needs, offering hope to patients and families affected by these devastating diseases.
