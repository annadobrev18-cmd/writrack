### Senior Housing Market Trends
The senior housing market has experienced steady growth, driven by an aging population and increasing life expectancy. This trend is expected to continue, with the US Census Bureau projecting that the population of Americans aged 65 and older will reach 73 million by 2030. As the demand for senior housing continues to rise, investors are looking to capitalize on this growth by investing in REITs that specialize in this area.

### Skilled Nursing Facilities Market Outlook
The skilled nursing facilities sector has also seen significant growth, driven by an increasing need for post-acute care services. The Patient-Driven Payment Model (PDPM) implemented by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) has led to improved reimbursement rates for skilled nursing facilities, making them a more attractive investment opportunity.
