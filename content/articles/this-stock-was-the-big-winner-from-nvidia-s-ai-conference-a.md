### Risk Factors: Challenges Facing Uber Stock
While the partnership with Nvidia is a significant positive catalyst for Uber stock, there are also several risk factors that investors should be aware of. One of the main challenges facing Uber is the increasing competition in the ride-sharing market. With new entrants such as Via and Juno, Uber faces significant competition for market share. Additionally, the company's self-driving car division is still in its infancy, and there are significant technical and regulatory challenges that need to be overcome.

#### Regulatory Challenges: The Future of Self-Driving Cars

According to a recent report, the regulatory challenges facing self-driving cars are significant, with many experts predicting that it will take several years for the necessary regulations to be put in place.
