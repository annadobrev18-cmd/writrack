The job market is undergoing a significant transformation with the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in recruitment processes. In this analysis, we will delve into the fundamentals of Refer, its valuation, risk factors, competitive landscape, and future outlook.

### Fundamentals of Refer
Refer is an AI recruitment startup that utilizes machine learning algorithms to match job seekers with potential employers. The platform uses a unique approach by focusing on the skills and qualifications of the job seeker, rather than just their resume. This approach enables Refer to provide more accurate job matches, increasing the chances of job seekers finding the right opportunity.
