## Fundamentals of Ciena Corp.
Ciena Corp., a leading provider of networking equipment, has been on a tear in recent years, with its stock price doubling in 2026 after nearly tripling in 2025. This impressive growth can be attributed to the company's strong fundamentals, including high demand for its products and growing market share. According to Bank of America, Ciena Corp. is well-positioned to continue this growth trajectory, driven by the increasing adoption of artificial intelligence (AI) and the need for faster, more reliable networking infrastructure.

### Historical Performance
To understand Ciena Corp.'s growth potential, it's essential to examine the company's historical performance. Over the past five years, Ciena Corp.'s revenue has grown at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 10.3%, with net income increasing at a CAGR of 15.1%. This growth has been driven by the company's expanding product portfolio, which includes a range of networking equipment, such as optical transport systems, packet-optical transport systems, and software-based solutions.

## Valuation and Risk Factors
While Ciena Corp.'s growth prospects are promising, it's essential to consider the company's valuation and potential risk factors. However, given the company's strong growth prospects and expanding profit margins, this valuation may be justified.
