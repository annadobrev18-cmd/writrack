### Historical Context: Pliant Therapeutics' Journey

Pliant Therapeutics was founded in 2015 with a focus on developing novel therapies for the treatment of fibrotic diseases and cancer. The company's lead candidates, PLN-74809 and PLN-49076, are being investigated for the treatment of various types of cancer, including non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) and pancreatic cancer. Over the years, Pliant Therapeutics has made significant progress in its clinical trials, with several milestones achieved in 2022.

#### Key Milestones in 2022

* Completed enrollment in the Phase 2 trial of PLN-74809 in NSCLC
* Initiated a Phase 1b trial of PLN-49076 in combination with chemotherapy in pancreatic cancer
* Presented positive interim data from the Phase 2 trial of PLN-74809 at the European Society for Medical Oncology (ESMO) Congress
