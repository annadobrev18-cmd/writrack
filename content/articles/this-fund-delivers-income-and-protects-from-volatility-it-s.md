### Fundamentals of the Franklin International Low Volatility High Dividend Index ETF
This ETF is designed to track the performance of the Morningstar Global ex-US Target Volatility Index, which focuses on high dividend-yielding stocks from developed and emerging markets outside the United States. By investing in a diversified portfolio of international stocks with a history of stable dividend payments, the fund aims to provide investors with a relatively calm investment experience, even during periods of market turbulence.

## Fed Implications and Global Ripple Effects
The actions of the Federal Reserve, particularly regarding interest rates, have a significant impact on the global economy and, by extension, on the performance of the Franklin International Low Volatility High Dividend Index ETF. An increase in interest rates can lead to a strengthening of the US dollar, which may negatively affect the value of international stocks held by the fund. Conversely, a decrease in interest rates can lead to a flow of capital into international markets, potentially boosting the fund's performance.
