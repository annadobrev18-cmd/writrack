Hedging is a risk management strategy that involves taking a position in a security that offsets the risk of another security. By hedging their investment, investors can minimize their losses and maximize their returns.

#### Stop-Loss Orders
Stop-loss orders are a risk management strategy that involves setting a price at which to sell a security to minimize losses. By using stop-loss orders, investors can limit their losses and protect their investment.
