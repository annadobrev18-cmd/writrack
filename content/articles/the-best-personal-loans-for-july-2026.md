### Historical Context: The Evolution of Personal Loans

The personal loan market has undergone significant changes over the past decade. Prior to the financial crisis of 2008, personal loans were primarily offered by traditional banks and credit unions, with strict credit requirements and high interest rates. However, with the rise of online lending platforms, borrowers now have access to a broader range of lenders and more competitive rates.
