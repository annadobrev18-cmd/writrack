### RBC Capital's Outperform Rating: A Vote of Confidence
RBC Capital's reiteration of IBM's Outperform rating is a significant vote of confidence in the company's strategic direction. The rating is based on the firm's expectation that IBM will continue to benefit from its investments in AI security, which is becoming an increasingly critical component of the company's portfolio. The Outperform rating suggests that RBC Capital expects IBM's stock to outperform the broader market, driven by the company's strong fundamentals and growth prospects.

#### AI Security: A Key Driver of Growth
AI security is a rapidly growing market, driven by the increasing demand for advanced cybersecurity solutions that can detect and respond to sophisticated threats in real-time. IBM's investments in AI security have positioned the company as a leader in this space, with a range of solutions that leverage machine learning and other AI technologies to enhance threat detection and incident response. The company's AI security portfolio includes a range of products and services, including its QRadar security analytics platform and its X-Force threat intelligence platform.
