The recent initiation of FedEx Freight stock with a buy rating by Jefferies, accompanied by a $200 target, has sent ripples of excitement through the investment community. ### Historical Context: FedEx Freight's Journey
To appreciate the significance of Jefferies' buy rating, it's essential to delve into FedEx Freight's history and its position within the FedEx Corporation. FedEx Freight, formerly known as FedEx National LTL, has been a cornerstone of the FedEx network, providing less-than-truckload (LTL) freight services across the United States. Over the years, the company has expanded its services, investing in technology and infrastructure to improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.
