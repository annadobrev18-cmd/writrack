### Overview of Form 144
Form 144 is a legal document that must be filed with the SEC when an insider intends to sell restricted or controlled securities. These securities are typically obtained through private placements or as compensation and are subject to certain restrictions on their resale. The filing of Form 144 does not necessarily mean the sale will occur, but it does signal the insider's intent to do so.
