### Historical Context: Nvidia's Rise to Prominence

Nvidia's journey to becoming a leader in AI infrastructure began in the early 2000s, when the company started developing GPUs for the gaming industry. The high-performance capabilities of these GPUs made them an attractive choice for AI researchers, who were looking for powerful computing resources to train and deploy AI models. As the demand for AI computing grew, Nvidia adapted its GPUs to meet the needs of the AI community, introducing specialized AI-focused products such as the Tesla V100 and the A100.

#### Key Milestones in Nvidia's History

* 2007: Nvidia introduces its first GPU designed specifically for AI computing, the Tesla 8-series.
* 2016: Nvidia launches the Tesla P100, a GPU designed for deep learning applications.
* 2018: Nvidia introduces the Tesla V100, a GPU that becomes the industry standard for AI computing.
* 2020: Nvidia launches the A100, a GPU that offers unprecedented performance and efficiency for AI workloads.
