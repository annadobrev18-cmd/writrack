### Historical Context of Retirement Planning
Retirement planning has undergone significant changes over the years. The traditional pension system, which provided a guaranteed income stream for life, has given way to defined contribution plans, such as 401(k)s and IRAs. This shift has placed a greater burden on individuals to manage their own retirement savings. As a result, it is essential to develop a well-thought-out strategy to ensure a sustainable income stream in retirement.

#### Q: What is the sustainable withdrawal rate, and how is it calculated?
