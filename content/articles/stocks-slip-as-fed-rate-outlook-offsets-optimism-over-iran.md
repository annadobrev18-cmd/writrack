## Fundamentals of the Current Market Situation
The recent signing of a peace deal between the US and Iran has sparked optimism in the global economy, with potential implications for the stock market. However, this optimism has been countered by a hawkish Federal Reserve rate outlook, leading to a slip in stocks. To understand the dynamics at play, it's essential to delve into the fundamentals of the current market situation.

### Impact of the Iran Deal on the Global Economy
The Iran deal is expected to have a positive impact on the global economy, particularly in the energy sector. With the lifting of sanctions, Iran is likely to increase its oil production, leading to an increase in global oil supply. This could result in lower oil prices, which would be beneficial for oil-importing countries and could lead to an increase in consumer spending.

### Federal Reserve Rate Outlook
The Federal Reserve has been hinting at a hawkish rate outlook, with potential interest rate hikes on the horizon. This has led to a decrease in stocks, as higher interest rates can make borrowing more expensive and reduce consumer spending. The Fed's decision to raise interest rates is aimed at controlling inflation, which has been rising due to the ongoing economic recovery.
