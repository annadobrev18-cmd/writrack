The recent price glitch, which resulted in $27 million in liquidations, has raised concerns about the stability of the DeFi market. Liquidations occur when a borrower's collateral falls below a certain threshold, triggering the sale of the collateral to repay the loan. In this case, the price glitch caused a sudden and unexpected drop in the value of LEND, triggering a wave of liquidations.
