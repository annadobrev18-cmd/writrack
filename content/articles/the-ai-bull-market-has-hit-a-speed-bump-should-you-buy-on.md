## The AI Bull Market: Historical Context and Current State
The AI bull market has been one of the most significant trends in the technology sector over the past few years. With the increasing adoption of artificial intelligence and machine learning across various industries, AI-focused companies have seen their stock prices soar. However, the recent market correction has raised concerns among investors, with many wondering if this is a speed bump or a sign of a more significant downturn.

### The Rise of AI Stocks
The AI bull market began to gain momentum in 2020, with the COVID-19 pandemic accelerating the need for digital transformation and automation. Companies like NVIDIA, Alphabet, and Microsoft, which have significant AI-related businesses, saw their stock prices surge. The trend continued in 2021 and 2022, with the launch of new AI-focused companies and the expansion of existing ones.

## Market Impact: Understanding the Correction
The recent market correction has been attributed to various factors, including rising interest rates, inflation concerns, and a slowdown in the global economy. The AI sector, which has been heavily reliant on venture capital and private funding, has been particularly affected. With investors becoming more risk-averse, many AI-focused companies have seen their stock prices decline.

### The Impact on CoreWeave
