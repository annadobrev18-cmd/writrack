### What is Form 144?
Form 144 is a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) that is required when an insider or affiliate of a company intends to sell securities. The form provides details about the proposed sale, including the number of shares to be sold, the price at which they will be sold, and the identity of the seller. In the case of Codere Online Luxembourg, the Form 144 filing indicates that the company's insiders or affiliates are planning to sell a significant number of shares.
