## The Overlooked Opportunity in Utilities
The utilities sector, often considered a stable and defensive play, has been gaining attention recently for its potential to benefit from the AI trade. According to Wells Fargo, a few 'out-of-favor' names in this sector could be poised for significant gains, making them an attractive opportunity for investors seeking both growth and dividend income.

### Historical Context: Utilities Sector Performance
The utilities sector has historically been characterized by its stability and low volatility, making it a favorite among income-seeking investors. However, the sector has also been subject to periods of underperformance, particularly when compared to more growth-oriented sectors like technology. The current environment, with its focus on AI and digital transformation, presents a unique opportunity for utilities companies to leverage technology and improve their operations, potentially leading to increased efficiency and profitability.

### Market Impact: The AI Trade
The AI trade, which refers to the investment theme centered around companies that are expected to benefit from the adoption and development of artificial intelligence technologies, has been a significant driver of market performance in recent years. While the technology sector has been the primary beneficiary of this trend, other sectors, including utilities, are beginning to see the potential benefits of AI adoption. By leveraging AI and machine learning, utilities companies can optimize their operations, predict energy demand, and improve customer service, among other advantages.

### Technical Analysis: Identifying Opportunities
From a technical analysis perspective, several utilities stocks are showing promising signs. These include:
- **Duke Energy (DUK)**: With a strong dividend yield and a history of stable performance, Duke Energy is well-positioned to benefit from the AI trade.
- **Exelon Corporation (EXC)**: As one of the largest utilities companies in the United States, Exelon has the scale and resources to invest in AI and digital technologies, potentially leading to improved efficiency and customer satisfaction.
- **NextEra Energy (NEE)**: With its focus on renewable energy and commitment to innovation, NextEra Energy is an attractive play on the AI trade, offering both growth and income potential.
