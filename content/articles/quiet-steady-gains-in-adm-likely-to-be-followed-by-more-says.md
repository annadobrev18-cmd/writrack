### Business Segments
ADM operates through three primary business segments: Ag Services and Oilseeds, Carbohydrate Solutions, and Nutrition. The Ag Services and Oilseeds segment is the largest contributor to the company's revenue, accounting for approximately 60% of total sales. This segment is involved in the procurement, processing, and distribution of agricultural commodities such as corn, soybeans, and wheat.
