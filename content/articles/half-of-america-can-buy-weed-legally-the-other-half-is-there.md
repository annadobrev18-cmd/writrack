## The Growing Trend of Cannabis Legalization
The landscape of cannabis legislation in the United States has undergone significant changes in recent years. With half of the country now allowing the legal purchase of weed, the other half is quickly catching up. This shift in policy has far-reaching implications for the financial sector, particularly for investors and businesses operating within the cannabis industry.

### Historical Context
To understand the current state of cannabis legalization, it's essential to look at the historical context. The first state to legalize medical cannabis was California in 1996. However, it wasn't until 2012 that Colorado and Washington became the first states to legalize recreational cannabis. Since then, the trend has gained momentum, with more states joining the ranks of those allowing the legal purchase and use of cannabis.
