### Understanding Countertrend Buy Signals
A countertrend buy signal is a technical indicator that suggests a potential reversal in a stock's trend. This signal is often generated by various technical indicators, such as moving averages, relative strength index (RSI), and Bollinger Bands. The signal is considered "countertrend" because it goes against the current trend of the stock, indicating a potential shift in market sentiment.
