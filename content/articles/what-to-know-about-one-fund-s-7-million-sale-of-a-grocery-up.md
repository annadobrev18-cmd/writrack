### Fundamentals of Grocery REITs
Grocery REITs are a type of real estate investment trust that focuses on properties leased to grocery stores. These REITs can provide a steady income stream and relatively low volatility, making them attractive to investors seeking stable returns. The performance of grocery REITs is closely tied to the health of the retail sector, consumer spending habits, and the overall economy.
