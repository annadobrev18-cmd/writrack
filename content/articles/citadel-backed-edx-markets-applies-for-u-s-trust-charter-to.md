### Implications of the U.S. Trust Charter Application
The application for a U.S. trust charter by EDX Markets is a significant development in the institutional crypto space. A trust charter would allow EDX Markets to offer a broader range of services, including custody, settlement, and other post-trade services, to its institutional clients. This would provide a higher level of regulatory clarity and oversight, which is essential for institutional investors who require a high level of security and compliance.
