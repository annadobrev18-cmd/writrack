### Background and History
Cerebras is a pioneer in the field of AI computing, with its flagship product, the WSE (Wafer Scale Engine), being the largest and most powerful AI-focused chip in the world. Founded in 2016 by Andrew Feldman and Sean Lie, the company has been at the forefront of developing cutting-edge AI technologies, with a focus on deep learning and natural language processing.
