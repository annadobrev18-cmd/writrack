### Background on BitLicense
The BitLicense, introduced in 2015, is a business license required for companies that engage in virtual currency business activities in New York. The license is designed to protect consumers and prevent money laundering, while also providing a framework for companies to operate in the state. The approval process is rigorous, with applicants required to meet strict standards for anti-money laundering (AML) and know-your-customer (KYC) procedures, as well as demonstrate the ability to safeguard customer assets.
