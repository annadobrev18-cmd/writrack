### Risk Factors
Investing in mining stocks involves inherent risks, including commodity price volatility, operational disruptions, and environmental concerns. The following section examines the risk factors associated with Rio Tinto and Vale:

#### Commodity Price Volatility
Commodity prices can significantly impact mining companies' revenue and profitability. The prices of iron ore, copper, and nickel have experienced fluctuations in recent years, affecting the financial performance of Rio Tinto and Vale.

#### Operational Disruptions
Mining operations can be disrupted by various factors, including equipment failures, labor disputes, and natural disasters. Companies with a strong track record of operational efficiency and risk management are better positioned to mitigate these risks.
