### Risk Factors: Challenges Facing the Adoption of Blockchain Technology
While the use of blockchain technology in asset management has the potential to drive significant benefits, there are also a number of challenges that need to be addressed. Some of the key risk factors include:

* **Regulatory Uncertainty**: The regulatory environment for blockchain technology is still evolving, and there is a need for greater clarity and consistency in the rules and regulations governing its use.
* **Scalability**: The use of blockchain technology is still in its early stages, and there are concerns about its ability to scale to meet the needs of large asset managers.
* **Cyber Security**: The use of blockchain technology also raises concerns about cyber security, and the need for robust measures to protect assets from attack.
