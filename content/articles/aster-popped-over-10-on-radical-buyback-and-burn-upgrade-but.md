### The 'Buyback and Burn' Mechanism: A Primer
The 'buyback and burn' mechanism is a strategy employed by some cryptocurrency projects to reduce the circulating supply of their tokens. The process involves buying back a portion of the outstanding tokens from the market and subsequently 'burning' them, effectively removing them from circulation. This reduction in supply can lead to increased demand, potentially driving up the token's price.
