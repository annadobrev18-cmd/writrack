#### Evolution of Cryptocurrency Derivatives
The approval of perpetual swaps is the latest development in the evolution of cryptocurrency derivatives. Perpetual swaps are a type of derivative contract that allows investors to bet on the price of a cryptocurrency without actually holding the underlying asset. They are similar to traditional futures contracts but do not have an expiration date, hence the term 'perpetual.' This type of contract has become increasingly popular in the cryptocurrency market, with many exchanges offering perpetual swap contracts to their clients.
