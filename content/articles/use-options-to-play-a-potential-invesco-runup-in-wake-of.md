### Options Trading: A Strategy for Playing the Invesco Runup
Options trading can provide a flexible and potentially lucrative way to play the Invesco runup. By purchasing call options or selling put options, investors can gain exposure to the stock's potential upside while limiting their downside risk.

#### Bullish Options Strategies
The following bullish options strategies can be used to play the Invesco runup:
* Buying call options: This involves purchasing call options with a strike price near the current market price. If the stock price increases, the call option will increase in value, allowing the investor to sell it for a profit.
* Selling put options: This involves selling put options with a strike price near the current market price. If the stock price remains above the strike price, the put option will expire worthless, and the investor will retain the premium received from selling the option.
