### Historical Context: The Rise of AI Chip Manufacturing

The AI chip boom is not a new phenomenon. Over the past decade, the tech industry has witnessed a gradual shift towards AI-powered solutions, driven by advancements in machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. This shift has created a massive demand for specialized chips that can handle the complex computations required for AI processing. Companies like Samsung, Micron, and now SK Hynix have been at the forefront of this trend, investing heavily in research and development to create cutting-edge AI chips.
