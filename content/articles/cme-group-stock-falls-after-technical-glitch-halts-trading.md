### Historical Context of CME Group

CME Group has a long history dating back to 1898 when it was founded as the Chicago Butter and Egg Board. Over the years, the exchange has undergone significant transformations, including its merger with the Chicago Mercantile Exchange (CME) in 2002 and its acquisition of the New York Mercantile Exchange (NYMEX) in 2008. Today, CME Group operates several exchanges, including the Chicago Mercantile Exchange, the Chicago Board of Trade, and the New York Mercantile Exchange.
