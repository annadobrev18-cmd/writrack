To understand the significance of Tesla's recent upgrade, it's essential to look at the company's history. Founded in 2003 by Elon Musk, Martin Eberhard, and Marc Tarpenning, Tesla has come a long way since its early days as a small startup. With the launch of the Roadster in 2008, Tesla proved that electric vehicles could be both stylish and performance-driven. Since then, the company has expanded its product line to include the Model S, Model X, Model 3, and Model Y, among others.
