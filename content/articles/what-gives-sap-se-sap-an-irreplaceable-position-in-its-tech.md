### Historical Context and Evolution

To understand SAP's current dominance, it's essential to delve into its history. Founded in 1972 by five former IBM employees, SAP started as a small enterprise resource planning (ERP) software developer. Over the years, the company has expanded its product portfolio through strategic acquisitions and organic growth, adapting to the evolving needs of the market. Today, SAP offers a broad range of solutions, from ERP and customer relationship management (CRM) to supply chain management and cloud computing services.
