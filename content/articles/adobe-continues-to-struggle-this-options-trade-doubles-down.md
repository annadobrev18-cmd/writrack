## Options Trading Strategy
Options trader Nishant Pant has revisited the bull call spread strategy for Adobe, doubling down on a potential rebound. The bull call spread involves buying a call option with a lower strike price and selling a call option with a higher strike price. This strategy is designed to profit from a potential increase in the stock price while limiting the risk.

### Bull Call Spread Details
The specific bull call spread strategy involves buying the Adobe $370 call option and selling the $400 call option. The trade has a net cost of $10, with a potential profit of $20 if the stock price reaches $400. The trade has a limited risk of $10 if the stock price falls below $370.
