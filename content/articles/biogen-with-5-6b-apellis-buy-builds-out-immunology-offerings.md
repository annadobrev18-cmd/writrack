Biogen's recent acquisition of Apellis for $5.6 billion marks a significant move to enhance its immunology portfolio. This strategic expansion is expected to strengthen Biogen's position in the pharmaceutical industry, particularly in the immunology segment. The acquisition is a testament to Biogen's commitment to investing in innovative therapies that can potentially transform the lives of patients worldwide.

### Background on Apellis
Apellis is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on developing transformative therapies for patients with serious diseases. The company's lead product candidate, pegcetacoplan, is an investigational, targeted C3 therapy for the treatment of several complement-mediated diseases, including paroxysmal nocturnal hemoglobinuria (PNH) and geographic atrophy (GA). Apellis's pipeline also includes other promising therapies, such as APL-2, which is being investigated for the treatment of various complement-mediated diseases.
