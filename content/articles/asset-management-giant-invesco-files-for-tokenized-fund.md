### The Stablecoin Reserve Market: An Emerging Opportunity
The stablecoin reserve market has gained significant attention in recent years, driven by the growing demand for stablecoins as a store of value and a medium of exchange. Stablecoins are cryptocurrencies designed to maintain a stable value relative to a fiat currency, such as the US dollar. The reserve market for stablecoins refers to the pool of assets held in reserve to back the value of these stablecoins. Invesco's tokenized fund is targeting this reserve market, aiming to provide investors with a new avenue for exposure to the growing stablecoin ecosystem.
