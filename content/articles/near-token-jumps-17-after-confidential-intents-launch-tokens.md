### Understanding 'Confidential Intents'
'Confidential Intents' is a new feature launched on the NEAR protocol, designed to enhance the privacy and security of transactions on the blockchain. This innovation allows users to conceal their transaction intentions, making it more challenging for external parties to trace the origin and destination of funds. The primary goal of 'Confidential Intents' is to provide an additional layer of privacy, which is a critical aspect of blockchain technology.
