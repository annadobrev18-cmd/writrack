## Risk Factors

While the CION Ares Diversified Credit Fund offers attractive returns and valuation metrics, there are several risk factors that investors should be aware of. These include:

### Credit Risk

The fund's investments in corporate loans and high-yield bonds are subject to credit risk, which is the risk that the borrower will default on their obligations. The fund's investment team seeks to mitigate this risk through careful credit analysis and diversification.

### Interest Rate Risk

The fund's investments in fixed-rate debt securities are subject to interest rate risk, which is the risk that changes in interest rates will affect the value of the securities. The fund's investment team seeks to mitigate this risk through active portfolio management and duration management.

### Liquidity Risk

The fund's investments in less liquid debt securities are subject to liquidity risk, which is the risk that the fund will be unable to sell the securities quickly enough or at a fair price. The fund's investment team seeks to mitigate this risk through careful portfolio construction and liquidity management.
