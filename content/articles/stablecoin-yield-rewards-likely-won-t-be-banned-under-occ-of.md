### Background: Stablecoins and Yield Rewards

Stablecoins are a type of cryptocurrency that is pegged to the value of a traditional currency, such as the US dollar. They are designed to reduce the volatility associated with other cryptocurrencies, making them an attractive option for investors seeking stability. Yield rewards, on the other hand, refer to the interest earned by investors who lend or stake their stablecoins. This concept has gained popularity in recent years, with many platforms offering yield rewards to attract investors.

### The OCC Proposal: Key Takeaways

The OCC proposal aims to provide clarity on the regulatory framework for stablecoins, including yield rewards. The key takeaways from the proposal are:

* Stablecoins are considered a type of bank-issued digital currency, and as such, are subject to banking regulations.
* Yield rewards are not considered a security, and therefore, are not subject to securities laws.
* The proposal emphasizes the importance of risk management and consumer protection in the stablecoin market.

### Implications for the Crypto Market
