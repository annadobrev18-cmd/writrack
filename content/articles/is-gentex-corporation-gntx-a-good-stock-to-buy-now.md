## Gentex Corporation Overview
Gentex Corporation (GNTX) is a leading global manufacturer of automatic-dimming rearview mirrors, automotive electronics, and other related products. Founded in 1974, the company has established itself as a key player in the automotive industry, with a strong presence in the global market. As an investor, it's essential to evaluate the company's financial health, market trends, and competitive landscape to determine if GNTX is a good stock to buy now.

### Historical Context
Gentex Corporation has a long history of innovation and growth. The company's early success was driven by its development of automatic-dimming rearview mirrors, which quickly became a standard feature in many vehicles. Over the years, Gentex has expanded its product line to include a range of automotive electronics, such as cameras, sensors, and displays. This strategic diversification has enabled the company to maintain its competitive edge and adapt to changing market trends.
