### Historical Context: Broadcom's Journey to Success
Broadcom has been a major player in the semiconductor industry for decades. Founded in 1991, the company has undergone significant transformations, including its acquisition by Avago Technologies in 2016. Since then, Broadcom has focused on expanding its product portfolio, particularly in the area of specialty chips. These chips are designed for specific applications, such as artificial intelligence, 5G, and the Internet of Things (IoT).
