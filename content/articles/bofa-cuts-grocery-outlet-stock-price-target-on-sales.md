## Fundamentals of Grocery Outlet
Grocery Outlet is a leading extreme-value grocery retailer in the United States, offering a wide selection of products at discounted prices. The company operates on a unique business model, leveraging a flexible supply chain and opportunistic purchasing to provide customers with significant savings. However, recent sales weakness has raised concerns among investors, prompting BofA to cut its stock price target for Grocery Outlet.

### Historical Performance
To understand the current situation, it's essential to examine Grocery Outlet's historical performance.
