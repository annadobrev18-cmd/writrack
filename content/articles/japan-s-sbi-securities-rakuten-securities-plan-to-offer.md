## Investment Trusts
Investment trusts are a type of investment vehicle that allows individuals to invest in a diversified portfolio of assets. In the case of crypto investment trusts, the trust will invest in a portfolio of digital assets, providing investors with exposure to the cryptocurrency market.

### Benefits
The introduction of crypto investment trusts is expected to provide several benefits to investors, including:

* Diversification: Investment trusts allow investors to gain exposure to a diversified portfolio of digital assets, reducing the risk associated with investing in a single cryptocurrency.
* Convenience: Investment trusts provide a convenient way for investors to gain exposure to the cryptocurrency market, without the need to directly purchase and store digital assets.
* Regulatory compliance: Investment trusts are subject to regulatory oversight, providing an additional layer of protection for investors.

### Risks
While investment trusts can provide several benefits, there are also risks associated with investing in these vehicles. Some of the key risks include:

* Market risk: The value of the investment trust can fluctuate based on changes in the cryptocurrency market.
* Regulatory risk: Changes in regulations can impact the investment trust and the underlying digital assets.
* Security risk: The investment trust may be subject to security risks, such as hacking and theft.
