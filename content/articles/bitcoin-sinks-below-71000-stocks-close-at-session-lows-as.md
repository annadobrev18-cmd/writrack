### Historical Context of Fed Rate Cuts

To understand the significance of the current situation, it's essential to look at the historical context of Fed rate cuts. The Federal Reserve has been using interest rates as a tool to control inflation and stimulate economic growth. In recent years, the Fed has been on a rate-hiking cycle, aiming to combat rising inflation. However, with the economy showing signs of slowing down, many investors had been hoping for a rate cut in 2026 to provide a much-needed boost.

#### Impact of Rate Cuts on Bitcoin and Stocks

Rate cuts can have a significant impact on both Bitcoin and stocks. For Bitcoin, a rate cut can lead to an increase in investment, as lower interest rates make it more attractive to invest in riskier assets. On the other hand, stocks can also benefit from a rate cut, as lower borrowing costs can lead to increased consumer spending and economic growth.

## Market Impact of Fading Rate Cut Hopes

The fading hopes of a 2026 Fed rate cut have had a significant impact on the market. Bitcoin, which had been hovering around the $80,000 mark, has sunk below $71,000. Stocks have also been affected, with the major indexes closing at session lows.
