## Allegations of Surveillance Pricing: Understanding the Lawsuit
The recent lawsuit against JetBlue over alleged surveillance pricing practices has sent shockwaves through the airline industry. The lawsuit claims that JetBlue has been using advanced technology to track and analyze consumer behavior, allowing the company to adjust prices in real-time and maximize profits. This practice, known as surveillance pricing, has raised concerns among consumers and regulators alike.

### History of Surveillance Pricing
Surveillance pricing is not a new concept in the airline industry. In fact, many airlines have been using similar practices for years to stay competitive. However, the use of advanced technology to track consumer behavior has raised the stakes. With the ability to analyze vast amounts of data, airlines can now adjust prices with unprecedented precision.
