### Understanding Form 144

Form 144 is a document filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) by insiders of a publicly traded company, such as officers, directors, or significant shareholders, to disclose their intention to sell securities. This filing is mandatory under SEC Rule 144, which governs the sale of restricted and control securities.
