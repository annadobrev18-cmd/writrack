The cryptocurrency market is experiencing its worst weekly decline since the collapse of FTX, with Bitcoin and Ethereum leading the downturn. ## Historical Context
The FTX collapse in November 2022 sent shockwaves through the cryptocurrency market, leading to a significant decline in prices. Bitcoin, the largest cryptocurrency by market capitalization, fell by over 20% in the aftermath of the collapse. Ethereum, the second-largest cryptocurrency, also suffered significant losses. The market has been struggling to recover ever since, with periodic bouts of volatility.
