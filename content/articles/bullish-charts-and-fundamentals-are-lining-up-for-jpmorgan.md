### Fundamentals: A Strong Q2 Earnings Report

JPMorgan Chase reported a net income of $9.6 billion for the second quarter, a 10% increase from the same period last year. The company's revenue also increased by 5% to $33.8 billion, beating analyst expectations of $32.6 billion. The strong earnings report was driven by a significant increase in interest income, which rose by 15% to $14.1 billion. The company's non-interest expenses also decreased by 2% to $16.3 billion, contributing to the overall increase in net income.
