## Bitcoin's Recent Surge: A Reaction to Global Uncertainty
The recent surge in Bitcoin's price can be attributed to various factors, including the geopolitical tensions arising from the Iran situation. As investors seek safe-haven assets, cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin have witnessed significant inflows, with BTC ETFs posting one of the quarter's biggest inflow days.

### Historical Context: Bitcoin's Performance During Times of Uncertainty
Bitcoin has historically performed well during times of global uncertainty. The cryptocurrency's decentralized nature and limited supply make it an attractive asset for investors seeking to diversify their portfolios. In 2020, during the COVID-19 pandemic, Bitcoin's price surged as investors turned to safe-haven assets.
