### Historical Context: Deckers Outdoor's Journey

Deckers Outdoor is a leading designer, marketer, and distributor of footwear, apparel, and accessories. The company was founded in 1973 by two University of California, Santa Barbara students, and it has since grown into a global brand with a portfolio of popular brands, including UGG, Teva, Sanuk, and Hoka One One. Over the years, Deckers Outdoor has demonstrated resilience and adaptability, navigating through various market trends and consumer preferences.

#### Key Milestones

* 1973: Deckers Outdoor was founded by Doug Otto and Karl Lopker
* 1995: The company went public with an initial public offering (IPO)
* 2000: Deckers Outdoor acquired the Teva brand
* 2007: The company acquired the Sanuk brand
* 2015: Deckers Outdoor acquired the Hoka One One brand
* 2020: The company launched its e-commerce platform, further expanding its online presence
