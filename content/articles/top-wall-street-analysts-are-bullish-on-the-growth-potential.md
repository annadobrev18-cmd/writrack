Top analysts believe that Alphabet's commitment to AI will continue to drive growth, with the company's stock expected to increase by 15% in the next 12 months.

Microsoft Corporation has also been investing heavily in AI research and development, with a focus on integrating AI into its core products and services. ### Company 3: NVIDIA Corporation (NVDA)

NVIDIA Corporation has been a key player in the AI space, with its graphics processing units (GPUs) being used to power many AI applications. The company's investments in AI research and development have yielded significant returns, with its AI-powered data center business experiencing rapid growth. Top analysts believe that NVIDIA's commitment to AI will continue to drive growth, with the company's stock expected to increase by 20% in the next 12 months.

#### Risk Factors

Despite the bullish sentiments of top analysts, there are several risk factors that investors should be aware of. These include:

* Regulatory uncertainty surrounding AI adoption
* Potential disruptions to global supply chains
