### Apple: A Diversified Tech Giant

Apple, one of the world's most valuable companies, has been a consistent performer in the tech sector. ### Nike and Netflix: Consumer Discretionary Plays

Nike, the leading sportswear brand, and Netflix, the pioneer in streaming services, were also in the spotlight on Friday. Analyst calls for Nike focused on the company's plans to expand its e-commerce platform, improve its supply chain efficiency, and enhance its product offerings.
