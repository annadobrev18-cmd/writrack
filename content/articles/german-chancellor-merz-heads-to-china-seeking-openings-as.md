### Risk Factors: Challenges and Opportunities
The visit poses both challenges and opportunities for Germany, the European Union, and the global economy as a whole. On one hand, a successful visit could lead to increased trade and investment between Germany and China, potentially offsetting the negative impacts of the trade war and boosting economic growth. On the other hand, the visit may be perceived as a departure from the EU's collective stance on China, potentially straining relationships with other EU member states and the United States.

#### Key Risk Factors
