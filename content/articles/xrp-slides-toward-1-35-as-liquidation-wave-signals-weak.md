## XRP's Price Slide: A Closer Look
XRP, one of the most widely traded cryptocurrencies, has been experiencing a significant price slide, with its value plummeting towards $1.35. This downward trend has been exacerbated by a liquidation wave, which has sent shockwaves throughout the crypto market. As an institutional investor or sophisticated trader, it's essential to understand the underlying factors contributing to this price slide and its implications for the broader crypto market.

### Liquidation Wave: A Key Driver of XRP's Price Slide
The liquidation wave that has engulfed XRP is a critical factor in its price slide. Liquidation waves occur when a large number of traders are forced to sell their assets, often due to margin calls or other financial pressures. This can create a cascade effect, where the selling pressure becomes self-reinforcing, driving prices lower. In the case of XRP, the liquidation wave has been particularly severe, with many traders being caught off guard by the sudden price drop.
