## Fundamentals of Trane Technologies
Trane Technologies is a global climate innovator, with a portfolio of brands that include Trane and Thermo King. The company provides sustainable climate solutions for buildings, homes, and transportation. As the world shifts towards more sustainable and energy-efficient solutions, Trane Technologies is well-positioned to capitalize on this trend.

### Historical Performance
Trane Technologies has consistently delivered strong financial performance over the past few years. The company's revenue has grown at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 10% from 2020 to 2025, driven by increasing demand for sustainable climate solutions. The company's net income has also grown at a CAGR of 15% over the same period, driven by operational efficiency and cost savings.
