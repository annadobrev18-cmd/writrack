### Background on Payward and Kraken

Payward is a private company founded in 2011 by Jesse Powell. The company's primary business is operating the Kraken cryptocurrency exchange, which was launched in 2013. Kraken is one of the largest and most reputable cryptocurrency exchanges in the world, offering a wide range of digital assets for trading. The exchange is known for its high liquidity, robust security features, and user-friendly interface.

### Funding and Valuation

Payward's decision to seek fresh funding at a $20 billion valuation is a significant development in the cryptocurrency industry.
