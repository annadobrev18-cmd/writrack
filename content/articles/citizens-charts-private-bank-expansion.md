### Historical Context of Private Banking Expansion

The private banking sector has experienced significant growth in recent years, driven by an increase in wealth creation and a rising demand for sophisticated financial services. According to a report by Deloitte, the global private banking market is expected to reach $83.4 billion by 2025, growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.1%. This growth is driven by the increasing wealth of high net worth individuals, who require tailored financial solutions to manage their assets.

### Sector Rotation and Market Implications

The expansion of Citizens Bank's private banking operations is expected to have significant implications for the financial sector. ### Global Ripple Effects of Private Banking Expansion

The growth of the private banking sector is not limited to the United States; it is a global phenomenon. According to a report by Knight Frank, the number of ultra-high net worth individuals (UHNWIs) in Asia is expected to increase by 39% over the next five years, driving demand for private banking services.
