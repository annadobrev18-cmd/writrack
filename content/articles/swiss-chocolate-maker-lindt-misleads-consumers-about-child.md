## Lindt's Child Labor Controversy: An Overview
Lindt, the renowned Swiss chocolate maker, is facing a lawsuit in the US over claims that it misleads consumers about child labor practices in its supply chain. This controversy has significant implications for the company's financials, reputation, and social responsibility.

### Historical Context
Lindt has been a leader in the chocolate industry for over 175 years, known for its high-quality products and commitment to sustainability. However, the company has faced criticism in the past over its sourcing practices, particularly with regards to cocoa. In 2020, Lindt announced its commitment to sourcing 100% sustainable cocoa by 2025, but the current lawsuit alleges that the company has not done enough to prevent child labor in its supply chain.
