### Background: Binance.US and its Regulatory Challenges

Binance.US was launched in 2019 as a separate entity from its parent company, Binance, to cater to the US market. However, it has faced significant regulatory challenges, including a lawsuit from the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in 2022. The lawsuit alleged that Binance had failed to register as a securities exchange and had engaged in unregistered securities offerings. As a result, Binance.US has been operating with limited functionality and a restricted range of trading products.
