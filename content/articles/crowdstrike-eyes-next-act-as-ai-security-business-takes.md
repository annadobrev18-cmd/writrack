## The Rise of CrowdStrike: A Leader in Cybersecurity
CrowdStrike has been making waves in the cybersecurity industry with its innovative approach to threat detection and prevention. The company's cloud-native platform has been a game-changer, providing real-time protection against sophisticated cyber threats. With the increasing demand for AI-powered security solutions, CrowdStrike is poised to take its business to the next level.

### Historical Context: The Evolution of Cybersecurity
The cybersecurity landscape has undergone significant changes over the years. The rise of cloud computing, mobile devices, and the Internet of Things (IoT) has created new vulnerabilities that traditional security systems are unable to handle. CrowdStrike's founding in 2011 marked a turning point in the industry, as the company introduced a cloud-native platform that leveraged artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to detect and prevent threats.

#### Key Milestones in CrowdStrike's History
- 2011: CrowdStrike was founded by George Kurtz, Dmitri Alperovitch, and Gregg Marston.
- 2013: The company launched its Falcon platform, a cloud-native security solution that used AI and ML to detect and prevent threats.
- 2017: CrowdStrike went public with an initial public offering (IPO) that raised $612 million.
- 2020: The company acquired Preempt Security, a provider of zero-trust security solutions.
