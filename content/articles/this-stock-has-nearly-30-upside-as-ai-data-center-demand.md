## AI Data Center Demand on the Rise
The increasing adoption of artificial intelligence (AI) is driving the demand for specialized data center infrastructure. As AI models become more complex and require significant computational power, the need for high-performance data centers is growing. This trend is expected to continue, with the global AI market projected to reach $190 billion by 2025, growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 38%.

### Historical Context
The growth of the AI market has been rapid, with significant investments in AI research and development over the past decade. The development of deep learning algorithms and the availability of large datasets have enabled the creation of sophisticated AI models. However, the deployment of these models requires significant computational resources, which has led to an increase in demand for data center infrastructure.
