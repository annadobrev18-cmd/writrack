## Dell's AI Server Ambitions
Dell, a household name in the technology sector, is poised for a significant surge in its stock price, according to a recent prediction by Bernstein. The investment firm believes that Dell's AI server revenue could reach a staggering $50 billion by fiscal year 2027, setting the stage for a potential 50% rally in the company's stock. This prediction is based on the growing demand for AI-powered servers, which are becoming increasingly essential for businesses and organizations looking to harness the power of artificial intelligence.

### Historical Context
To understand the significance of this prediction, it's essential to delve into Dell's history and its evolution in the technology sector. Founded in 1984 by Michael Dell, the company started as a small PC manufacturer and has since grown into a global technology leader. Over the years, Dell has made strategic acquisitions, including the purchase of EMC in 2016, which expanded its portfolio of products and services. Today, Dell is a major player in the server market, with a wide range of products that cater to the needs of various industries.

Dell's market share in the server market has been steadily increasing over the years.
