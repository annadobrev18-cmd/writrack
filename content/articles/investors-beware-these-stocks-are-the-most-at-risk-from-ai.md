## The Emergence of AI Disruption
The rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries at an unprecedented pace, leaving some companies vulnerable to disruption. A recent study by Jefferies identified a basket of stocks that are at high risk of being impacted by AI, including MongoDB and Robinhood. This analysis will delve into the historical context of AI disruption, the current market impact, and provide a technical analysis of the affected stocks.

### Historical Context of AI Disruption
The concept of AI has been around for decades, but recent breakthroughs in machine learning and natural language processing have accelerated its adoption. The first wave of AI disruption was seen in the early 2000s, with the emergence of big data and analytics. However, the current wave is more profound, with AI being applied to various industries such as healthcare, finance, and transportation.

### Expert Opinions on AI Disruption
Experts have varying opinions on the impact of AI disruption on the stock market. Some experts believe that AI will create new opportunities for investors, while others believe that it will lead to significant job losses and market volatility.
