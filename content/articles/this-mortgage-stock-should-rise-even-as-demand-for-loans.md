## Historical Context of Mortgage Stocks and Interest Rates
The relationship between interest rates and mortgage stocks has been a subject of interest for investors and analysts alike. Historically, when interest rates peak, mortgage stocks tend to follow a specific pattern. According to Morgan Stanley, Rocket Companies, a leading mortgage lender, is poised to see its stock rise as rates peak, if history is any indication.

### Analysis of Past Trends
In the past, when interest rates have peaked, mortgage stocks have experienced a surge in value. This can be attributed to the fact that as interest rates rise, the demand for loans decreases, leading to a decrease in competition among lenders. As a result, lenders like Rocket Companies can increase their profit margins, leading to an increase in their stock price.
