## Market Overview
The stock market has experienced a significant sell-off in recent times, with the S&P 500 index declining by over 10% from its peak. This downturn has led to a decrease in investor confidence, with many opting to sell their holdings and move to safer assets. ### Identifying Growth Stocks
Growth stocks are companies that are expected to experience high growth rates in the future, driven by factors such as innovative products, expanding market share, and strong management teams. These stocks are often characterized by high price-to-earnings ratios, reflecting the market's expectations of future growth. During a market sell-off, growth stocks can be particularly affected, as investors become risk-averse and sell their holdings.
