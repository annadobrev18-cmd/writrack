Circle Internet Group is a leading stablecoin issuer, with its USDC stablecoin being one of the most widely used in the market. The company has established partnerships with major players in the industry, including exchanges, wallets, and DeFi protocols. This has enabled USDC to become a widely accepted and trusted stablecoin, with a strong reputation for stability and security.
