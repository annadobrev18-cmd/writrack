## Fundamentals of Domino's Stock
Domino's Pizza, Inc. (DPZ) is a well-established player in the restaurant industry, with a strong brand presence globally. The company's success can be attributed to its focus on delivery and digital channels, which have enabled it to stay ahead of the competition. As of 2026, Domino's has over 15,000 locations in more than 85 countries.

### Revenue Growth
Domino's has consistently demonstrated strong revenue growth, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12.1% over the past five years. The company's revenue has been driven by an increase in same-store sales, as well as the expansion of its global footprint. In 2025, Domino's reported revenue of $14.3 billion, up 10.3% from the previous year.
