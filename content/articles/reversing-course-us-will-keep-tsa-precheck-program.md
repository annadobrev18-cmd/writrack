## TSA PreCheck Program: A Brief Overview
The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) PreCheck program has been a cornerstone of the US air travel security system since its inception in 2013. Designed to provide low-risk travelers with expedited security screening, the program has grown in popularity, with over 10 million enrolled members. The program's benefits, including reduced wait times and a more streamlined security process, have made it an attractive option for frequent flyers.

### Economic Benefits of TSA PreCheck
The TSA PreCheck program has had a positive impact on the US economy, particularly in the air travel sector. By reducing wait times and increasing the efficiency of the security screening process, the program has helped to boost air travel demand, which in turn has contributed to the growth of the US economy. According to a study by the US Travel Association, the TSA PreCheck program has generated an estimated $1.4 billion in economic benefits per year.
