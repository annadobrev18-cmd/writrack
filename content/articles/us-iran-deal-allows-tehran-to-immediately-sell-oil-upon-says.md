## Technical Analysis: Oil Price Trends and Patterns
From a technical analysis perspective, the US-Iran deal could lead to a decrease in oil prices, which could be seen as a bearish signal. However, it is essential to consider other technical indicators, such as moving averages and relative strength index (RSI), to get a more comprehensive view of the market.

### Moving Averages: A Key Indicator
The 50-day and 200-day moving averages are crucial indicators in technical analysis. A crossover of the 50-day moving average above the 200-day moving average is seen as a bullish signal, while a crossover below is seen as bearish.

#### Relative Strength Index (RSI): A Momentum Indicator
The RSI is a momentum indicator that measures the magnitude of recent price changes to determine overbought or oversold conditions. An RSI reading above 70 is seen as overbought, while a reading below 30 is seen as oversold.
