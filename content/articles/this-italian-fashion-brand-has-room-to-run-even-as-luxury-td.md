The luxury fashion brand, Ermenegildo Zegna, has been upgraded to 'buy' from 'hold' by TD Cowen, despite the current uncertainty in the luxury market. The upgrade is based on the brand's strong fundamentals, improving valuation, and its position in the competitive luxury market.

### Fundamentals of Ermenegildo Zegna
Ermenegildo Zegna is an Italian luxury fashion house that was founded in 1910. The company is known for its high-quality clothing, accessories, and leather goods. Zegna has a strong presence in the global luxury market, with a significant brand reputation and a loyal customer base. The company's financial performance has been improving over the years, with increasing revenue and profitability.
