### What are 'Sandwich' Bots?

'Sandwich' bots are a type of automated trading bot that exploits the predictability of decentralized finance (DeFi) transactions on the Ethereum blockchain. These bots typically operate by placing buy and sell orders around a pending transaction, effectively 'sandwiching' it. This allows the bot to profit from the price movement caused by the original transaction.

#### How Do 'Sandwich' Bots Work?

The process of a 'sandwich' bot exploit involves the following steps:

1. **Monitoring the mempool**: The bot continuously monitors the Ethereum mempool for pending transactions.
2. **Identifying profitable opportunities**: The bot identifies transactions that are likely to cause a price movement, such as a large buy or sell order.
3. **Placing orders**: The bot places buy and sell orders around the pending transaction, effectively 'sandwiching' it.
4. **Profiting from the price movement**: The bot profits from the price movement caused by the original transaction.
