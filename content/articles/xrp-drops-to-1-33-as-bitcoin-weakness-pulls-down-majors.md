## Sector Rotation: Impact on the Crypto Market
The crypto market is not immune to sector rotation, a phenomenon where investors shift their focus from one sector to another in response to changing market conditions. The recent weakness in Bitcoin has led to a sector rotation, with investors moving away from major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum towards more stable or promising alternatives.

### Competitor Analysis: Alternative Cryptocurrencies
