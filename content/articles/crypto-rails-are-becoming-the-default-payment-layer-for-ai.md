### The Rise of Crypto Rails

Crypto rails refer to the infrastructure that enables the transfer of cryptocurrency between parties. The rise of crypto rails has been driven by the growing demand for fast, secure, and low-cost payment solutions. Blockchain technology, which underlies most cryptocurrencies, provides a decentralized and transparent platform for transactions. The use of crypto rails has gained significant traction in recent years, with many businesses and individuals adopting cryptocurrency as a means of payment.

### AI Agents and Payment Processing

AI agents, on the other hand, are software programs that use artificial intelligence to perform tasks autonomously. These agents can be used to execute trades, manage portfolios, and even make payments. The integration of AI agents with crypto rails enables the automation of payment processes, reducing the need for human intervention and increasing the speed and efficiency of transactions.
