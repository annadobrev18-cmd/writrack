Data centers are the backbone of the digital age, providing the necessary infrastructure for cloud computing, e-commerce, social media, and a myriad of other online services. The COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the shift towards digitalization, leading to an unprecedented demand for data storage and processing capabilities. This trend is expected to continue, driven by the growth of IoT (Internet of Things), AI (Artificial Intelligence), and 5G technologies, all of which require substantial data center capacity.
