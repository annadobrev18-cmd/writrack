Trump has a history of filing lawsuits against media outlets and individuals who he perceives as having wronged him. If the lawsuit is successful, it could lead to a significant payout for Trump and a corresponding decline in News Corp's stock price. On the other hand, if the lawsuit is unsuccessful, it could be seen as a victory for the media industry and a affirmation of the importance of a free press.
