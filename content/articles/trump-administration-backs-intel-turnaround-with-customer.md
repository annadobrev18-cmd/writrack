## Trump Administration's Backing of Intel's Turnaround
The Trump administration has recently expressed its support for Intel's efforts to revamp its operations and regain its competitive edge in the semiconductor industry. This move is seen as a strategic push to bolster the US semiconductor sector, which has been facing intense competition from Asian rivals. The administration's backing is expected to have a positive impact on Intel's stock price and the overall US market.

### Historical Context
Intel, once the undisputed leader in the semiconductor industry, has been struggling to keep pace with its Asian competitors, particularly Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) and Samsung. The company's struggles can be attributed to its delayed transition to 10nm and 7nm process technologies, which has resulted in a loss of market share to its competitors. However, with the Trump administration's support, Intel is poised to make a comeback.
