## Risk Factors
As with any investment, there are risk factors associated with the Invesco Commercial Real Estate Finance Trust. Some of the key risk factors include:

### Interest Rate Risk
The trust's investments in commercial real estate debt and equity are sensitive to interest rate changes. An increase in interest rates could lead to a decline in the trust's net income and asset values.

### Credit Risk
The trust's investments in senior loans and mezzanine loans are subject to credit risk. A default by a borrower could result in a loss of principal and interest income.

### Market Risk
The trust's investments in commercial real estate properties are subject to market risk. A decline in property values could result in a loss of asset values and net income.
