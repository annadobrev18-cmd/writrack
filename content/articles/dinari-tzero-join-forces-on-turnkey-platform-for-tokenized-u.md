## Fundamentals of Tokenized U.S. Equities
Tokenized U.S. equities represent a new paradigm in investment, where traditional assets are converted into digital tokens on a blockchain. This process enhances liquidity, reduces transaction costs, and increases accessibility for a broader range of investors. The tokenization of assets also enables the creation of fractional ownership, making high-value assets more accessible to retail investors.

### Benefits of Tokenization
- **Increased Efficiency**: Tokenization streamlines the process of buying, selling, and trading assets, reducing the need for intermediaries and thus lowering costs.
- **Improved Transparency**: Blockchain technology provides a transparent and immutable record of all transactions, enhancing trust and security in the market.
- **Enhanced Liquidity**: By making assets more accessible and easier to trade, tokenization can increase market liquidity, benefiting both investors and issuers.
