## Insider Selling: A Red Flag for Investors
The recent news of Fitzgerald, a key figure at StubHub, selling $10k in stock has sent shockwaves through the investment community. This move has sparked concerns about the company's future prospects and has left many wondering what this means for their investments.

### Understanding Insider Selling
Insider selling refers to the practice of company executives, directors, or other insiders selling their shares in the company. While it is not uncommon for insiders to sell their shares, large or frequent sales can be a red flag for investors. Insider selling can indicate a lack of confidence in the company's future prospects or a belief that the stock is overvalued.
