## IoTeX Cross-Bridge Hack: An Overview
The recent cross-bridge hack of IoTeX, resulting in a loss of $4.4 million, has sent shockwaves through the cryptocurrency community. In an unprecedented move, IoTeX has offered the hackers a 10% bounty if they return the stolen funds within 48 hours. This development raises several questions about the security of cross-bridge protocols, the effectiveness of bounty programs, and the future of blockchain technology.

### Historical Context of Cross-Bridge Hacks
Cross-bridge hacks are not a new phenomenon in the cryptocurrency space. In recent years, several high-profile hacks have targeted cross-bridge protocols, resulting in significant financial losses. The Poly Network hack in 2021, for example, resulted in a loss of over $600 million, making it one of the largest cryptocurrency hacks in history. Similarly, the Harmony Horizon Bridge hack in 2022 resulted in a loss of approximately $100 million.
