## Fundamentals of Micron Technology
Micron Technology, Inc. is an American producer of computer memory and computer data storage including dynamic random-access memory, flash memory, and USB flash drives. The company is headquartered in Boise, Idaho, and is one of the largest memory chip manufacturers in the world. 

### Historical Performance
Micron Technology has a long history of innovation and growth. The company was founded in 1978 and has since become a leader in the semiconductor industry. Over the years, Micron has made several strategic acquisitions, including the purchase of Elpida Memory in 2013 and the acquisition of Inotera Memories in 2016. These acquisitions have helped Micron expand its product offerings and increase its market share.
