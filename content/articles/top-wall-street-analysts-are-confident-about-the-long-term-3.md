## Risk Factors and Mitigation Strategies
Investing in the stock market inherently carries risks, including market volatility, regulatory changes, and unforeseen events. Understanding these risks and implementing mitigation strategies is crucial for long-term success.

### Market Volatility
Market volatility can significantly impact stock prices, with sudden drops or surges affecting investor confidence. Diversification is a key strategy to mitigate this risk, spreading investments across different sectors and asset classes to reduce exposure to any one particular stock or market.

#### Regulatory Changes
Changes in regulatory policies can also affect stock performance, particularly for companies operating in heavily regulated industries. Staying informed about potential regulatory shifts and adjusting investment portfolios accordingly can help mitigate this risk.
