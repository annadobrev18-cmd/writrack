## Sustainability in Data Centers: A Growing Concern for Investors
The increasing demand for cloud computing and data storage has led to a significant expansion of data centers in the US. As a result, investors are now pressing Amazon, Microsoft, and Google to disclose their water and power usage in these facilities. This growing concern for sustainability in data centers is driven by the environmental impact of these operations.

### Water Usage in Data Centers
Data centers require significant amounts of water for cooling systems, which is essential for maintaining the optimal operating temperature of servers. According to a report by the US Department of Energy, data centers in the US consume approximately 1.8 billion gallons of water per day. This substantial water usage has raised concerns among investors, who are now seeking transparency on the water usage of Amazon, Microsoft, and Google's data centers.
