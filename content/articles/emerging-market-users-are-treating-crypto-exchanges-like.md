### Financial Inclusion and Crypto

Emerging markets have long struggled with financial inclusion, with many individuals lacking access to traditional banking services. Cryptocurrencies and crypto exchanges have stepped in to fill this gap, providing an alternative means of storing and transferring value. The use of crypto exchanges as banking apps is a natural progression of this trend, as users become more comfortable with the technology and its applications.

#### Key Statistics

| Region | Percentage of Population with Bank Accounts | Percentage of Population Using Crypto |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Latin America | 55% | 15% |
| Southeast Asia | 60% | 20% |
| Africa | 40% | 10% |
| India | 80% | 5% |

As the table above illustrates, there is still a significant gap in financial inclusion across emerging markets. However, the adoption of cryptocurrencies is growing rapidly, with many users turning to crypto exchanges for their financial needs.
