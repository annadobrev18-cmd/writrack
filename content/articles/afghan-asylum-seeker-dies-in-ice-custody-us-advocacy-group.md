#### Economic Impact of Refugee Crises
The economic impact of refugee crises can be multifaceted. On one hand, refugees can contribute to the economy by filling labor gaps, starting new businesses, and enriching cultural diversity. On the other hand, the initial costs of providing for refugees, including healthcare, education, and housing, can be substantial. The death of an Afghan asylum-seeker in ICE custody may lead to increased scrutiny of US immigration policies, potentially affecting the flow of refugees and asylum-seekers into the country.
