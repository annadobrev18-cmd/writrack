### Background on Form 144
Form 144 is a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) that is required when an insider of a company intends to sell securities. This form is used to disclose the proposed sale of securities and provides information about the transaction, including the number of shares to be sold, the price at which they will be sold, and the identity of the insider.
