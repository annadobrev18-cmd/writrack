## Fundamentals: Assessing the Current Market Landscape
To understand the current market situation, it's essential to examine the fundamentals of the Dow, S&P 500, and Nasdaq. Each of these indices has its unique composition and reacts differently to geopolitical events.

### Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA)
The DJIA, often considered a benchmark for the US stock market, is composed of 30 significant stocks. Its performance is heavily influenced by the industrial and financial sectors. Given the current geopolitical climate, stocks within the DJIA that are closely tied to international trade and global stability may experience volatility.

### S&P 500
The S&P 500 is a broader index, covering approximately 500 stocks across various sectors. It provides a more comprehensive view of the US stock market. The S&P 500's performance during geopolitical crises tends to be more resilient compared to the DJIA, due to its diversification across different sectors.

### Nasdaq Composite
The Nasdaq Composite is heavily weighted towards technology stocks. The performance of the Nasdaq can be significantly influenced by the tech sector's reaction to geopolitical events, especially those that may impact global supply chains or trade relations.
