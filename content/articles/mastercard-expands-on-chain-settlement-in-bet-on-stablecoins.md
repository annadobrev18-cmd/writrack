### Historical Context: Mastercard's Journey into Blockchain

Mastercard's interest in blockchain technology dates back to 2016 when it filed patents for blockchain-based payment systems. Since then, the company has been actively exploring the potential of blockchain to improve the efficiency, security, and transparency of its payment networks. In 2018, Mastercard launched its blockchain-based cross-border payment system, which enabled faster and more secure transactions. The company has also partnered with various blockchain startups and organizations to further develop its blockchain capabilities.

### Market Impact: The Rise of Stablecoins

The market for stablecoins has experienced significant growth in recent years, with the total market capitalization of stablecoins reaching over $100 billion. Stablecoins, which are designed to maintain a stable value relative to a fiat currency, have become increasingly popular as a means of payment and store of value. Mastercard's expansion into on-chain settlement is a strategic move to capitalize on the growing demand for stablecoins and to position itself as a leader in the always-on finance space.

### Technical Analysis: On-Chain Settlement and Its Benefits

On-chain settlement refers to the process of settling transactions directly on a blockchain network, without the need for intermediaries. This approach offers several benefits, including increased efficiency, reduced costs, and improved security. By expanding on-chain settlement, Mastercard aims to provide its customers with faster and more secure payment solutions, while also reducing its own operational costs.

#### Benefits of On-Chain Settlement

* **Increased Efficiency**: On-chain settlement enables real-time transaction processing, reducing the need for intermediaries and increasing the speed of settlements.
* **Reduced Costs**: By eliminating the need for intermediaries, on-chain settlement can significantly reduce transaction costs.
* **Improved Security**: On-chain settlement provides an additional layer of security, as transactions are recorded on a decentralized and immutable blockchain network.

### Always-On Finance: The Future of Payments

The concept of always-on finance refers to the ability to conduct financial transactions at any time, from anywhere, and on any device. Mastercard's expansion into on-chain settlement is a key component of its always-on finance strategy, which aims to provide customers with seamless and secure payment solutions. As the payments industry continues to evolve, the adoption of always-on finance is expected to increase, driven by the growing demand for convenience, speed, and security.

#### Key Drivers of Always-On Finance

* **Increased Mobility**: The widespread adoption of smartphones and mobile devices has enabled consumers to conduct financial transactions on-the-go.
* **Growing Demand for Convenience**: Consumers are increasingly expecting seamless and convenient payment solutions, driving the demand for always-on finance.
* **Advances in Technology**: Advances in blockchain, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things (IoT) are enabling the development of more secure and efficient payment solutions.
