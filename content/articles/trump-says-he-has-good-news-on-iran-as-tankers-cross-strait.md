### Historical Context

The Iran-US conflict has been a longstanding issue, with tensions escalating in recent years due to disagreements over nuclear programs, oil exports, and regional influence. The Strait of Hormuz, a critical waterway connecting the Persian Gulf to the Arabian Sea, has been a focal point of contention, with Iran threatening to block oil shipments in response to US sanctions.

#### Key Events

* 2018: The US withdraws from the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA), a nuclear deal signed between Iran, the US, and other world powers.
* 2019: The US imposes stringent sanctions on Iran, targeting its oil exports and banking sector.
* 2020: Iran retaliates by attacking US military bases in Iraq, leading to a significant escalation of tensions.
