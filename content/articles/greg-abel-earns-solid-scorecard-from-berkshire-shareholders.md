## Berkshire Hathaway's New Chapter
The 2026 annual meeting of Berkshire Hathaway marked a significant milestone, as it was the first meeting led by Greg Abel, following the transition from the legendary Warren Buffett. The event drew considerable attention from investors and analysts, all eager to gauge Abel's leadership style and vision for the future of the conglomerate.

### Shareholder Reception
The reviews from longtime shareholders and professional investors were broadly positive, acknowledging Abel's efforts to engage with the audience and provide insights into Berkshire's operations.
