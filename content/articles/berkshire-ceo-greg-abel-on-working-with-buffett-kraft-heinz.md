## Berkshire Hathaway's New Era
Berkshire Hathaway, one of the world's most successful conglomerates, has entered a new era with Greg Abel at the helm. As the newly appointed CEO, Abel has been making waves with his recent interview on CNBC's 'Squawk Box'. The half-hour live session provided valuable insights into Abel's vision for the company, his working relationship with Warren Buffett, and his investment strategy.

### Working with Warren Buffett
Abel's relationship with Warren Buffett is a crucial aspect of his role as CEO. Buffett, known for his exceptional investment acumen, has been a mentor and guide for Abel. In the interview, Abel spoke highly of Buffett, praising his ability to simplify complex ideas and his unwavering commitment to the company's values. Abel also emphasized the importance of maintaining a strong working relationship with Buffett, ensuring a seamless transition and continuity of the company's vision.

### Investment Strategy
Abel's investment strategy is centered around long-term growth and value creation. He emphasized the importance of investing in companies with strong fundamentals, talented management, and a competitive edge. Abel also highlighted his personal commitment to the company, revealing that he uses his entire salary to purchase Berkshire Hathaway stock. This move demonstrates his confidence in the company's future prospects and aligns his interests with those of the shareholders.

### Kraft Heinz: A Turning Point
The Kraft Heinz investment has been a significant turning point for Berkshire Hathaway. Although the company incurred substantial losses, Abel views the experience as a valuable learning opportunity. He acknowledged the mistakes made and emphasized the importance of adapting to changing market conditions. Abel's willingness to acknowledge and learn from mistakes is a testament to his leadership style and commitment to continuous improvement.
