#### The Impact on Cryptographic Algorithms
The problem with quantum computing is that it has the potential to break the cryptographic algorithms that are used to secure the bitcoin network. Bitcoin's security is based on a cryptographic algorithm called SHA-256, which is used to verify transactions and control the creation of new units. However, quantum computers have the ability to process complex calculations at an exponential rate, making it possible for them to break the SHA-256 algorithm.
