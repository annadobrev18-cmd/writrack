## Vicor Corp: An Overview
Vicor Corp is a leading provider of power conversion solutions, designing, manufacturing, and marketing modular power components and complete power systems. The company's products are used in a variety of applications, including aerospace, defense, and industrial markets.

### Business Segments
Vicor Corp operates through two main business segments: Brick Business Unit (BBU) and Vicor Power Systems (VPS). The BBU segment offers modular power components, while the VPS segment provides complete power systems.
