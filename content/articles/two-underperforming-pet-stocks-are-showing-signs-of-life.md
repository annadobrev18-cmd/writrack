### Historical Context of the Pet Stock Sector
The pet stock sector has experienced fluctuations over the years, influenced by various factors such as consumer spending, pet ownership trends, and the overall economic environment. In the past, the sector has been driven by the growing humanization of pets, with pet owners increasingly willing to spend on premium products and services for their pets. However, in recent times, the sector has faced challenges, including increased competition, pricing pressures, and changing consumer preferences.

The resurgence of the pet stock sector has significant implications for investors.
