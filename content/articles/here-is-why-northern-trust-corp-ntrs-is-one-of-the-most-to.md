## Fundamentals of Northern Trust Corp.
Northern Trust Corp. (NTRS) is a leading provider of wealth management, asset servicing, asset management, and banking solutions for corporations, institutions, and affluent individuals. With a history spanning over 130 years, the company has established itself as a trusted and reliable partner in the financial services industry.

### Historical Performance
NTRS has consistently demonstrated strong financial performance, with a proven track record of generating stable revenue and earnings growth. The company's net income has increased steadily over the years, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.5% from 2015 to 2022. This growth can be attributed to the company's strategic expansion into new markets, improved operating efficiency, and a disciplined approach to risk management.

### Business Segments
NTRS operates through two primary business segments: Corporate & Institutional Services (C&IS) and Wealth Management. The C&IS segment provides asset servicing, asset management, and related services to corporate and institutional clients, while the Wealth Management segment offers investment management, wealth planning, and banking services to high-net-worth individuals and families.

#### C&IS Segment
The C&IS segment is the largest contributor to NTRS' revenue, accounting for approximately 70% of total revenue. This segment has experienced steady growth, driven by increased demand for asset servicing and asset management services. The company's strong reputation, combined with its advanced technology platform and global reach, has enabled it to attract and retain large institutional clients.

#### Wealth Management Segment
The Wealth Management segment has also demonstrated significant growth, with assets under management (AUM) increasing by 10% annually from 2015 to 2022. This growth can be attributed to the company's expanding wealth management capabilities, including investment management, wealth planning, and banking services. The segment's strong performance has been driven by the company's ability to attract and retain high-net-worth individuals and families, who value the company's personalized service and expertise.
