## Yields: A Critical Component of the Global Economy

Yields are another critical component of the global economy, and they have a significant impact on the Dow Jones Futures. Yields are the returns on investment for bonds, and they are a key indicator of the overall health of the economy. When yields are high, it can indicate a strong economy, as investors are willing to lend money at higher interest rates. On the other hand, when yields are low, it can indicate a weak economy, as investors are less willing to lend money.
